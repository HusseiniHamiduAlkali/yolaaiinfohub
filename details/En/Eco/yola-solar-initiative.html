About Yola Solar Initiative

The Yola Solar Initiative is dedicated to promoting solar energy adoption and renewable power solutions in Yola. The organization works to increase awareness and accessibility of solar technology while reducing dependence on fossil fuels and supporting sustainable energy transition.

Mission

To accelerate the adoption of solar energy in Yola through education, advocacy, and practical implementation of renewable power solutions for households, businesses, and institutions.

Services and Programs

  • Solar Education: Training and workshops on solar technology
  • System Design: Customized solar solutions for different needs
  • Installation Support: Professional installation services
  • Maintenance Programs: Regular servicing and support
  • Financing Assistance: Help with solar investment options

Target Groups

  • Residential households seeking energy independence
  • Small and medium enterprises
  • Schools and educational institutions
  • Healthcare facilities
  • Government offices and institutions

Benefits of Solar Energy

  • Reduced electricity costs long-term
  • Energy independence and reliability
  • Environmental protection through clean energy
  • No fuel costs once installed
  • Long lifespan and low maintenance
  • Job creation in solar sector

Recent Achievements

  • Installed 500+ solar systems in homes and businesses
  • Trained 200+ people on solar technology
  • Reduced energy costs for beneficiaries by 60%
  • Created 50+ jobs in solar installation
  • Displaced 1,000+ tons of CO2 emissions
