Association of Plastic Recyclers, Yola

Non-Profit Environmental Education and Conservation Organization

About the Association

The Association of Plastic Recyclers Yola is a non-profit organization focused on environmental education and conservation, particularly in plastic waste management. The organization brings together recycling professionals, businesses, and community members to advance plastic recycling practices.

Mission and Goals

  • Promote plastic waste reduction at the source
  • Advance plastic recycling technology and practices
  • Educate communities about plastic pollution
  • Build a sustainable circular economy for plastics
  • Advocate for supportive environmental policies

Programs and Activities

  • Recycler Training: Professional development for recycling industry workers
  • Community Education: Awareness campaigns on plastic waste
  • Industry Collaboration: Networking among recyclers and businesses
  • Research and Innovation: Finding new recycling solutions
  • Policy Advocacy: Promoting supportive environmental regulations

Membership Benefits

  • Access to training and professional development
  • Networking with other recyclers and businesses
  • Information on market trends and opportunities
  • Representation in industry discussions
  • Support in business development

Environmental Impact

  • Diverted thousands of tons of plastic from landfills
  • Trained hundreds in professional recycling practices
  • Engaged thousands in environmental awareness
  • Established sustainable business models
  • Contributed to policy improvements
