About Roundabout Maishanu

Roundabout Maishanu in Jimeta, Yola is a major traffic circle that serves as a critical junction connecting multiple main roads in the Jimeta area. This important intersection facilitates the movement of vehicles between various directions and neighborhoods, serving as a hub for commercial activity, daily commuting, and inter-city travel. The roundabout is a recognizable landmark in the Jimeta commercial district.
