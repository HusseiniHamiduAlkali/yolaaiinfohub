About International Polo Ground

The International Polo Ground in Yola is a professional polo field designed to international standards and specifications. The facility hosts prestigious polo tournaments, equestrian events, and competitive matches. The polo ground represents Yola's commitment to promoting this elite equestrian sport and hosting high-level sporting competitions.
