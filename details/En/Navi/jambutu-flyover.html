Jambutu Flyover, Jimeta Yola

Elevated Roadway Connecting Jambutu to Central Jimeta

About Jambutu Flyover

The Jambutu Flyover is an elevated roadway structure in Jimeta, Yola, that connects the Jambutu residential and commercial area with central Jimeta. This modern infrastructure project significantly improves connectivity and traffic flow between these important areas of the city, facilitating easier movement for residents, commuters, and commercial vehicles.
