Catholic Secretariat, Yola

Administrative Center for the Catholic Diocese

About Catholic Secretariat

The Catholic Secretariat in Yola serves as the administrative headquarters for the Catholic Diocese of Yola. It is a vital center for coordinating Catholic religious activities, pastoral services, and community development programs across the diocese. The secretariat manages spiritual and administrative affairs for the Catholic community in Adamawa State.

Administrative Functions

  • Diocese administrative headquarters
  • Coordinating pastoral activities across parishes
  • Managing diocesan finances and resources
  • Overseeing clergy assignments and movements
  • Handling marriage records and documentation
  • Coordinating religious education and training

Pastoral Services

  • Spiritual guidance and counseling
  • Sacraments administration oversight
  • Marriage and family services
  • Burial and funeral arrangements
  • Baptism records and documentation
  • Confession and reconciliation services

Community Programs

  • Catechism and religious education
  • Youth development and formation programs
  • Women's ministry and empowerment
  • Social welfare and charity work
  • Healthcare and hospital services
  • Education and scholarship programs

Facilities Available

  • Office spaces for administrative staff
  • Meeting and conference rooms
  • Library and resource center
  • Prayer chapel for staff and visitors
  • Educational facilities
  • Comfortable visitor waiting areas

Services for Visitors

  • Religious guidance and counseling
  • Information about Catholic parishes in the region
  • Records and documentation services
  • Sacrament scheduling assistance
  • Educational resources and materials
  • Community referral services

Contact Information

Location: Yola, Adamawa State

The Catholic Secretariat is a welcoming center for all seeking Catholic spiritual guidance, administrative assistance, or community services. Visitors are welcome to inquire about Catholic activities and programs in the diocese.
