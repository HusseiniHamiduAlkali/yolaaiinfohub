About NIPOST Yola

The Nigerian Postal Service (NIPOST) Yola branch is the official postal service provider in Adamawa State, offering reliable mail delivery, parcel services, and postal solutions to individuals and businesses. As a government agency under the Federal Ministry of Communications and Digital Economy, NIPOST provides essential communication services across Nigeria.

Core Services Offered

  • Domestic mail delivery - Letters and documents
  • Parcel and package services
  • Express mail services (EMS)
  • International mail and parcels
  • Registered mail services
  • Insured mail and valuable items handling

Postal Products and Solutions

  • Standard Letters and Envelopes
  • Parcels Up Service - For packages and parcels
  • Speed Post - Express delivery service
  • Registered Mail - Tracked and confirmed delivery
  • International Parcels - Cross-border shipping
  • Philatelic Services - Stamps and postal collectibles

Business and Corporate Services
