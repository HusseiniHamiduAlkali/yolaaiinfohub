Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC), Yola Office

Government Agency for Business Registration and Corporate Regulation

About CAC Yola Office

The Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) Yola Office is a government agency responsible for the registration and regulation of businesses, corporate entities, and associations in Adamawa State. As a key stakeholder in Nigeria's business environment, CAC ensures compliance with corporate laws and facilitates the formal registration of businesses, partnerships, and organizations.

Core Functions and Services

  • Business name registration and renewal
  • Company incorporation and registration
  • Registered Trustees (NGO) registration
  • Business information and documentation retrieval
  • Company compliance and filings
  • Corporate governance and regulatory oversight

Business Registration Services

  • Business Name Registration - For sole proprietors and partnerships
  • Company Registration - For limited liability companies
  • Registration of Trustees - For non-profit organizations
  • Registration of Foreign Companies - Operating in Nigeria
  • Branch Registration - For expanding businesses
  • Certificate of Incorporation - Official business registration proof

Compliance and Regulation

  • Annual returns and financial reporting requirements
  • Directors and shareholders information updates
  • Audit reports and corporate governance compliance
  • Enforcement of corporate laws and regulations
  • Investigation of corporate violations
  • Legal advisory services on corporate matters

Documentation and Records

  • Issuance of Certificates of Registration
  • Certified true copies of registered documents
  • Company search and verification services
  • Name availability checking
  • Document authentication services
  • Historical records and archival services

Support for Entrepreneurs

  • Guidance on business registration processes
  • Information on corporate regulatory requirements
  • Business name suggestions and approval
  • Registration fee clarification and payment options
  • Assistance with compliance documentation
  • Customer care and support services

Operating Hours and Access

  • Monday to Friday business hours
  • Multiple service counters for quick processing
  • Online registration and filing options available
  • Walk-in services and appointment scheduling
  • Accessible facilities for persons with disabilities
  • Customer information center with guides and forms

Contact Information

Location: Yola, Adamawa State

The CAC Yola Office provides comprehensive business registration and corporate regulation services to entrepreneurs, businesses, and organizations in Adamawa State. Visit the office for assistance with business registration, compliance matters, or corporate inquiries.
