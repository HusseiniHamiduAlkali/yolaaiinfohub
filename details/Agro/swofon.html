Small Scale Women Farmers Organization in Nigeria (SWOFON)

Empowering Women in Agriculture for Food Security

About SWOFON

SWOFON is a non-governmental organization dedicated to empowering small-scale women farmers in Nigeria. Recognizing the crucial role women play in food production, our organization works to address the unique challenges they face, such as limited access to land, credit, and modern farming tools. We provide a platform for women to organize, share knowledge, and collectively advocate for policies that support their agricultural activities and improve their livelihoods.

Our Initiatives

  • Capacity Building: Training workshops on financial literacy, business management, and modern farming.
  • Resource Mobilization: Helping members access micro-credit and grants for their farming businesses.
  • Advocacy: Championing gender-sensitive agricultural policies at the state level.
