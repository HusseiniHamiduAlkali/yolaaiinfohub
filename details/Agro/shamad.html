About Our Mill

Shamad Rice Processing Limited is a state-of-the-art rice processing mill committed to transforming local paddy rice into high-quality, polished, and stone-free rice for consumption. We work directly with rice farmers in Adamawa and the surrounding regions, providing them with a reliable market for their produce. Our advanced milling technology ensures minimal waste and superior product quality, contributing to the "grow local, eat local" movement and supporting the state's economy.

Our Services

  • Paddy Rice Milling: Transforming raw paddy into polished rice.
  • Grain Sorting & Packaging: Ensuring clean, ready-to-use rice.
  • Off-take Agreements: Providing a stable market for local rice farmers.
