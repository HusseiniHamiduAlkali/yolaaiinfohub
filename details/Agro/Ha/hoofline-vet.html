Hoof-line Veterinary

Kiwon Likita Musamman don Abubuwan Kasua da Noma

Shimfidar Aiki-Akina Mu

Hoof-line Veterinary shine gidagen likita musamman a Yola, wanda ke nuisa lafiyar da kuma karuwa ta abubuwan kasua da noma maimako. Mun ba da abubuwan likita marhabare manoma, makaryaci, da kuma mai-kasuwa likita masu karfin waje. Ma'aikatatun mu suna da sani-sani a tsare matsalalin lafiyar musamman na mauda, jirgawa, tumbaki, da wasu abubuwa maimako. Mun taffatse ga taimakon jama'a ta gida ta gidan lafiyar da kuma karuwa ta abubuwan kasua."

Ƙwarewarren Mu

  • Tsare Cututtuka: Ciwon jiya da kuma gajiyar matsala."
  • Lafiyar Hamada: Abubuwan noma da kuma kula ga abubuwan kasua."
  • Gaida Kasua na Rashin Jiya: Saurin jiya ga abubuwan kasua da abubuwan lafiyar."
  • Tunanin Cin Abinci: Kula a kan abinci da kuma abubuwan karuwa ga lafiyar daidai."
