Ba Manoma Ilimin Noman Zamani

Shimfidar Karatun Nan

International Institute of Tropical Agriculture (IITA) ya aiwatar da karatun mai karfin waje ga manoma 35,000 a Yola da kasuwan da ke kusa. Jigon ya daidaitawa don kunna ramin tsakanin jajirori na gida da kuma jajirori masu karfin waje na gida. Karatun ya takarda abubuwa marwan wanda ke niyya karuwa ta samuni, lafiyar jiji, da karuwa daidai na noma. Ta ba manoma sani-sani da ilimi, kanatun ya niyya karuwa ta cin abinci da kuma karfin kuɗi a kasuwa."

Karatun Karatu

  • Kula da Jiji: Hanyoyi na bincike jiji, karuwa gida, da tsare rua."
  • Tsare Kasua da Cututtuka: Hanyoyi na hadabe-hadabe kasua da amfani mara waji na abubuwan tsare ciyar."
  • Iri Mai Karfin Waje: Gabatarwa ga samuni-karfin waje da iri wanda ke jira sanyi."
  • Jajirori Bayan Shai: Hanyoyi na gajarta gajarta hasara bayan shai da kuma karuwa ta jiya."
