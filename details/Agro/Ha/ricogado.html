Ciyar Dabbobi ta Premium don Lafiyar Dabbobi Mafi Kyau

Game da Injin Mu

Rico Gado Quality Feed Mills wani kamfani ne na samar da ciyar dabbobi na ƙwarai wanda ke ba da bambancin ciyar dabbobi masu wadatar abinci da daidaitawa don kaza, kifi, da sauran dabbobi. Shirye-shiryen ciyarmu an haɓaka su ta hanyar kimiyya don tabbatar da ci gaba mafi kyau, lafiya, da yawan amfani na dabbobinku. Muna amfani da albarkatun ƙasa masu inganci da aka samo daga gida kuma muna bin ƙa'idodin sarrafa inganci na tsauri don samar da ciyar da ke ba da sakamako masu daidaitawa. Mu abokin tarayya ne mai aminci ga gonakin kasuwanci da masu dabbobi kanana.

Layukan Samfura

  • Ciyar Kaza: Ciyar broiler starter, finisher, da layer.
  • Ciyar Kifi: Ciyar mai yawan furotin don catfish da sauran nau'ikan ruwa.
  • Ciyar Dabbobi: Ciyar mai tattarawa don shanu, awaki, da tumaki.
