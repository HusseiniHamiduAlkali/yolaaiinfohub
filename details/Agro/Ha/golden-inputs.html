Kasua Gaskiya da Taimakon ba Noma-Kayaye Karfin Waje

Shimfidar Aiki-Akina Mu

Golden Agroinputs Supply shine kasuwar dama na noma-kayaye karfin waje, wanda ke taima manoma a Yola da balagi na Adamawa. Mun gani cewa kasuwan girke samun noma-kayaye da karfin waje. Dalilana shine ba manoma noma-kayaye gaskiya, masu karfin waje, da kuma abubuwan tsare ciyar da ke taimakon karuwa da karuwa ta jiya. Mun taffatse ga gaskiya da kuma tunanin sani ga jama'a da mu ke aiki."

Noma-Kayaye Muke Bada

  • Iri Mai Karfin Waje: Masa, shinkafa, soybeans, da wasu iri na gari.
  • Gida: NPK, Urea, da kuma wasu gida na jiji musamman."
  • Kasua-Tsare da Tsare Ciyar: Zaɓi maidowan abubuwan tsare ciyar."
