Karuwa na Sabon Jenereyishan na Agri-Entrepreneurs

Shimfidar HYFAN

Himma, kalmar ta kasuwa da ke niyya "aiki mai karfi da taffatse," mai tsarki shine jaromar Himma Youth Farmers Association of Nigeria (HYFAN). Jigon mu ya ƙufa jira da taimakon matasa don gayyada gida kasua kamar noma kasuwa. Mun ba da karatu a kan jajirori na kasuwan agri, daga samar ta masai har zuwa karuwa da kasuwa. Dalilana shine gajarta waje ta matasa da kuma samar da aje sabon class na agri-preneurs a Jihar Adamawa."

Aiki-Akina Muka Yi

  • Karatun Sani: Tuƙi matasa manoma tare da sani da karfin waje."
  • Kuɗin Fara: Taimakon samun grant da kuma kuɗin fara ga aikin noma."
  • Tuƙi Jajirori: Gabatarwa ga matasa manoma ga jajirori masu karfin waje kamar abubuwan daidai."
Komawa Gida
