Ƙungiyar Manoman Shinkafa ta Najeriya (RIFAN)

Taimaka wa Samarin Shinkafa da Tsaron Abinci a Adamawa

Game da Rukunin RIFAN Adamawa

Rukunin jihar Adamawa na Ƙungiyar Manoman Shinkafa ta Najeriya (RIFAN) wani ƙungiya ce ta musamman da aka sadaukar da tallafa wa manoman shinkafa da haɓaka samar da shinkafa a cikin jihar. Tun da shinkafa abinci ne mai mahimmanci kuma babban amfani a Adamawa, ƙungiyarmu tana taka muhimmiyar rawa wajen tabbatar da tsaron abinci da wadata ta tattalin arziƙi. Muna ba membobi dama zuwa irin inganci, takin zamani, da kayan aikin noma na zamani, kuma muna haɗa su da masu karɓa da kamfanonin sarrafawa.

Ayyukan Mu

  • Rarraba Kayayyaki: Sauƙaƙe rarraba kayayyaki a ƙarƙashin shirye-shiryen da gwamnati ke goyan baya.
  • Zurfan Horo: Gudanar da horo kan mafi kyawun ayyuka a cikin noman shinkafa, daga shuka zuwa girbi.
  • Haɗin Kasuwa: Tattauna farashi mai adalci da haɗa manoma zuwa kasuwanni masu riba.
  • Ba da Shawara: Wakiltar muradun manoman shinkafa zuwa hukumomin jihar da tarayya.
