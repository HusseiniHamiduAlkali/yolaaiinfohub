Comfort Agro Chemicals

Yin Noma Mafi Sauƙi da Mafi Amfani

Shimfidar Aiki-Akina Mu

Comfort Agro Chemicals shine kasua da ke taimako kan sauffering noman. Dalilana shine ba manoma da jajirori masu amfani da kuma sauƙi. Mun ba da tunanin jama'a don taimakon zaɓi na abubuwa da musamman da kuma karfin kudanda. Mun yarda da jira dogurin aiki-jaji da kuma ba da aiki mai kyau.

Abin da Muke Bada

  • Tsare Kasua: Jajirori masu karfin waje da tsare kasua musamman.
  • Tsare Ciyar: Tsarewar ciya kaɗai da bayan tsarewa.
  • Abubuwa Masu Kuwo: Foliar abincin gari da kuma ƙananan abubuwan karfin gida.
