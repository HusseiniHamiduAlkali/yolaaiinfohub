Small Scale Women Farmers Organization in Nigeria (SWOFON)

Ƙarfafa Mata a Noma don Tsaron Abinci

Game da SWOFON

SWOFON ƙungiya ce ba ta gwamnati ba wacce ta jajirce ga ƙarfafa manoman mata masu ƙanana a Nigeria. Ganin muhimmiyar rawa da mata ke taka wajen samar da abinci, ƙungiyarmu tana aiki don magance matsalolin musamman da suke fuskanta, kamar ƙarancin samun fili, bashi, da kayan noma na zamani. Muna bayar da dandamali ga mata don shirya, raba ilimi, da kuma yin gargadi tare ga manufofi da ke tallafa wa ayyukan nomansu da inganta rayuwarsu.

Ayyukanmu

  • Gina Ƙarfi: Taron horo kan ilimin kuɗi, gudanar da kasuwanci, da noma ta zamani.
  • Tara Albarkatu: Taimakawa membobi su samu ƙananan bashi da tallafi ga kasuwancin nomansu.
  • Gargadi: Jagoranci manufofi na noma masu la'akari da jinsi a matakin jiha.
