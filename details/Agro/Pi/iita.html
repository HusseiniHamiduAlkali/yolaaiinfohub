IITA Training for Farmers

Teach Farmer New Way Make Farm Work Better Better

Wetin Di Training Be

IITA na international place wey teach farmer how make farm work better-better. Dem teach many thousand farmer for Yola bout how change old farm way to new way wey go work well. Dem teach how make crop grow plenty, how keep soil good, na all toro wey go make farm work bring plenty money na good food.

Wetin Dem Go Teach

  • How Make Soil Good: Test soil, add toro wey go make soil better, na stop soil from go away.
  • Stop Bug Na Sickness: Way how to stop bug bite crop without use poison wey bad.
  • Seed Wey Go Grow Fast: Show farmer seed wey go grow fast na carry weight when rain no fall.
  • How Keep Crop After Cut: Way make crop no spoil quick-quick after cut am from farm.
