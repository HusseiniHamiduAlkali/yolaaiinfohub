'Every Home a Garden' - Movement by the First Lady

Make Every House Get Small Farm So Family Chop Fine Toro

Wetin Di Movement Be

Di 'Every Home a Garden' movement wey di First Lady lead am, na plan make every family grow di dia food for small place. It make sure say every family chop toro wey fresh from dia own garden. Di idea be say if every house make dia own food, di whole village go get food enough. Di program dey give free seed na tool fo start, na dey teach simple garden way too.

Wetin Di Program Go Do

  • Give Seed Na Tool Free: Give all di ting wey family need make dem start.
  • Teach How Garden Wey Simple: Show dem how make garden easy na use rotten leaf toro go become soil good.
  • Make Ota Garden People Become Family: Build one big family wey garden people dey share wetin dem learn.
