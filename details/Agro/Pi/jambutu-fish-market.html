Jambutu Smoked Fish Market

Place Wey Dem Dey Sell Fish Wey Smoke Finish Na Taste Good

Wetin Di Market Be

Jambutu Smoked Fish Market na place wey dem dey sell fish wey dem smoke finish. Dem get fish from river wey near here na dem smoke am so am no go spoil quick. Di market na place wey dem dey sell di fish dem, na many person from Adamawa na other place come buy. Di market dey help di fish person dem na people wey smoke fish make dem get money.

Wetin Dem Dey Sell

  • Many kind fish wey dem smoke finish.
  • Fresh fish wey dem just catch dey get am some day.
  • Tell you how pick good fish na how cook am.
