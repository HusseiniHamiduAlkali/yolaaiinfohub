Coldhubs Storage Center Jambutu Yola

Place Wey Dey Keep Crop Fresh Fresh When Am Cold

Wetin Our Place Dey Do

Coldhubs Storage Center for Jambutu, Yola, dey help farmers, traders, na food makers by give dem cold room wey clean-clean na cheap. Our cold room wey sun power am dey help farmer make say dia fruit na vegetable no spoil quick-quick, na make am remain good. When farmers get place make store dia crop, dem go wait when price go up before sell, so dem make more money na waste go come down.

Wetin We Dey Offer

  • Keep Am Cold: Short time na long time keep fruit, vegetable, na oda stuff wey spoil quick.
  • Keep Temperature Right: Make sure say temperature stay correct fo all di ting dem.
  • Help Move Ting: Help carry di crop na move am go where dem want.
