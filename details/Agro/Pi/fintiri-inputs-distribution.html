Distribution of Farm Inputs by the Adamawa State Governor

Gọmenti Dey Help Farmer Ma Make Chop Plenty

Wetin Di Plan Be

Di government plan na way fo help farm people grow plenty toro. Di program give seed wey new-new, ntụ, na chemical to all farmer. It come because farm people no get money wey plenty na food go finish. Di plan go give farmer power to grow plenty toro every season.

How Di Work Go Happen

  • Write Name: Farmer dem dey put dia name from dia group.
  • Check Say Na True: Dem go check to make sure say na real farmer dem dey give toro.
  • Give Dem Toro: Dem go put all toro wey dem get for market place so farmer go collect am.
