Climate-Smart Seed Production Training

Make Crop Strong When Weather Dey Give Problem

Wetin Di Training Be

Di Climate-Smart Seed Production Training na important program wey teach farmers na farm people how make seed wey go strong even when weather change bad. Di training teach about how pick crop wey no get thirsty, how make seed wey no take long time grow, na how do farm work wey save water na make soil good. Dis program na important because farm work go remain good money even when weather no dey behave well-well.

Wetin Dem Go Learn

  • Pick Good Seed: Choose crop wey go grow well when weather dey hot or cold.
  • Save Water: Way how to give water good so water no waste well-well.
  • Keep Soil Good: Do farm work wey make soil no spoil like put plant thing on top, no use plow too much.
  • Farm Wey No Use Poison: How do farm work wit no bad poison wey go help nature.
