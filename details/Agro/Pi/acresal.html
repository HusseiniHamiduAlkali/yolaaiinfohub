ACRES AL Adamawa State Project

One Wey Dem Plan Am Togetah Fo Betta Farm

Wetin Di Project Wan Do

Di ACRES AL (Adamawa State Collaborative for Rural Economic Sustainability) project na plan wey plenty-plenty person come togetah make to change di farm work wey dey for Adamawa. Di project na focus on make strong chains for main crops, help di farmers get good place make sell dia own, and make di village people dem strong well-well. E bring government people, NGO people, and business people togetah make dem follow one plan wey go solve all di farm problem wey dey for di steyt farm work.

Wetin Dem Go Do For Di Project

  • Make Chain Wey Go Remain: Focus on rice, corn, na cassava make dem link di farmers wit people wey dey cook am na people wey dey buy.
  • Make Money Reach Village: Help di small farmers dem get money fo borrow na save.
  • Fix Road Na Store: Help make di village road good na di place wey dem dey keep crop.
  • Farm Wey No Hurt Weather: Teach farmers how make dem fit do farm work when weather change.
