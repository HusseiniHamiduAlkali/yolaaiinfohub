All Farmers Association of Nigeria (AFAN)

Make All Farmer Unite Na Get Strong for Adamawa

Wetin AFAN Be

Di All Farmers Association of Nigeria (AFAN) na di big body wey hold all farmers for di whole country. Di Yola Branch na di person wey speak fo farmers for Adamawa State. Wetin we dey do na speak fo farmers, teach dem new farm way, na help dem get money from government. We na NGO organization wey want make all farmers fine well-well, from small farmers to big business farmers.

Wetin We Wan Accomplish

  • Speak Fo Farmers: Tell government wetin farmers need at all level.
  • Teach Dem: Make workshop wey teach dem new farm way, how make soil good, na how fight bug.
  • Get Resource: Help member get crop stuff, money fo borrow, na market.
  • Make Strong Family: Build network where farmers dey help one another for di steyt.
