Fisheries Society of Nigeria (FISON)

For Fish People Dem Wey Know How Make Fish Farm Work

Wetin FISON Be

FISON na group fo fish people. We get plenty fish person for Yola and Adamawa. We dey teach dem how make fish farm work-well, help fish person make money, na make sure say we get enough fish fo eat. We bring all fish people togetah make dem know one another, learn new toro, na talk wit government fo help fish people.

Wetin We Dey Do

  • Teach Fish Farm: Show fish person how make fish grow well well na how take care am
  • Learn New Toro: Do study to know new fish wey go grow fast na new way make fish farm
  • Help Sell Fish: Link fish person wit market na people wey dey buy
  • Talk Wit Government: Tell government wetin fish person need fo help dem
  • Make Fish Person Know Onenanother: Help dem meet one another na share wetin dem learn
  • Make Sure Fish Dey Good: Make sure say all fish wey sell am fine fine na good

Fish Farm Service Dem

  • Make Baby Fish: Place wey dem make baby fish na small fish fo farm
  • Make Fish Food: We make good food fo fish make fish grow big
  • Help Build Fish Farm: Tell how make fish farm place na how take care am
  • Stop Fish Sickness: Help find sickness wey fish get na how stop am
  • Check Water Good Good: Test water make sure say water fine fo fish
  • After Catch Fish Work: Teach how smoke fish na keep am so no spoil

Learn How Fish Farm

  • Start Learn Fish Farm: Tell new person wetin dem go do fish farm
  • Learn Better Farm: Teach how make fish farm bring plenty fish na money
  • Make Baby Fish Learn: Show how make baby fish na take care am
  • Make Fish Food Learn: Teach how smoke fish na sell am fo money
  • Money Learn: Tell how count money fo fish farm business
  • No Hurt World Learn: Teach how make fish farm no hurt di river na land

Membership Benefits

  • Access to technical training and capacity building programs
  • Regular newsletters and updates on fisheries research and best practices
  • Networking opportunities with other fish farmers and professionals
  • Preferential rates on equipment and input supplies
  • Market information and linkage support
  • Representation in fisheries policy discussions
  • Insurance and social support programs
