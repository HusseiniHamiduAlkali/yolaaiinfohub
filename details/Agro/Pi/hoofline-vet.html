Wetin We Dey Do

Hoof-line Veterinary na doctor place for Yola wey focus on big animal like cow, goat, na sheep. We dey help farmer na person wey raise big animal. Our doctor dem know all di sickness wey big animal get. We dey make sure say farmer big animal dem fine well-well na work hard.

Wetin We Know How Do

  • Stop Sickness: Give medicine before animal get sick na clean inside belly.
  • Make Baby Animal: Help animal make baby wey healthy.
  • Come Quick When Animal Sick: We go come fast when animal dey sick for farm.
  • Tell About Food: Tell farmer wetin go make animal strong strong.
