Himma Youth Farmers Association of Nigeria (HYFAN)

Young Person Dem Wey Dey Learn Farm Work Make Money

Wetin HYFAN Be

HYFAN na organization fo young people wey want farm. We teach young person how make farm work give money well-well, from plant crop to sell am. We go reduce di number wey young person no get work na make new successful farm person for Adamawa.

Wetin We Dey Do

  • Get Teacher: Bring old farm person make dem teach young person.
  • Give Money Start: Help get money wey go start farm.
  • Teach New Way Make Farm: Teach young person new machine wey go make farm work easy.
