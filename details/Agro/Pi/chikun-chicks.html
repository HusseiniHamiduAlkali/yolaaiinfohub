Wetin Chikun Chicks Be

Chikun Chicks na business wey focus on sell small chicken wey just born. We dey help chicken farmers for Adamawa na area around here by make sure say dem get healthy chicken wey strong well-well, na dat go make dia chicken business work well-well. We dey make sure say our chicken fine-fine wey we sell am.

Wetin We Dey Offer

  • Small Chicken Wey Just Born: Healthy chicken fo meat na fo egg.
  • Tell Farmers What To Do: Know how bout farm setup, food fo chicken, na how fight chicken sickness.
  • Chicken Thing: We get plate fo chicken food, thing fo chicken drink, na oda stuff.
