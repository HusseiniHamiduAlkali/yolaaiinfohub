About Our Brand

La'crest Frozen Chicken is a leading brand in the frozen food sector, providing a convenient and high-quality source of protein to households and food businesses in Yola. Our frozen chicken is hygienically processed, carefully packaged, and stored to ensure it retains its freshness, flavor, and nutritional value. We are committed to upholding strict food safety standards, guaranteeing a safe and wholesome product for our consumers.

Product Highlights

  • Hygienically Processed: Prepared in a clean and controlled environment.
  • Convenient Packaging: Various sizes available for families and businesses.
