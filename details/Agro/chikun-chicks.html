About Chikun Chicks

Chikun Chicks is a specialized poultry enterprise focused on the production and sale of high-quality day-old chicks. We provide a crucial service to poultry farmers in Adamawa and the wider region by ensuring they have access to healthy, genetically sound chicks that are a strong foundation for a profitable poultry business. We are committed to strict biosecurity measures and expert breeding practices to guarantee the quality and vigor of our chicks.

Our Services

  • Day-Old Broiler & Layer Chicks: Healthy chicks for meat and egg production.
  • Poultry Consultation: Expert advice on farm setup, feeding, and disease management.
  • Poultry Equipment: Supply of feeders, drinkers, and other essential equipment.
