About Wakili Farms

Wakili Poultry Farms is a leading commercial poultry enterprise in Yola, specializing in the breeding and rearing of chickens for meat and eggs. Our farm is committed to employing modern, humane, and sustainable practices to ensure the health and quality of our birds. We provide a consistent and reliable supply of high-quality eggs and poultry products to local markets and restaurants, contributing significantly to the state's protein supply. Our focus is on maintaining the highest standards of hygiene and animal welfare to deliver superior products to our customers.

Our Products

  • Fresh Eggs: Sourced daily from our healthy, well-fed layers.
  • Broiler Chickens: Healthy and plump chickens for meat.
  • Day-Old Chicks: For other poultry farmers in the region.
