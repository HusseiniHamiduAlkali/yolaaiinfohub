Ngurore Livestock Market

Adamawa's Premier Hub for Cattle and Livestock Trade

About the Market

The Ngurore Livestock Market is a major commercial center and a vital component of the local economy. Located just outside Yola, it serves as a central point for the trading of cattle, goats, sheep, and other livestock. The market attracts traders and buyers from across Adamawa State and neighboring regions, facilitating a robust trade in livestock. It plays a crucial role in the supply chain for meat production and is a key source of income for herders and traders.

Market Activities

  • Buying and Selling: A bustling market for commercial and private transactions.
  • Veterinary Services: On-site health checks for animals to ensure quality and safety.
  • Transportation Hub: A key center for the movement of livestock to various parts of the country.
