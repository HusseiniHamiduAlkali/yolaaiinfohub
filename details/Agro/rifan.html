Rice Farmers Association of Nigeria (RIFAN)

Driving Rice Production and Food Security in Adamawa

About RIFAN Adamawa Chapter

The Adamawa State Chapter of the Rice Farmers Association of Nigeria (RIFAN) is a specialized body dedicated to supporting rice farmers and boosting rice production in the state. As rice is a staple food and a major crop in Adamawa, our association plays a crucial role in ensuring food security and economic prosperity. We provide members with access to quality seeds, fertilizer, and modern farming equipment, as well as connect them to off-takers and processing companies.

Our Activities

  • Input Distribution: Facilitating the distribution of inputs under government-backed programs.
  • Training Sessions: Conducting training on best practices in rice cultivation, from planting to harvesting.
  • Market Linkage: Negotiating fair prices and connecting farmers to profitable markets.
  • Advocacy: Representing the interests of rice farmers to state and federal authorities.
