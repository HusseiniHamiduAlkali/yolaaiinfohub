About Our Mill

Rico Gado Quality Feed Mills is a top-tier animal feed production company providing a wide range of nutritious and balanced feed for poultry, fish, and other livestock. Our feed formulations are scientifically developed to ensure optimal growth, health, and productivity of your animals. We use high-quality, locally sourced raw materials and adhere to strict quality control standards to produce feed that delivers consistent results. We are a trusted partner for commercial farms and small-scale livestock owners.

Product Lines

  • Poultry Feed: Broiler starter, finisher, and layer feeds.
  • Fish Feed: High-protein feed for catfish and other aquatic species.
  • Livestock Feed: Feed concentrates for cattle, goats, and sheep.
