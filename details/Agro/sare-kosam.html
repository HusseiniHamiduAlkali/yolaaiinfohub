About Share Kosam

Share Kosam is a local enterprise in Yola specializing in the production and distribution of fresh dairy products. The name "Kosam" means milk in the local Fulfude language, reflecting our commitment to providing pure, fresh, and wholesome milk to the community. We work directly with local dairy farmers to ensure that our products are of the highest quality. We play a key role in the dairy value chain, providing a stable market for milk producers and a reliable source of nutritious dairy products for consumers.

Our Products

  • Fresh Milk: Sourced daily from local dairy farms.
  • Yogurt & Cream: Processed and packaged for consumption.
  • Cheese: Locally made cheese from fresh milk.
