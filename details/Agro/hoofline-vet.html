About Our Practice

Hoof-line Veterinary is a specialized veterinary practice in Yola, with a primary focus on the health and well-being of livestock and large farm animals. We provide comprehensive veterinary services to farmers, herders, and commercial livestock operators. Our team of experts is highly experienced in managing the unique health challenges faced by cattle, goats, sheep, and other large animals. We are committed to supporting the agricultural community by ensuring the health and productivity of their livestock.

Our Expertise

  • Disease Prevention: Prophylactic vaccinations and deworming programs.
  • Reproductive Health: Breeding services and management for livestock.
  • Emergency Farm Calls: Rapid response for sick or injured farm animals.
  • Nutritional Advice: Guidance on feed and supplements for optimal health.
