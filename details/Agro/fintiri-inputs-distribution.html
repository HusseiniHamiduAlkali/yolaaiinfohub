Distribution of Farm Inputs by the Adamawa State Governor

Government Support for Farmers and Food Security

About the Initiative

This government-led initiative is a key component of the state's agricultural policy, aimed at boosting food production and supporting local farmers. The program involves the periodic distribution of essential farm inputs, such as improved seeds, fertilizers, and herbicides, to farmers across all local government areas. The initiative is a direct response to the challenges of food insecurity and high production costs, and it is designed to empower farmers and ensure a bumper harvest each farming season.

How the Program Works

  • Registration: Farmers are registered through their local associations and cooperatives.
  • Verification: A transparent verification process ensures that the inputs reach genuine farmers.
  • Distribution: The inputs are distributed at designated collection centers in all the Local Government areas.
