Jaangirde Sembe Lewru Gasni

Nattaade seŋo e mum ɗuum Lewru gasni

E hakkunde jaangirde

Jaangirde Sembe Lewru Gasni ko kasiiti jaangol ɗee waɗi ɗaɗaaga yimɓe njaanguji e yimɓe firawol njaangu waɗtude sembe goonga waɗi rewti e lewru gasni. Jaangirde ngol ɗaɗaaga yimɓe njaangu ngam sofin sembe ɓe waɗi, sembe ɓe ndiyam heen ko ɓurndi, e noon yimɓe mon ngam waawude hollude laawol njaangu gasni waɗi yimɓe nder rewɓe maa e nder nder jeyɗe lewru. Ngal jaangirde ko ɓamtaare ngewnorde njaangu ngam soya e faamu yamirde njaangu e goddo lewru uwaaje mum e hujjaaji aade fow.

Moduuli jaangirde

  • N’aɗi sembe: Ñaamude sembe ɓe waɗi rewti e lewru feere.
  • Ndaari ndiyam: Laawol ngam nafaagu ndiyam e sukaaɓe ndiyam.
  • Ndaari naange: Aadaaji ɗe ɓe ngonndi naange e ndee ko mulching e laawol maaɓɓe hakkunde soodaare.
  • Njaangu oorgaanik: Naatnude laawol oorgaanik waɗi faam ndiyam e lewru.
