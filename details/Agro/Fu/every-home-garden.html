Guddal ley-ley nder hoggu kaɗe - Gollol 'First Lady'

Seeto Jeewle Caggal haa Gollol Howru Buuɗu

Baɗe Seeto

Seeto "Guddal ley-ley nder hoggu kaɗe" ko seeto jeewle caggal joo watii e nappade golleeji e handetaaku ɓilteere. Seeto oo hokkadinol faamere haa guddal galey nanngal haa nappade handetaaku. Seeto nappade jiggu kaɗe e gollol gooraaji.

Caggal Seeto

  • Galey e Gollol Taame: Nappade galey e gollol taame jeewle.
  • Jaajeefol e Gorko: Jaajeefol gollol guddal e kela-kela.
  • Waala Jamma: Diy golleeji haa waala caggal e jiggu.
