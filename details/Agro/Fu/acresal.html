Goddo ACRES AL Saare Adamawa

Laawol noon waɗde njaangu makko e nder kawtal

E hakkunde jiitol

Jiitol ACRES AL (Adamawa State Collaborative for Rural Economic Sustainability) ko laawol nguurndam e teelte nguurndam tawii hono jokkude njaangu makko e Saare Adamawa. Jiitol ngol waɗiima huuwde e dow yowitaaji aduna, ndiyam deɓɓe, e so’administration e makko ngol ngoodi haa ndee njaatude so wonaa nde. Ko ngam jokkude ɓeen junngoɓe nguurndam, NGOji e jawdi haande nguurndam ɗumɓe, ɓe iwti laawol ngal ngam fotaade kesum e yimɓe nder saare ngam njaabe laawol sadugo saare.

Ɓeenɓe jiitol

  • Njaoum mum ɓurndu: Foti ɗee ko ɓataaki ɓe mblugol ngasgobe kaŋko, risi, e garri ndi nde tawii jokkitii ɓe e nder mawɗe e suumaaji.
  • Ndeerte finans ngel ɓuri mbay: Joynude ndeerteeji e senngooji ngam yimɓe njarɓe.
  • Taƴre ngel njaaji: Yiwde nder njilli saare e boyngal jowroore.
  • Lewru gasni e jam: Ina dañnooɓe laawol lewru gasni ngam yimɓe njarɓe anndude ko ɗum waɗi e lewru feere.
