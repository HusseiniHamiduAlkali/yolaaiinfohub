Garee Togal Gollol Gollaaji Yola

Walluwal Golleeji ngel Innovation e Jawdi

Baɗe Garee

Garee togal gollol gollaaji kaɓe hadde galey nanngal haa golleeji, agro-dealers, ngoongal dawle, e ngajaata. Garee nappade jiggu gaas haa nappade innovation gollaaji gooraaji: galey, galyeeje, e gollol. Ko bayti mahmudu haa golleeji haa waala caggal togal nanngal.

Maaɓe Garee

  • Bayti Nappade: Nappade ɗam gollol gollaaji nanngal e waala.
  • Jaajeefol: Jaajeefol gollol gooraaji gaas e gollol ɓilteere.
  • Walluwal Caggal: Bayti golleeji haa walluwal mahmudu e golleeji nanngal.
