Jamma Golleeji Ndungo Nigeria (RIFAN)

Nappade Togal Ndungo e Howru Buuɗu e Adamawa

Baɗe RIFAN Adamawa Chapter

RIFAN Adamawa ko ngoongal mahmudu ɓe watii e nappade golleeji ndungo. Ndungo ko gollol mahmudu e Adamawa, jamma amen ko caggal mahmudu haa howru buuɗu. Amen nappade golleeji galey mahmudu, fertilizer, e galyeeje gooraaji.

Gollol Amen

  • Wadata Gollol: Walluwal wadata gollol ngel gollol seeto.
  • Jaajeefol: Jaajeefol mahmudu haa togal ndungo.
  • Walluwal Kaasee: Walluwal golleeji haa kaasee togal mahmudu.
  • Hokkadinol: Hokkadinol golleeji ndungo nder gollol dawle.
