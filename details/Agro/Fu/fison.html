Jamma Gollol Hura Nigeria (FISON)

Baɗe FISON

FISON ko ngoongal mahmudu ɓe watii e nappade gollol hura e aquaculture e Nigeria. E Yola, FISON watii e nappade golleeji hura e sayiwde howru buuɗu. Ngoongal oo walluwal golleeji hura, sello gooraaji hura, ngajaata, e golleeji ho nappade.
