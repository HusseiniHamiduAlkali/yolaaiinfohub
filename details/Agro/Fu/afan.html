Ko Daaɗoowo Njaanguji Nigeria (AFAN)

Ɓeydude e kokkaade njaanguji e Saare Adamawa

E AFAN

Ko Daaɗoowo Njaanguji Nigeria (AFAN) ko laawol geseeji njaanguji e leydi. Haɓɓere Yola ko ngo ɗaɓɓitii yimɓe njaanguji e Saare Adamawa. Ngal laawol ngol ɗee nder fiya e hakkunde yimɓe njaanguji, ina waawi ɓurndude yamirde laawol njaangu to kaɓe mum, e ɗeɓɓude heɓugol seedaaji, lendooji e cuunooji nguurndam. Min NGO ngol ɗee ngoodi haa seƴtude jam e fotaade dow laabi e yimɓe njaanguji, tan e waɗtude fow ɓe njahɗaade kala feere.

Ɓeenɓe amen

  • Feereede: Nandude njaanguji ɗe njogora e heɓugol goonga.
  • Jaangirde: Ndo onde njaŋnude aade njaangu fayde, naange caɗe e ngal boobere.
  • Heɓugol Ndeerteeji: Ndaɗaɓde junngo e laawol, seedaaji, e njaŋorde.
  • Ɓarkisogi Koojungo: Noon e na’i e ɓe waɗi yimɓe ngam na’i liiwo.
