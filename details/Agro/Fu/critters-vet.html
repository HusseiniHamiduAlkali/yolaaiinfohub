E klinik amen

Critters Veterinary nder Yola ko klinik vetɓe ɓuri ɓamtaare, ɗee ina ngiɗɗo ndaari e jeyaaɓe dow feereje belɗe, nyamaaji suudu e ndiyam daande. Leemɗi mawɗo amen woodi wondi wooju, ɓe anndii woodi ɗakkuuji neeɓe ɗe, e ɓe ina heɓa ndeerteeji to mbiɗɗoɓe faa caggal, e soɓɓe ciftorde bangi. Min ina ndokki yimɓe ɓe naraade konngi, ɗum kadi karaama e mum woɗɓe faam. Klinik amen ko waɗi ngorndam ngam ndokki naatnude ndiyam e gese e nder sojji.

Ngol teeŋol amen

  • Ƴeewtude mbayle: Teeŋol mawɗo ka njullude e ndoppi mbayle e mbaye.
  • Teeŋol nyamaaji pere: Ndaari hoore, dardare e nyamaaji pere fere.
  • Ndaari siginar e kaña: E meemooji njaatii kaña e welndeeji farilla.
  • Labooratoori diagnoosi: Nyeenugol e takko-ngol nder laaboore ngam faamɗe ɗum e ndew.
