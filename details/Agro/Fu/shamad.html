Gollol Ndungo Mahmudu haa Ndungo Jeewle

Baɗe Gollol Amen

Shamad Rice Processing Limited ko kasoore togal ndungo mahmudu ɓe togge ndungo awre ndugol e ndungo togge joo togol haa hoga. Amen walluwal golleeji ndungo nder Adamawa, nappade golleeji kaasee mahmudu haa togal nanngal. Amen watii e nappade "grow local, eat local" e nappade seeto dawle.

Gollol Amen

  • Togal Ndungo Awre: Togge ndungo awre e ndungo togge.
  • Seerti e Pakkeeru: Nappade ndungo togge, dilan.
  • Caggal Togal: Nappade kaasee mahmudu haa golleeji ndungo.
