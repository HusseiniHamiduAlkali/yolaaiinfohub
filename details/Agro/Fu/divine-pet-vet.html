E klinik amen

Divine Pet Veterinary ko klinik vetɓe ko dulɗi nder Yola, ɗee ina woni teeŋol ndawmen e yimɓe maɓɓe kala. Leemɗi amen ɓe ina jogii laawol anndugol e kapannde yimɓe komɗe e leemɗi, ɓe yaɓɓii e kelende e humpitooɓe dow nafaagu yimɓe. Min yiɗi yimɓe garma, e min ina jogori e noon ngam naftude ɓe sheedaaji mawɗo, heɓugol jam e ɓernde kadi. Golfu amen woodi ngollu ngam ɓe imɓe ɗeeyaan e ɓernde ɗi nder barke e nder laamirde.»

Teeŋol ɗe ñaam

  • Ƴeewtude kala faggu & kaliɗe: Fotaade yimɓe maɓɓeŋ e jam e iwde.
  • Ndaari njillito: Taƴitde 24/7 gaaɗa ngam ɓagon njillito.
  • Iraade njillaaji: Ngam bawdi spaying, neutering e njillaaji maɓɓe ɗe ɓe feewi.
  • Konseltugo leydi: Laawol ardooje ngamɗe yimɓe maɓɓe e shape e jannde jam.
