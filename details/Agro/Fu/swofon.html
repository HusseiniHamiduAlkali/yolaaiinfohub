Ngoongal Golleeji Maɓɓe Ɓilteere Nigeria (SWOFON)

Nappade Maɓɓe Gollaaji haa Howru Buuɗu

Baɗe SWOFON

SWOFON ko ngoongal non-governmental ɓe watii e nappade golleeji maɓɓe ɓilteere. Amen wahitinude gollol mahmudu ɓe maɓɓe waawi gollaaji. Ngoongal amen nappade maɓɓe hokkadinol caggal, jiggu jawdi, e gollol gooraaji.

Seeto Amen

  • Jaajeefol Mahmudu: Jaajeefol haa handetaaku daalga, gollol sello, e gollaaji gooraaji.
  • Walluwal Jawdi: Waylude maɓɓe daalga dure e golleeji.
  • Hokkadinol: Hokkadinol gollol ɓe mahmudu maɓɓe e gollol dawle.
