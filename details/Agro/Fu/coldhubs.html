Centru Jaajol Coldhubs Jambutu Yola

Njifle njaajol lewru ndiyam e ciyâre ɓernde

E hakkunde nguurndam

Centru jaajol Coldhubs nder Jambutu, Yola, ina heɓi laawol mum ko dume njaangu faa jeyɗeahaaji ƴeewtude ngam seernude njilli ɓernde e hulaaɓe. Jaajol mum powered e naange ina ɗaɗa yimɓe njaanguji, joge, e tajaaji ɓernde ngol tuma haa ɓe ndiyam jokkude kesum. Ko ngam jaajude ɓernde mum, min njaɓataa ɓe walaa suusude, min ɓurtii ɓe hono ahaali anndaa, ndawrude yaasi mum, e min njaɓtaa ɓe jokkude ɓernde mum so o weltii, tawii ɗum ɓuri ina heɓa cuɗɗi e nder gese.
