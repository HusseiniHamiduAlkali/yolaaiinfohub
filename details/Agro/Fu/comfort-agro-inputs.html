Comfort Agro Chemicals

Sariyaade njaangu e ɓuri fota

E nguurndam amen

Comfort Agro Chemicals ko jom finiiraade laawol njaangu anndaa tuuba. Ɗiɗo am ko ndee heɓugol yimɓe njaangu seedaaji fow ngol nice e easy ɗum holli. Min ina waɗi konsultasyon maɓɓe jokkude yimɓe njaangu ɗe njikii e cuɓii maa, ngam hollude ko ɓe naftii. Min yaali finnde fayde e yimɓe njaanguji e nder suudu mawɗe, tawa miijo goonga e solfaade mawɗo.

Ko ɗon no waɗi

  • Insecticides: Laawol kuugal hakkeeji e waɗtude fombinaaji.
  • Herbicides: Laawol waɗtude waraabe kadi ko timmitere kadi e kaɗeele.
  • Seedaaji Njaangu: Fitaare fitinaande e minirnuutraaji ngam jom sembe.
