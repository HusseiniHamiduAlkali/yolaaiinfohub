Solinol Mahmudu haa Keesal Gollol Gonngal

Baɗe Sello Amen

Yusash Agro Chemicals ko jokkere mahmudu gollol gooraaji e gollol keesal gollol. Amen mahmudu gollol keesal daltede, pesticide, e fungicide ɓe mahmudu e gonngal. Amen watii e nappade golleeji kuutre gollol kaɗe nder keesal e marado. Amen ko waala gooraaji haa fiilnde gollol togal.

Mahmudu Gollol Amen

  • Herbicides: Gollol toombu nanngal gollol ngernde.
  • Pesticides: Solinol ɗam keese insect nanngal.
  • Fungicides: Kuutre gollol fungal infection e marado.
