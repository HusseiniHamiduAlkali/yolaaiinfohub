Jamma Golleeji Ɓilteere Nigeria

Hokkadinol Gollaaji Jeewle e Togal Buuɗu

Baɗe Jamma Amen

Jamma golleeji ɓilteere ko ngoongal jeewle ɓe hokkadinol golleeji ɓilteere nanngal ɓe ko jom gollaaji Nigeria. Jamma amen e Adamawa watii e hadde marayuji golleeji ɓilteere: jawdi ɗum haa daalga, jiggu gooraaji gaas, e waɗdiminji baayagol. Amen walluwal caggal mahmudu haa golleeji.

Gollol Amen

  • Biye Jamma: Walluwal heccude galey e fertilizer njaati dum.
  • Waala Gollol: Gollol ngel baayagol hokkadinol mahmudu.
  • Hokkadinol e Waala: Hokkadinol golleeji ɓilteere nder gollol dawle.
