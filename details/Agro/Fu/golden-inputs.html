Golden Agroinputs Supply

Caggal Mahmudu haa Gollol Gollaaji Mahmudu

Baɗe Sello Amen

Golden Agroinputs Supply ko jokkere mahmudu gollol gollaaji ɓilteere. Amen watii e nappade golleeji galey mahmudu, fertilizer, e gollol keesal. Amen ko caggal mahmudu haa golleeji.

Gollol Nappade

  • Galey Mahmudu: Ndungo, ndungo, soya, e ngernde nanngal.
  • Fertilizers: NPK, Urea, e nutrient nanngal.
  • Gollol Keesal e Toombu: Gollol gooraaji keesal daltede.
