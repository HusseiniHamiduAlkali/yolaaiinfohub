Ngajaata e Nappade Rawdu (RMRDC)

Nappade Innovation e Togal Baɗe Gollaaji

Baɗe RMRDC

RMRDC ko ngoongal dawle ɓe watii e nappade togal industri ngel ngajaata e nappade rawdu jeewle. Kasoore Yola watii e nappade gollol gooraaji Adamawa. Gollol amen ko maaɓe rawdu jeewle, huccugol njure e nappade caggal walluwal golleeji ngel industri nanngal.

Gollol Caggal

  • Ngajaata Rawdu: Ngajaata e jambaynde rawdu jeewle gootal.
  • Ngajaata e Nappade: Ngajaata jiggu gaas haa nappade ɓilteere togal.
  • Waala Gooraaji: Waala gooraaji sello e seeto agro-processing.
