Coldhubs Storage Center Jambutu Yola

Modern Cold Storage Solutions for Fresh Produce

About Our Facility

Coldhubs Storage Center in Jambutu, Yola, provides a vital service in the agricultural value chain by offering a clean, reliable, and affordable cold storage solution for perishable farm produce. Our solar-powered cold rooms help farmers, traders, and food processors reduce post-harvest losses, preserve the quality of their products, and extend their shelf life. By enabling farmers to store their produce, we help them sell at a better price when demand is high, thereby increasing their income and reducing food waste in the community.

Our Services

  • Cold Storage: Short-term and long-term storage for fruits, vegetables, and other perishables.
  • Temperature Control: Optimal temperature settings for a variety of products.
  • Logistics Support: Assistance with product handling and transportation.
