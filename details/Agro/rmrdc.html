Driving Innovation and Value Addition in Agriculture

About the RMRDC

The Raw Material Research and Development Council (RMRDC) is a government agency with a mandate to support industrial growth through the research and development of local raw materials. The Yola office focuses on agricultural resources unique to Adamawa State. Our role is to identify and promote the commercial utilization of these materials, connecting farmers to industries that can process their produce into high-value products. We conduct research, provide technical assistance, and facilitate partnerships to ensure that the state's agricultural wealth is fully utilized for economic development.

Our Key Functions

  • Raw Material Survey: Cataloging and analyzing the agricultural raw materials available in the state.
  • Research & Development: Conducting research on new uses and value-added processing for agricultural products.
  • Technical Support: Providing technical expertise to local businesses and entrepreneurs in the agro-processing sector.
