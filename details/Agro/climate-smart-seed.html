Climate-Smart Seed Production Training

Building Resilience in the Face of Climate Change

About the Training

The Climate-Smart Seed Production Training is an essential program designed to equip farmers and agricultural professionals with the knowledge and skills to produce high-quality seeds that are resilient to the effects of climate change. The training covers topics such as selecting drought-resistant varieties, developing seeds with a shorter growing cycle, and implementing sustainable farming practices that conserve water and improve soil health. This initiative is crucial for ensuring that agriculture remains productive and profitable in an era of unpredictable weather patterns and environmental challenges.

Training Modules

  • Seed Selection: Choosing varieties that thrive in specific climatic conditions.
  • Water Management: Techniques for efficient irrigation and water conservation.
  • Soil Conservation: Practices like mulching and no-till farming to protect soil.
  • Organic Farming: Introduction to organic methods that are beneficial for the environment.
