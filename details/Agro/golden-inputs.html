About Our Business

Golden Agroinputs Supply is a leading dealer of high-quality agricultural inputs, serving farmers in Yola and the wider Adamawa region. We understand that the foundation of a successful harvest is the quality of the inputs. Our mission is to provide farmers with genuine seeds, effective fertilizers, and reliable crop protection products to help them maximize their yield and improve their livelihoods. We are committed to transparency and providing expert advice to our customers.

Our Product Range

  • High-Yield Seeds: Maize, rice, soybeans, and other cereal seeds.
  • Fertilizers: NPK, Urea, and other soil-specific nutrients.
  • Pesticides & Herbicides: A wide selection of crop protection chemicals.
