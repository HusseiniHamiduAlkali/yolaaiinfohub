IITA Training for Farmers

Na-enye ndị Ọrụ Ugbo Ọmụma Ugbo Oge a

Banyere Ọzụzụ A

The International Institute of Tropical Agriculture (IITA) rụrụ ọrụ zuru oke ọzụzụ maka ndị ọrụ ugbo ihe karịrị 35,000 na Yola na mpaghara ya. Ihe omume a chepụtara iji mebie oghere n'etiti usoro ugbo ụzọ ôma na usoro ugbo na-adịgide adịgide oge a. Ọzụzụ a kpukötara isiokwu dị iche iche nke elekwasị anya na ịkwalite mmepụta mkpụrụ, ahụike ala, na mmepụta ogige zuru oke. Site n'inye ndị ọrụ ugbo nka na ọmụma ọhụrụ, mmemme a chọrọ imụte nri ziri ezi na ogo ụlọ ọrụ n'mpaghara.

Modul Ọzụzụ

  • Njikwa Ala: Usoro nnwale ala, nmeghachi nri, na mgbochi ala na-esipu.
  • Njikwa Pests na Ọrịa: Atụmatụ njikwa pests gunitere na iji pesticides nke ọma.
  • Mkpụrụ Mmụba: Mmalite na ụdị mkpụrụ dị mmepụta elu na ike iguzogide ihu igwe.
  • Teknụ After-Ụ: Ụzọ belata ọnwụ after-ụ na ime ogologo ndụ ngwaahịa.
