Jambutu Vegetables and Fruits Market

Yola's Vibrant Center for Fresh Produce

About the Market

Known locally as "Kasuwan Gwari," the Jambutu Vegetables and Fruits Market is the largest and most vibrant market for fresh produce in Yola. The market is a meeting point for local farmers and city residents, offering a wide variety of fresh, seasonal vegetables and fruits sourced directly from farms in and around Adamawa State. It is a critical hub for the distribution of fresh produce and plays a significant role in ensuring food availability for the urban population.

What You'll Find

  • A diverse array of local and seasonal vegetables.
  • Fresh fruits, including mangoes, oranges, and watermelons.
  • Spices and herbs, sourced locally and from other regions.
  • Processed farm products like dried yams and local condiments.
