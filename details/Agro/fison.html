Fisheries Society of Nigeria (FISON)

Professional Organization for Fish Farmers and Fisheries Professionals

About FISON

The Fisheries Society of Nigeria (FISON) is a professional organization dedicated to the advancement of fisheries and aquaculture in Nigeria. With a strong presence in Yola and Adamawa State, FISON works to promote sustainable fish farming practices, improve livelihoods of fish farmers, and contribute to food security. The organization brings together fish farmers, fisheries professionals, researchers, and policymakers to share knowledge, develop best practices, and advocate for fisheries development in the region.

Core Activities

  • Technical Training: Capacity building in fish farming techniques and aquaculture management
  • Research and Development: Promoting research into improved fish species and farming methods
  • Market Linkages: Connecting farmers with markets and buyers
  • Policy Advocacy: Advocating for supportive policies and regulations in the fisheries sector
  • Networking: Facilitating peer learning and collaboration among members
  • Quality Assurance: Promoting food safety and quality standards in fish production

Aquaculture Services

  • Fish Breeding: Hatchery services and fingerling production
  • Feed Production: Formulation and supply of quality fish feed
  • Pond Management: Technical advice on pond construction and management
  • Disease Management: Support in diagnosing and treating fish diseases
  • Water Quality Testing: Services for monitoring water parameters
  • Post-Harvest Processing: Training in fish processing and preservation techniques

Training Programs

  • Basic Aquaculture Course: Introduction to fish farming for beginners
  • Advanced Fish Farming: Intensive production systems and commercial aquaculture
  • Fingerling Production: Breeding and hatchery management techniques
  • Fish Processing and Value Addition: Smoking, packaging, and marketing
  • Business Management: Financial planning and profitability in fish farming
  • Environmental Management: Sustainable and eco-friendly farming practices

Membership Benefits

  • Access to technical training and capacity building programs
  • Regular newsletters and updates on fisheries research and best practices
  • Networking opportunities with other fish farmers and professionals
  • Preferential rates on equipment and input supplies
  • Market information and linkage support
  • Representation in fisheries policy discussions
  • Insurance and social support programs
