Himma Youth Farmers Association of Nigeria (HYFAN)

Cultivating a New Generation of Agri-Entrepreneurs

About HYFAN

Himma, a local word meaning "hard work and dedication," perfectly encapsulates the spirit of the Himma Youth Farmers Association of Nigeria (HYFAN). Our organization is committed to encouraging and supporting young people to embrace agriculture as a viable and profitable career. We provide training on modern agribusiness models, from crop production to value addition and marketing. Our goal is to reduce youth unemployment and create a new class of innovative and successful agri-preneurs in Adamawa State.

Our Activities

  • Mentorship Programs: Connecting young farmers with experienced mentors.
  • Start-up Funding: Facilitating access to grants and seed funding for agricultural projects.
  • Technology Transfer: Introducing young farmers to new technologies like precision agriculture.
