About Our Brand

Easy Life Frozen Chicken is a popular brand known for its affordability and consistent quality. Our goal is to make healthy, protein-rich food accessible to every household. We provide a wide range of frozen chicken parts that are perfect for everyday cooking. We partner with local farmers and distributors to ensure a reliable and steady supply of our products to markets and retail stores across the region, making healthy living an "easy life" for everyone.

Product Offerings

  • Frozen Chicken: Various cuts including wings, laps, and gizzards.
  • Affordable Pricing: Competitive prices without compromising on quality.
  • Wide Availability: Our products can be found in most retail outlets in Yola.
