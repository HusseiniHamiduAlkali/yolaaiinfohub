Jambutu Smoked Fish Market

A Delicacy Hub for Locally Sourced Fish

About the Market

The Jambutu Smoked Fish Market is a unique and specialized commercial center in Yola, famous for its high-quality smoked fish. The market sources fish from local rivers and fish farms, which are then expertly smoked and preserved for sale. It serves as a major distribution point for this local delicacy, attracting buyers from within the state and beyond. The market supports local fishermen and processors, contributing to the a of the region's aquatic resources.

What We Offer

  • A wide variety of smoked fish species.
  • Fresh fish and seafood are available on certain days.
  • Expert advice on fish selection and preparation.
