About Our Clinic

Critters Veterinary in Yola is a full-service veterinary clinic that provides expert medical care for a wide variety of animals, including pets, small farm animals, and exotic species. Our team is trained to handle a diverse range of cases, from routine wellness checks for cats and dogs to treating more complex conditions in other animals. We are committed to providing personalized and compassionate care, ensuring that every animal that comes through our doors receives the attention and treatment it deserves. Our clinic is dedicated to promoting animal health and well-being in the community.

Our Services

  • General Pet Care: Comprehensive services for dogs and cats.
  • Small Animal Services: Care for rabbits, guinea pigs, and other small animals.
  • Surgical & Dental Care: From routine dental cleanings to complex surgeries.
  • Laboratory Diagnostics: On-site testing for quick and accurate diagnoses.
