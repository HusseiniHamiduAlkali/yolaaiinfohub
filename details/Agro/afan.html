All Farmers Association of Nigeria (AFAN)

Unifying and Empowering Farmers in Adamawa State

About AFAN

The All Farmers Association of Nigeria (AFAN) is the umbrella body for all agricultural practitioners in the country. The Yola Branch serves as the primary representative body for farmers in Adamawa State. Our mission is to advocate for farmers' rights, promote modern farming techniques, and facilitate access to government grants, loans, and resources. We are a non-governmental organization dedicated to improving the welfare and productivity of all farmers, from smallholders to commercial producers.

Our Objectives

  • Advocacy: Representing farmers' interests at all levels of government.
  • Training: Organizing workshops on modern farming practices, soil management, and pest control.
  • Access to Resources: Helping members secure access to agricultural inputs, credit facilities, and markets.
  • Community Building: Fostering a strong network of support among farmers in the state.
