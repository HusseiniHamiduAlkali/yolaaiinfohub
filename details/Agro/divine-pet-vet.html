About Our Clinic

Divine Pet Veterinary is a dedicated veterinary clinic in Yola, providing a wide range of medical services for your cherished pets. Our clinic is staffed by experienced and caring veterinarians and technicians who are passionate about animal welfare. We believe that pets are part of the family, and we are committed to providing them with the highest standard of care, from routine check-ups to advanced medical treatments. Our facility is equipped to handle various pet health needs in a stress-free and welcoming environment.

Services Offered

  • Routine Check-ups & Vaccinations: Ensuring your pet is healthy and protected.
  • Emergency Care: 24/7 service for urgent medical cases.
  • Surgical Procedures: Including spaying, neutering, and other specialized surgeries.
  • Nutritional Counseling: Expert advice on pet diet and wellness.
