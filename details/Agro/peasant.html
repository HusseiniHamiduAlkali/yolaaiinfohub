Peasant Farmers Association Yola

A Collective Voice for Traditional Farmers

About Our Association

The Peasant Farmers Association of Yola is a grassroots organization representing the interests of traditional and subsistence farmers. Our members often rely on traditional methods and are deeply connected to the land. Our association works to preserve traditional knowledge while also introducing sustainable and simple modern techniques that can enhance productivity without compromising traditional practices. We are a community of farmers helping each other to navigate the challenges of climate change, market fluctuations, and resource scarcity.

Our Focus

  • Traditional Knowledge Preservation: Documenting and sharing traditional farming methods.
  • Resource Sharing: Facilitating the sharing of tools, seeds, and labor among members.
  • Advocacy: Representing the needs of peasant farmers in government forums.
