'Every Home a Garden' - Movement by the First Lady

A Grassroots Initiative for Food Self-Sufficiency

About the Movement

The 'Every Home a Garden' movement, championed by the First Lady of Adamawa State, is a grassroots initiative designed to promote household food security and healthy eating. The movement encourages every family to cultivate a small garden, no matter the size, to grow fresh vegetables and fruits for their consumption. It is based on the principle that if every home contributes to its own food supply, the community as a whole will be more resilient and self-sufficient. The initiative provides free starter kits, including seeds and basic tools, and offers training on simple gardening techniques.

Program Components

  • Seed & Tool Distribution: Providing free resources to get families started.
  • Training & Workshops: Teaching basic gardening skills and composting techniques.
  • Community Support: Creating a network of gardeners for mutual support and knowledge sharing.
