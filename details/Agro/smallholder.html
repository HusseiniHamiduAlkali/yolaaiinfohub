Supporting Grassroots Agriculture and Food Production

About Our Association

The Small Holder Farmers Association of Nigeria is a grassroots organization that represents the millions of small-scale farmers who are the backbone of Nigeria's food production. Our association in Adamawa State is dedicated to addressing the challenges faced by these farmers, including poor access to credit, limited knowledge of modern farming techniques, and exploitation by middlemen. We work to create a collective voice for our members and provide them with the resources they need to thrive.

Our Services

  • Group Purchasing: Facilitating the collective purchase of seeds and fertilizer at lower prices.
  • Extension Services: Organizing visits from agricultural extension workers to provide tailored advice.
  • Advocacy & Support: Advocating for government policies that protect and support smallholder farmers.
