Agriculture Input Trade Fair Yola

Connecting Farmers with Innovation and Resources

About the Trade Fair

The annual Agriculture Input Trade Fair in Yola is a major event that brings together farmers, agro-dealers, government agencies, and researchers from across the state. The fair serves as a platform for showcasing the latest innovations in agricultural technology, equipment, and inputs, including seeds, fertilizers, and pesticides. It is an invaluable opportunity for farmers to interact directly with suppliers, learn about new products, and gain access to the resources they need to improve their farming practices and increase their yields.

Key Features

  • Exhibition Stalls: Featuring a wide range of agricultural products and services.
  • Training Sessions: Workshops on new farming techniques and best practices.
  • Networking Opportunities: A chance for farmers to connect with experts and fellow practitioners.
