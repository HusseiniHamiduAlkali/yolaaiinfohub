Health Outreach Programs

Promoting Community Health and Wellness

About Our Health Programs

Our community health outreach programs aim to improve the overall health and well-being of Yola residents through education, prevention, and accessible healthcare services. We work closely with local healthcare providers and volunteers to bring essential health services to those who need them most.

Volunteer Opportunities

  • Health Education: Conduct workshops on basic health and hygiene practices.
  • Medical Camps: Assist in organizing and managing free medical check-up camps.
  • First Aid Training: Help teach basic first aid skills to community members.
  • Wellness Programs: Support exercise and nutrition programs for different age groups.
  • Healthcare Navigation: Guide community members in accessing healthcare services.

Requirements and Training

  • Basic healthcare knowledge (training provided)
  • Commitment to confidentiality
  • Regular availability for scheduled programs
  • Completion of health and safety training
  • Good communication and interpersonal skills
