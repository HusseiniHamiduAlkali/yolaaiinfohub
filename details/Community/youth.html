Programs for Young People

About Us

The Youth Association is dedicated to empowering young people in Yola through educational, recreational, and leadership development programs. We provide a platform for youth to develop their skills, pursue their interests, and contribute to community development.

Our Programs

  • Leadership Development: Training and mentorship
  • Sports Activities: Team sports and competitions
  • Academic Support: Tutoring and study groups
  • Creative Arts: Music, dance, and drama
  • Community Service: Volunteer opportunities

How to Join

  • Age Group: 15-25 years
  • Registration: Open year-round
  • Membership Fee: Affordable annual dues
  • Requirements: Parental consent for under 18
