Yolde Pate Youth Development Association

Fostering Community Growth Through Youth Empowerment

Mission: To promote youth development and community advancement in Yolde Pate through education, skill acquisition, and social engagement.

About YPYDA

Yolde Pate Youth Development Association (YPYDA) is a dynamic community organization committed to fostering youth development and community growth in the Yolde Pate area of Yola.

Core Programs

  • Youth Empowerment: Skills training and capacity building
  • Community Development: Infrastructure and environmental projects
  • Education Support: Scholarships and tutoring
  • Sports Development: Youth sports programs
  • Cultural Activities: Heritage preservation initiatives

Development Initiatives

  • Skill Acquisition:
    • Vocational training programs
    • Entrepreneurship development
    • Digital skills training
  • Community Projects:
    • Infrastructure improvement
    • Environmental conservation
    • Health awareness campaigns

Impact Areas

  • Youth employment and entrepreneurship
  • Community infrastructure development
  • Education and skills development
  • Sports and recreation
  • Cultural heritage preservation
