AUN Graduation Ceremony

A Celebration of Academic Excellence and Achievement

About the Ceremony

The American University of Nigeria's graduation ceremony is an annual event that marks the culmination of years of hard work and dedication for its students. It is a grand occasion attended by families, friends, faculty, and dignitaries, celebrating the academic achievements of the graduating class. The ceremony is a source of pride for the university and the Yola community, as it showcases the talent and potential of a new generation of leaders and innovators.

Ceremony Highlights

  • Valedictory Speeches: Inspirational words from top-performing students.
  • Award Presentations: Recognizing students for outstanding academic and extracurricular achievements.
  • Guest Speakers: Addresses from prominent figures in academia and industry.
