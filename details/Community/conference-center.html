International Conference Centre Project

N18.9 Billion Development Initiative

Project Value: N18.9 Billion
Status: Groundbreaking Ceremony Completed

Project Overview

Governor Ahmadu Fintiri has performed the groundbreaking ceremony for the construction of an ultramodern International Conference Centre in Yola, marking a significant milestone in the state's infrastructure development.

Facility Features

  • Main Hall: Large-capacity conference space
  • Meeting Rooms: Multiple breakout spaces
  • Exhibition Area: Flexible display space
  • Modern Amenities: State-of-the-art facilities

Economic Impact

  • Creation of local employment opportunities
  • Boost to tourism and hospitality sector
  • Increased conference hosting capacity
  • Enhanced business infrastructure

Timeline and Implementation

  • Groundbreaking: May 29, 2025
  • Construction Phase: Multiple stages
  • Quality Assurance: International standards
  • Community Input: Ongoing consultation
