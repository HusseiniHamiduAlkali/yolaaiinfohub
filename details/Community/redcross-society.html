Red Cross Society North-east Zonal Branch Yola

Providing Humanitarian Aid and Emergency Services

About the Organization

The Red Cross Society's North-east Zonal Branch in Yola is a vital humanitarian organization dedicated to alleviating human suffering and promoting peace. The branch provides a wide range of services, including emergency first aid, disaster relief, and health and hygiene education. The Red Cross is at the forefront of responding to humanitarian crises in the region, offering assistance to displaced persons, and working with communities to build resilience and preparedness for future emergencies. They rely on a network of dedicated volunteers to carry out their noble mission.

Key Activities

  • Disaster Response: Providing aid and shelter during natural disasters.
  • First Aid Training: Offering life-saving skills to the public.
  • Blood Donation Drives: Encouraging voluntary blood donation to save lives.
