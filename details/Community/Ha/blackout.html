Ayyukan Gyara na TCN na Ci Gaba

Halin Yanzu: Rashin wuta na tsawon kwanaki biyar yana shafar Yola da yankunan kewaye yayin aikin gyara.

Bayanin Halin

Mazauna Yola da Jalingo, manyan biranen jihar Adamawa da Taraba, suna fuskantar rashin wuta na kwanaki biyar saboda muhimman ayyukan gyara da Kamfanin Watsawa na Najeriya (TCN) ke aiwatarwa.

Yankunan da Ake Shafa

  • Yola Kudu: Cikakken rashin wuta
  • Yola Arewa: Cikakken rashin wuta
  • Al'ummomin Kewaye: Ƙarancin samar da wuta
  • Muƙamuƙin Sabis: Aiki da wutar ajiya

Jadawalin Aikin Gyara na TCN

  • Tsawon Lokaci: Kwanaki 5
  • Irin Aikin: Muhimman gyare-gyaren kayayyakin aiki
  • Ranar Kammalawa: Kamar yadda aka tsara
  • Sabuntawar Ci Gaba: Rahotannin kullum suna samuwa

Shawarwarin Al'umma

  • Yi amfani da hanyoyin samar da wuta na madadin inda zai yiwu
  • Ajiye ruwa yayin lokacin rashin wuta
  • Sami fitilu na gaggawa a shirye
  • Duba makwabtan da ke cikin rauni
