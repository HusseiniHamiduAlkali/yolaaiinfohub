Inganta ci gaban matasa ta hanyar wasanni

Game da Gasar

Kofin Gwamna wata gasar kwallon ƙafa ce ta shekara-shekara da Gwamnatin Jihar Adamawa ke tallafawa. Gasar tana haɗa ƙungiyoyin matasa daga dukkan hukumar ƙananan hukumomi don fafatawa akan wannan kofin mai daraja. Ba wasa kawai ba ce; dandali ce na gano basira, karfafa matasa, da haɗa al'umma. Tana haɓaka aiki tare, ladabi, da gasa mai kyau, tana ba matasa hanya mai amfani da kuzari.

Manufofin Gasar

  • Shiga Matasa: Riƙe matasa a cikin ayyuka masu amfani da kuzari.
  • Gano Basira: Neman 'yan wasa masu kwarewa don wakiltar jiha a matakai mafi girma.
  • Gina Al'umma: Haɗa al'ummomi don tallafawa ƙungiyoyin su na gida.
