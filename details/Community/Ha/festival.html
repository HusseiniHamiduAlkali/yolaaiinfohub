Bikin Al'adu

Bikin Shekara-shekara na Al'adu na Gari

Cikakkun Bayanai na Taro

  • Rana: 15-17 Agusta, 2025
  • Lokaci: 10:00 AM - 8:00 PM kullum
  • Wuri: Cibiyar Al'adu ta Yola
  • Shiga: Kyauta

Manyan Abubuwan Bikin

  • Raye-raye na Gargajiya: Nunin raye-raye daga ƙungiyoyin gida
  • Nuni na Kiɗa: Kiɗa na gargajiya da na zamani kai tsaye
  • Nuna Zane: Nunin ayyukan masu zane na gida
  • Taron Abinci: Gwada abincin gargajiya
  • Bitocin Al'adu: Koyo sana'o'in gargajiya

Jadawali

  • Rana ta 1: Bikin bude taro, nunin raye-raye
  • Rana ta 2: Nuni na kiɗa, nuna zane
  • Rana ta 3: Taron abinci, bitoci, bikin rufewa
