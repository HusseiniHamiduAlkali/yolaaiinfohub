Game da Rediyo Gotel FM

Rediyo Gotel FM 91.1 wata tashar rediyo ta kasuwanci ce mai daraja wacce ke hidima ga Jihar Adamawa da ma fiye. An sanya mata suna daga dutsen Gotel mai girma wanda ke bayyana yanayin wannan yanki; tashar mu tana nuna sadaukarwa wajen isa kowanne lungu na jihar, daga cibiyoyin birni zuwa mafi nisa na ƙauyuka. Mu cibiyar watsa labarai ce mai zaman kanta da ke sadaukar da kai wajen samar da haɗin labarai masu gaskiya, nazari na abubuwan yau da kullum, shirye-shiryen ilimi, da nishaɗi mai inganci.

Babban manufarmu ita ce zama haske na bayani da dandali don muhawara. Muna gaskata ikon aikin jarida mai ɗawainiya wajen ƙarfafa 'yan ƙasa da riƙe shugabanni da alhakin ayyukansu. Abubuwan da muke watsawa an tsara su da kyau don nuna bambancin bukatu da sha'awar masu sauraro, suna rufe gibin tsakanin wayewar birni da gaskiyar karkara, tare da tallata ɗaƙilewa, zaman lafiya, da ci gaba a fadin Adamawa.

Shirye-shiryenmu

Jadawalin watsawar mu haɗin gwiya ne mai motsi tsakanin shirye-shiryen ilimi da jan hankali, an tsara shi don manyan masu sauraro.

  • Gotel a Safiya (6am - 9am): Shirin labarai mai sauri da al'amuran yau da kullum wanda ke ba da cikakken farawa ga yini tare da manyan labarai, sabbin labarai na wasanni, da yanayin ƙasa.
  • Tattaunawar Manufofi (Talata & Alhamis): Shirin tattaunawa mai hulɗa wanda ke kawo ƙwararru, jami'an gwamnati, da shugabannin al'umma don tattaunawa kan muhimman batutuwan manufofi da ke shafar ci gaban Jihar Adamawa.
  • Filin Karkara (Asabar): Shirin mayar da hankali ga al'umma wanda ke watsa a harsunan gida, yana magance kalubalen karkara, murnar al'adun gargajiya, da raba labaran nasara a noma da kananan kasuwanci.
  • 91.1 Haɗin Kiɗa (Kullum): Sashe ne da aka keɓe don kunna nau'ikan kiɗa daban-daban, ciki har da Afrobeats na Najeriya, shahararrun waƙoƙin yamma, da wakokin gargajiya, don biyan kowanne dandano.
  • Sabanin Lafiya & Walwala (Kowane Mako): A haɗin gwiwa da ƙungiyoyin lafiya na gida, wannan shirin yana ba da muhimman bayanai kan batutuwan lafiya na jama'a, rigakafin cutuka, da samun damar zuwa ayyukan lafiya.

Sadaukarwa ga Al'umma & Nauyi

Rediyo Gotel FM ta kasance muhimmin abokin haɗin gwiwa a ci gaban zamantakewa da tattalin arziki na Jihar Adamawa. Ba kawai mu kafar watsa labarai ba ce, har ma muna taka rawa a rayuwar al'umma. Ta hanyar shirinmu "Haske ga Al'umma", muna haskaka ayyukan NGOs na gida, kungiyoyin agaji, da shugabannin al'umma. Haka kuma muna bayar da lokaci kyauta don sanarwar hidimar jama'a, kuma a lokutan rikici, muna zama muhimmin tushe na bayanai ga ayyukan gaggawa da ƙoƙarin agaji. Sadaukarwarmu ga mutanen Adamawa tana zuciyar duk abin da muke yi.
