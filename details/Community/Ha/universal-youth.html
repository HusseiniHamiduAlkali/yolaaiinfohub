Universal Youth Development Initiative Yola

Karfafa Matasa don Ci Gaban Kowa

Manufa: Ƙirƙirar dandali don cikakken ci gaban matasa ta hanyar ilimi, koyo ƙwarewa, da horon shugabanci a dukkan al'ummomin Yola.

Game da UYDI

Universal Youth Development Initiative (UYDI) Yola ƙungiya ce ta matasa da ke buɗe kofa ga kowa, tana mayar da hankali wajen haɓaka cikakken ci gaban matasa a duk al'ummomi na Yola.

Core Programs

  • Tallafin Ilimi: Tallafin karatu da shawarwarin jagoranci
  • Horar da Ƙwarewa: Horo na sana'o'i da fasaha
  • Horar da Shugabanci: Shirye-shiryen shugabancin matasa
  • Shiga Al'umma: Ayyukan tasiri na zamantakewa
  • Jagorar Sana'a: Tallafin haɓaka ƙwarewar aiki

Shirye-shiryen Ci Gaba

  • Training Programs:
    • Horar da ƙwarewar dijital
    • Haɓakar sana'o'in ƙananan kasuwanci
    • Tarukan horar da ƙwarewar fasaha
  • Community Projects:
    • Kamfen ɗin karfafa matasa
    • Shirye-shiryen hidima ga al'umma
    • Shirye-shiryen wayar da kan muhalli

Fannonin Tasiri

  • Ilimi da haɓaka ƙwarewar matasa
  • Haɓaka shugabanci da ƙwarewar sana'a
  • Hidimar al'umma da haɗin kai
  • Dorewar muhalli
  • Harkokin kasuwanci na zamantakewa

Yadda Ake Shiga

  • Shiga Mu:
    • Zama memba
    • Damar sa kai
    • Shirye-shiryen jagoranci
  • Taimako:
    • Haɗa hannu da mu
    • Tallafa shirye-shirye
    • Ba da kayan aiki
