Game da Mu

Nas FM 99.1 Yola ita ce tashar rediyo ta farko da ta mayar da hankali kan matasa a Jihar Adamawa, mai sadaukarwa wajen samar da shirye-shirye masu motsawa waɗanda ke ilimantarwa, nishadantarwa, da wayar da kai. Sunanmu, Nas, yana nuna ruhin sabuwar ƙarni mai ƙwazo da hangen nesa. An ƙaddamar da tashar don cike gibi a shirye-shiryen matasa, inda muke mai da hankali kan kiɗa, labarai na yau da kullum, fasaha, da salon rayuwa, tare da riƙe alaƙa mai ƙarfi da al'ummar gida.

Manufarmu ita ce zama muryar gaskiya ta matasan Adamawa, ta samar da dandali ga ra'ayoyinsu, ƙwarewarsu, da burinsu. Mun himmatu wajen watsa shirye-shirye masu inganci waɗanda ke jan hankali kuma masu ɗaukar nauyi, suna haifar da ƙungiyar al'umma da canjin zamantakewa mai kyau a tsakanin masu sauraro.

Jadawalin Watsa Shirye-shiryenmu

Jadawalin watsawar mu hadadden haɗin kiɗa, shirye-shiryen tattaunawa masu hulɗa, da sassan ilimantarwa ne, an tsara su don biyan sha'awoyi daban-daban.

  • The Morning Rush (6am - 10am): Fara ranarka da kiɗa mai kuzari, sabbin labarai na gida, da barkwanci.
  • The Nas Top 10 Countdown (12pm - 1pm): Saurari mafi shahararrun wakoki na Afrobeats, Hip-Hop, da na duniya, kamar yadda masu sauraro suka zaɓa.
  • Youth Connect (3pm - 4pm): Shirin tattaunawa na matasa kan batutuwan aiki, kasuwanci, al'umma, da dangantaka.
  • Drive Time Mix (4pm - 7pm): Kiɗan da ya dace da hanyarka, tare da sabuntawar zirga-zirga.
  • Weekend Live (Asabar 10am - 2pm): Shiri na musamman don wasannin kiɗa kai tsaye, hira da mawakan gida, da labaran nishaɗi.

Hadin Gwiwa da Al'umma

Baya ga watsawa, Nas FM na taka rawar gani a ci gaban al'umma. Muna haɗin gwiwa da ƙungiyoyin gida don bitocin ilimi, gasar neman baiwa, da kamfen na kiwon lafiya. "Taron Muryar Matasa" shiri ne na wata-wata da ke tarawa matasa shugabanni da ƙwararru don tattauna hanyoyin warware matsalolin al'umma, ana watsa shi kai tsaye a tashar mu.
