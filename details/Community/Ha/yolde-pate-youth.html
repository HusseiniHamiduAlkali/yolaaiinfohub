Ƙungiyar Ci Gaban Matasa ta Yolde Pate

Haɓaka ci gaban al'umma ta hanyar karfafa matasa

Manufa: Tallafawa ci gaban matasa da haɓakar al'umma a Yolde Pate ta hanyar ilimi, koyon ƙwarewa, da shiga al'umma.

Game da YPYDA

Ƙungiyar Ci Gaban Matasa ta Yolde Pate (YPYDA) ƙungiya ce mai kuzari da ke sadaukar da kai wajen haɓaka ci gaban matasa da al'umma a yankin Yolde Pate na Yola.

Muhimman Shiri

  • Karfafa Matasa: Horar da ƙwarewa da gina ƙarfin aiki
  • Ci gaban Al'umma: Ayyukan ababen more rayuwa da muhalli
  • Tallafin Ilimi: Tallafin karatu da koyarwa
  • Ci gaban Wasanni: Shirye-shiryen wasannin matasa
  • Ayyukan Al'adu: Shirye-shiryen adana gadon al'adu

Shirye-shiryen Ci Gaba

  • Samar da Ƙwarewa:
    • Shirye-shiryen horon sana'o'i
    • Ci gaban ƙwarewar kasuwanci
    • Horar da ƙwarewar dijital
  • Ayyukan Al'umma:
    • Inganta ababen more rayuwa
    • Kare muhalli
    • Kamfen ɗin wayar da kai kan lafiya

Fannonin Tasiri

  • Samar da aikin yi da haɓaka kasuwanci ga matasa
  • Ci gaban ababen more rayuwa na al'umma
  • Ilimi da haɓaka ƙwarewa
  • Wasanni da nishadi
  • Adana gadon al'adu

Shiga Mu

  • Memba:
    • Zama memba
    • Damar sa kai
    • Shiga ayyukan mu
  • Taimako:
    • Ba da gudunmawa ga ayyukanmu
    • Haɗa hannu da mu
    • Tallafa shirye-shiryenmu
