Ranar Yaki da Zazzabin Cizon Sauro a Yola

Yaki da Cizon Sauro Tare

Manufar Taro: Wayar da kan jama'a game da rigakafi da magani na zazzabin cizon sauro tare da tallata ayyukan kiwon lafiya na al'umma.

Gajeren Bayani na Taro

A Yola ana gudanar da Ranar Yaki da Zazzabin Cizon Sauro tare da ayyuka daban-daban da ke ilmantar da al'umma game da rigakafi, magani, da matakan dakile zazzabin.

Ayyukan Taro

  • Tattaunawar Lafiya: Gabatarwar ƙwararru
  • Gwajin Kyauta: Tantancewar zazzabi
  • Rarraba Net: Rabon kyandirori na sauro
  • Fadakarwa a Gida: Kamfen ɗin gida zuwa gida
  • Sansanonin Lafiya: Ayyukan magani

Manyan Jigo

  • Dabarun rigakafi
  • Gano a farkon lokaci
  • Zabukan magani
  • Gudanar da muhalli
  • Shigar da jama'a

Mawadan Ilimi

  • Hanyoyin Rigakafi:
    • Amfani da katangar gado (bed nets)
    • Tsaftar muhalli
    • Hanyoyin kawar da sauro
  • Gane Alamun Cuta:
    • Alamun gargadi na farko
    • Yaushe a nemi taimako
    • Biya magani yadda ya kamata

Tasirin a Al'umma

  • Ƙaruwa a wayar da kan jama'a
  • Mafi kyawun hanyoyin rigakafi
  • Ingantaccen samun magani
  • Raguwar yawan cututtuka
  • Lafiyayyun al'ummomi
