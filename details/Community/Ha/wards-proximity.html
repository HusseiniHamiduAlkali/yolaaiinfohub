Sassan Yola & Nisan Kusa

Fahimtar tsarin zamantakewa da na gudanarwa na Yola

Mayar da Hankali: Bayanin sassan Yola North da Yola South, nisan kusa, da muhimman abubuwa.

Ƙaramar Hukumar Yola North (Jimeta)

  • Alkalawa
  • Ajiya
  • Doubeli
  • Gwadabawa
  • Jambutu (Yola International Airport)
  • Karewa
  • Limawa
  • Luggere
  • Nassarawo
  • Rumde
  • Yelwa

Muhimman Abubuwa: Matsayi a tsakiyar gari, Kasuwar Jimeta, kusanci da filin jirgin sama, haɗakar zamantakewa/ kasuwanci, hanyar mota mai inganci.

Ƙaramar Hukumar Yola South (Tsofaffin Garin Yola da Ƙewaye)

  • Adarawo
  • Bako
  • Bole/Yolde Pate
  • Namtari
  • Ngurore
  • Makama 'A'
  • Makama 'B'
  • Mbamba
  • Mbamoi
  • Toungo
  • Yolde Kohi

Muhimman Abubuwa: Cibiyar gargajiya, kusanci da Kogin Benue, haɗin yankin karkara-da-gari, bunƙasa ababen more rayuwa.

Nisan Kusa da Dangantaka

  • "Biranen biyu": Yola (Tsofaffin Garin) & Jimeta (Cibiyar Kasuwanci)
  • Nisa: 5-9 km tsakanin Jimeta da Tsofaffin Garin Yola
  • Hanyoyin haɗi: Hanyoyi, sufuri na jama'a, sabuwar hanyar kaucewa
  • Sufuri: Keke NAPEP, taksi, bas-bas
