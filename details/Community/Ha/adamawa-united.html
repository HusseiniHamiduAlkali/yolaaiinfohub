Kungiyar Kwallon Kafa ta Adamawa United

Alfaharin Jihar Adamawa

Manufa: Zama jagorar kungiyar kwallon kafa a Najeriya, tare da bunkasa baiwar matasa da inganta wasanni a Jihar Adamawa.

Game da Kungiyar

Kungiyar Kwallon Kafa ta Adamawa United kungiya ce ta kwararru da ke Yola, wadda ke wakiltar Jihar Adamawa a gasar kwallon kafa ta Najeriya. Kungiyar tana zama dandamali don bunkasa baiwar matasa da inganta wasanni a yankin.

Ayyukan Kungiya

  • Kwallon Kafa na Kwararru: Halartar gasar kasa
  • Bunkasa Matasan: Shirye-shiryen horo da makaranta
  • Hadin Gwiwar Al'umma: Shirye-shiryen wasanni na gida
  • Nemo Baiwa: Gano 'yan wasa masu hazaka
  • Ilimin Wasanni: Koyar da kwallon kafa da shirye-shirye

Shirye-shiryen Ci Gaba

  • Makarantar Matasan:
    • Horarwa ta fasaha
    • Gyaran jiki
    • Ci gaban sana'a
  • Shirye-shiryen Al'umma:
    • Shirye-shiryen kwallon kafa na makaranta
    • Gasar al'umma
    • Shirye-shiryen ilimin wasanni

Kayayyakin Aiki

  • Filin horo
  • Filin wasa na gida
  • Kayan horo
  • Kayayyakin lafiya
  • Kayayyakin makarantar matasa

Shiga Ciki

  • Goya wa Kungiya Baya:
    • Halartar wasanni
    • Sayen kayan tallafi
    • Shiga kungiyoyin masoya
  • Shirye-shiryen Matasan:
    • Gwajin makaranta
    • Sansanonin horo
    • Gasar matasa
