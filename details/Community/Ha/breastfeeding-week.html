Makon Shan Nonon Uwa a Duniya a Yola

Tallata Lafiyar Uwa da Yaro

Manufar Taro: Wayar da kan muhimmancin shan nonon uwa da tallafa lafiyar uwa a al'ummomin Yola.

Bayani game da Taro

Makon Shan Nonon Uwa na Duniya kamfen ne na duniya da ake murnar shekara-shekara a Yola don nuna muhimmancin shan nonon uwa wajen lafiyar yaro da ci gabansa. Taron yana haɗa kwararrun ma'aikatan lafiya, iyaye mata, da shugabannin al'umma.

Ayyukan Taro

  • Tattaunawar Lafiya: Gabatarwar ƙwararru kan shan nono
  • Ƙarfin Al'umma: Wayar da kan daga gida zuwa gida
  • Rukunin Tallafi: Hanyoyin tallafi daga uwa zuwa uwa
  • Shawarwari Kyauta: Tare da ƙwararrun ma'aikatan lafiya

Manyan Jigo

  • Amfanin shan nono na musamman
  • Hanyoyin shayarwa na daidai
  • Abincin uwa
  • Ƙalubale na gama gari da hanyoyin magance su
  • Tsarin tallafi na al'umma

Ƙungiyoyin da Suke Shiga

  • Hukumar Kula da Lafiyar Firamare ta Jihar Adamawa
  • Cibiyoyin lafiya na gida
  • Rukunonin tallafin mata
  • Ƙungiyoyin lafiya na ƙasa da ƙasa

Tasirin Taro

  • Ƙaruwa a wayar da kan amfanin shan nono
  • Ingantattun ayyukan lafiyar uwa
  • Ƙarfafa hanyoyin tallafi a al'umma
  • Mafi kyawun sakamakon lafiya ga yara
