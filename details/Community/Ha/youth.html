Ƙungiyar Matasa

Shirye-shirye don Matasa

Game da Mu

Ƙungiyar Matasa tana sadaukar da kai wajen karfafa matasa a Yola ta hanyar shirye-shiryen ilimi, nishadi, da haɓaka shugabanci. Muna samar da dandali ga matasa don haɓaka ƙwarewarsu, bin abubuwan da suke sha'awa, da bada gudunmawa ga ci gaban al'umma.

Shirye-shiryenmu

  • Horar da Shugabanci: Horo da jagoranci
  • Ayyukan Wasanni: Wasannin ƙungiya da gasa
  • Tallafin Ilimi: Koyarwa da ƙungiyoyin karatu
  • Fasahar Kirkire-kirkire: Kiɗa, rawa, da wasan kwaikwayo
  • Hidimar Al'umma: Damar sa kai

Yadda Ake Shiga

  • Shekaru: 15-25 shekaru
  • Rijista: A bude duk shekara
  • Kudin Memba: Kuɗin shekara-shekara mai araha
  • Sharuɗɗa: Izinin iyaye ga ƙasa da shekaru 18
