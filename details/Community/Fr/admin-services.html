Community Administrative Services

Essential Services for Residents of Yola

Section Focus: Information on local government, voter registration, NIN, and passport services in Yola.

Local Council Services

  • Birth Registration: For new births within the community. Required: Parents' IDs, hospital birth certificate (if any).
  • Marriage Registration: For customary and traditional marriages. Required: Proof of identity, traditional marriage rite confirmation.
  • Indigene/Resident Certificates: For proof of residency or indigene status. Required: Community attestation, proof of address.
  • Community Development Permits: For minor community building or event permits.
  • Collection of Local Levies and Taxes

Voter Registration

  • Handled by: INEC (Independent National Electoral Commission) officials
  • Location: Local Council Office or designated polling units during registration periods
  • Required Documents: Proof of identity (e.g., National ID Card, driver's license)

National Identification Number (NIN) Enrollment

  • Services: NIN Enrollment, Retrieval, Modification/Update/Correction, Renewal
  • Contact: 07002255646 (NIMC General Enquiry)

Passport Office

  • Location: Kofare, Numan Road, Jimeta Yola, Adamawa State
  • Application: Online via the Nigeria Immigration Service (NIS) website
  • Services: Application for standard/official passports, biometric data capture, collection
  • Processing Time: 6 weeks to 2 months (express services available)
