Women's Group

Empowerment and Support

About Our Group

The Women's Group is a supportive community dedicated to empowering women in Yola through education, skill development, and mutual support. We provide a safe space for women to learn, grow, and succeed together.

Our Activities

  • Skills Training: Vocational and business skills
  • Support Groups: Regular meetings and discussions
  • Health Programs: Wellness workshops
  • Financial Literacy: Savings and investment education
  • Mentorship: Career guidance and support

Join Us

  • Membership: Open to all women
  • Meetings: Weekly gatherings
  • Location: Community Center
  • Contact: Group coordinator available daily
