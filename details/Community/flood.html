Devastating Flood in Yola

August 2025

Impact Overview

The recent flooding in Yola has caused significant damage to residential areas and infrastructure, affecting thousands of residents. The flood, which followed heavy rainfall, has led to the displacement of many families and disruption of daily activities.

Affected Areas

  • Residential Areas: Multiple neighborhoods submerged
  • Infrastructure: Roads and bridges damaged
  • Public Facilities: Schools and markets affected
  • Agricultural Land: Farmlands flooded

Emergency Response

  • Evacuation Centers: Set up in safe locations
  • Relief Materials: Distribution ongoing
  • Medical Support: Emergency healthcare services available
  • Emergency Contacts: 24/7 helpline established

How to Help

  • Donate relief materials to evacuation centers
  • Volunteer for distribution efforts
  • Support affected families
  • Report emergencies to proper authorities
