Yola Renewal Foundation

Working to Revitalize and Develop the City of Yola

About the Foundation

The Yola Renewal Foundation is a non-profit organization committed to the sustainable development and revitalization of Yola. The foundation focuses on a variety of community-based projects, including urban renewal, environmental conservation, and youth empowerment. Through partnerships with local government and private sector organizations, the foundation works to implement projects that improve infrastructure, create economic opportunities, and enhance the overall quality of life for residents. The Yola Renewal Foundation is driven by a vision of a modern, clean, and prosperous Yola.

Key Projects

  • Urban Greening: Planting trees and developing public parks and gardens.
  • Waste Management: Promoting recycling and proper waste disposal.
  • Skills Acquisition: Training for young people in vocational skills and entrepreneurship.
