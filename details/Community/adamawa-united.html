Adamawa United Football Club Association

Pride of Adamawa State

Vision: To be a leading football club in Nigeria, developing local talent and promoting sporting excellence in Adamawa State.

About The Club

Adamawa United Football Club is a professional football club based in Yola, representing Adamawa State in Nigerian football competitions. The club serves as a platform for developing local football talent and promoting sports in the region.

Club Activities

  • Professional Football: Participation in national leagues
  • Youth Development: Academy and training programs
  • Community Engagement: Local sports initiatives
  • Talent Scouting: Identifying promising players
  • Sports Education: Football clinics and workshops
