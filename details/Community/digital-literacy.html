Digital Literacy Workshop

Bridging the Digital Divide

Workshop Goal: Empowering community members with essential digital skills for the modern world.

Workshop Overview

The Digital Literacy Workshop is a comprehensive program designed to help community members develop essential computer and internet skills needed in today's digital world.

Workshop Topics

  • Basic Computer Skills: Hardware, software, operating systems
  • Internet Basics: Web browsing, email, online safety
  • Digital Communication: Social media, video calls
  • Online Services: Banking, shopping, government services

Workshop Structure

  • Duration: 4 weeks
  • Sessions: 2 hours, twice a week
  • Class Size: Maximum 20 participants
  • Learning Method: Hands-on practice with computers

Who Should Attend

  • Beginners with little to no computer experience
  • Adults seeking to improve digital skills
  • Senior citizens wanting to stay connected
  • Job seekers needing computer skills

What We Provide

  • Computer workstations for practice
  • Comprehensive learning materials
  • Expert instructors
  • Certificate of completion
  • Post-workshop support
