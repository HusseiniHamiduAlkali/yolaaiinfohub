About Radio Gotel FM

Radio Gotel FM 91.1 is a premier commercial radio station serving Adamawa State and beyond. Named after the majestic Gotel Mountains that define the region's landscape, our station symbolizes a commitment to reaching every corner of the state, from the urban centers to the most remote rural communities. We are a private broadcast institution dedicated to providing a balanced mix of credible news, insightful current affairs, educational programming, and quality entertainment.

Our core mission is to serve as a beacon of information and a platform for dialogue. We believe in the power of responsible journalism to empower citizens and hold leaders accountable. Our content is carefully curated to reflect the diverse needs and interests of our audience, bridging the gap between urban sophistication and rural realities, while promoting unity, peace, and development across Adamawa.

Our Programming

Our broadcast schedule is a dynamic blend of informative and engaging shows, designed for a broad audience.

  • Gotel in the Morning (6am - 9am): A fast-paced news and current affairs show providing a comprehensive start to the day with news headlines, sports updates, and weather forecasts.
  • The Policy Dialogue (Tuesdays & Thursdays): An interactive talk show featuring experts, government officials, and community leaders discussing critical policy issues affecting Adamawa State's development.
  • Village Square (Saturdays): A community-focused program that airs in local languages, addressing rural challenges, celebrating traditional cultures, and sharing success stories in agriculture and small-scale enterprise.
  • 91.1 Music Mix (Daily): A segment dedicated to playing a wide range of music genres, including Nigerian Afrobeats, Western hits, and traditional local tunes, catering to every taste.
  • Health & Wellness Update (Weekly): In partnership with local health organizations, this show provides crucial information on public health issues, disease prevention, and access to medical services.

Commitment to Community & Responsibility

Radio Gotel FM stands as a key partner in the socio-economic development of Adamawa State. We are not just a broadcast medium but an active participant in community life. Through our "Community Spotlight" initiative, we highlight the work of local NGOs, charities, and community leaders. We also provide pro-bono airtime for public service announcements, and in times of crisis, we serve as a vital source of information for emergency services and relief efforts. Our commitment to the people of Adamawa is at the heart of everything we do.
