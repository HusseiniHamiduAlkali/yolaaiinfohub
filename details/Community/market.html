Community Market

Weekly Local Produce and Crafts

Market Information

  • When: Every Saturday
  • Time: 7:00 AM - 2:00 PM
  • Location: Yola Community Square
  • Entry: Free for visitors

What to Find

  • Fresh Produce: Local fruits and vegetables
  • Artisan Crafts: Handmade items and artwork
  • Local Food: Traditional snacks and meals
  • Organic Products: Natural and organic items
  • Community Stalls: Local business displays

For Vendors

  • Stall Registration: Available weekly
  • Setup Time: 6:00 AM - 7:00 AM
  • Requirements: Valid vendor permit
  • Cost: Affordable daily rates
