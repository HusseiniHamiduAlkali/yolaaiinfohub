AUN Class of 2026 Community Service

Making a Difference in Local Education

Initiative Goal: To enhance primary education through student-led community service and engagement.

Program Overview

The AUN Class of 2026 has embarked on an inaugural community service program targeting local primary schools in Yola, demonstrating their commitment to community development and educational support.

Service Activities

  • Educational Support: Basic subject tutoring
  • Material Distribution: School supplies and learning materials
  • Infrastructure: Basic facility improvements
  • Mentorship: Student guidance and motivation

Impact Areas

  • Literacy improvement
  • STEM education support
  • School environment enhancement
  • Student motivation and engagement
  • Community-university relations

Program Benefits

  • For Primary Schools:
    • Additional educational support
    • Access to university resources
    • Improved learning environment
  • For AUN Students:
    • Real-world community engagement
    • Leadership development
    • Social responsibility experience
