UNDP Training in Yola

Building Capacity for Sustainable Development

About the Training

The United Nations Development Programme (UNDP) organizes various training programs and workshops in Yola to support sustainable development goals in Adamawa State. These trainings are tailored to address critical local challenges, such as poverty reduction, climate change adaptation, and humanitarian assistance. By partnering with government agencies, NGOs, and community groups, the UNDP provides participants with the skills and resources needed to implement impactful projects and build a more resilient and prosperous community.

Training Areas

  • Peace & Security: Workshops on conflict resolution and community dialogue.
  • Economic Empowerment: Training on entrepreneurship and small business development.
  • Environmental Sustainability: Education on sustainable land management and renewable energy.
