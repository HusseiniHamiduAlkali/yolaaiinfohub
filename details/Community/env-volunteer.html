Creating a Greener Yola Together

About Our Environmental Initiatives

Join our community-driven environmental projects aimed at preserving and enhancing Yola's natural environment. Our initiatives focus on sustainable practices, waste management, and creating green spaces that benefit everyone in our community.

Current Projects

  • Community Clean-up Drive: Regular neighborhood clean-up events to maintain cleanliness and hygiene.
  • Tree Planting Initiative: Monthly tree planting activities to increase green cover in urban areas.
  • Waste Recycling Program: Organizing and managing community recycling centers.
  • Urban Gardens: Creating and maintaining community gardens in available spaces.
  • Environmental Education: Workshops on sustainable living and environmental conservation.

How to Get Involved

  • Sign up for regular clean-up events
  • Participate in tree planting drives
  • Join our recycling awareness team
  • Contribute to community garden maintenance
  • Help organize environmental workshops
