Youth And Women Empowerment Foundation (YAWEF)

Empowering Youth and Women for a Better Tomorrow

Mission: To empower youth and women through education, skills development, and economic opportunities for sustainable development.

Organization Overview

YAWEF is a non-profit organization dedicated to improving the lives of youth and women in Yola through various empowerment programs and initiatives.

Core Programs

  • Skills Training: Vocational and technical skills
  • Education Support: Scholarships and mentoring
  • Business Development: Entrepreneurship training
  • Health Programs: Awareness and support
  • Leadership Development: Capacity building

Key Initiatives

  • Women's Programs:
    • Business skills training
    • Microfinance support
    • Health education
  • Youth Programs:
    • Digital skills training
    • Career guidance
    • Leadership development
