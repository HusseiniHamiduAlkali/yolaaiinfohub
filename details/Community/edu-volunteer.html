Education Support Volunteer Program

Making a difference in local education

About the Program

The Education Support Volunteer Program connects passionate volunteers with local schools to enhance the learning experience of students in Yola. Our volunteers play a crucial role in supporting teachers, mentoring students, and creating an enriching educational environment that promotes academic success and personal growth.

Volunteer Opportunities

  • Classroom Assistant: Support teachers in daily classroom activities and help students with their assignments.
  • After-School Tutor: Provide one-on-one or small group tutoring in various subjects.
  • Library Helper: Assist in organizing and maintaining school libraries, help students find resources.
  • Reading Buddy: Partner with young students to improve their reading skills and foster a love for books.
  • STEM Workshop Leader: Lead hands-on science and math activities to make learning fun and engaging.

Requirements

  • Minimum commitment of 4 hours per week
  • Basic understanding of core subjects
  • Patience and good communication skills
  • Background check clearance
  • Attendance at orientation session
