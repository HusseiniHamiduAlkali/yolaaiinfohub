About Fombina FM

Fombina FM 101.5 Yola is the public-service radio station for the people of Adamawa State, named in honor of the historic Fombina Kingdom. Our station is committed to delivering accurate, timely, and impartial news, as well as broadcasting critical public service announcements and educational programming. We serve as a vital link between the government, traditional institutions, and the citizens, ensuring a well-informed and engaged populace.

Our mission is to be the most trusted voice in the region, promoting peace, unity, and development through responsible journalism and transparent communication. We are a platform for all voices, fostering dialogue on issues that matter to the community.

Core Mandate

Our core mandate is to serve the public interest. During emergencies or natural disasters, Fombina FM is the official channel for disaster relief information and public safety alerts. We are also dedicated to civic education, broadcasting programs that inform citizens about their rights, responsibilities, and the democratic process. Our deep roots in the Fombina Kingdom give us a unique perspective and a responsibility to uphold the highest standards of integrity and service to the people of Adamawa.
