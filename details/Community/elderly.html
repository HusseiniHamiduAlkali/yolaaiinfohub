Support for Senior Citizens

About Our Network

The Elderly Care Network is dedicated to supporting senior citizens in Yola, ensuring they remain active, healthy, and connected to the community. We provide various services and activities designed to enhance the quality of life for our elderly community members.

Our Services

  • Health Support: Regular health check-ups and counseling
  • Social Activities: Group meetings and outings
  • Home Visits: Regular companionship visits
  • Transportation: Assistance with appointments
  • Recreational Programs: Exercise and hobby groups

Get Involved

  • Membership: Open to seniors 60+
  • Support: Family member registration
  • Volunteer: Help with various activities
  • Donate: Support our programs
