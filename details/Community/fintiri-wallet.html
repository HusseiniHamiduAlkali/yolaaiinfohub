Fintiri Wallet Program

Financial Support for the Less Privileged

Program Goal: To provide financial assistance to underprivileged residents of Adamawa State through conditional cash transfers.

Program Overview

The Fintiri Wallet Program is a social welfare initiative by the Adamawa State Government aimed at providing direct financial support to vulnerable individuals and families in the state.

Program Benefits

  • Cash Support: Monthly financial assistance
  • Financial Inclusion: Bank account opening
  • Economic Empowerment: Small business support
  • Social Protection: Emergency assistance

Eligibility Criteria

  • Residence in Adamawa State
  • Proof of financial need
  • Valid identification
  • Bank account or willingness to open one

How to Apply

  • Registration: At designated centers
  • Documentation: Required paperwork
  • Verification: Home visits
  • Approval: Selection process

Program Features

  • Transparent Selection: Clear criteria and process
  • Digital Payments: Secure fund transfers
  • Regular Monitoring: Impact assessment
  • Support Services: Financial literacy training
