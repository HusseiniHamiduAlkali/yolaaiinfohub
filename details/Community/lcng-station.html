First LCNG Fuelling Station in Northeast

A Step Towards Sustainable Energy

Project Milestone

The Nigerian government has commissioned the first LCNG (Liquefied-Compressed Natural Gas) refuelling and conversion centre in Yola, marking a significant development in the region's energy infrastructure.

Facility Features

  • Refuelling Capacity: State-of-the-art LCNG dispensing
  • Conversion Center: Vehicle modification services
  • Safety Systems: Advanced safety protocols
  • Operating Hours: Extended service hours

Benefits to Community

  • Cheaper fuel alternative for vehicles
  • Reduced environmental impact
  • Job creation opportunities
  • Technology transfer to the region

Future Plans

  • Expansion: Additional stations planned
  • Training: Local workforce development
  • Awareness: Public education programs
  • Support: Ongoing technical assistance
