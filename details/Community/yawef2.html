YAWEF (Yola Area Women Empowerment Foundation)

Empowering Women and Girls for a Brighter Future

About the Foundation

YAWEF is a non-governmental organization dedicated to the social and economic empowerment of women and girls in the Yola area. The foundation works to address the challenges faced by women, including limited access to education, economic opportunities, and healthcare. Through a variety of programs, YAWEF provides women with the tools and support they need to become self-reliant, confident, and active participants in their communities. The foundation's work is crucial for achieving gender equality and promoting sustainable development in the region.

Our Programs

  • Skills Acquisition: Training in vocations like tailoring, catering, and soap making.
  • Micro-Credit Schemes: Providing small loans to help women start their own businesses.
  • Health & Education: Awareness campaigns on women's health and advocacy for girls' education.
