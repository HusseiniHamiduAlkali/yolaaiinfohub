Mahmudu Ribadu Square

Yola's Premier Public Gathering and Event Center

About the Square

Mahmudu Ribadu Square is a historical and cultural landmark in Yola, serving as the main public gathering space for large-scale events, rallies, and celebrations. The square is a symbol of community and unity, hosting everything from national day celebrations and political gatherings to cultural festivals and public awareness campaigns. Its central location and vast open space make it the ideal venue for events that require a large capacity and public accessibility.

Key Features

  • Large Capacity: Ideal for large public and government events.
  • Central Location: Easily accessible from all parts of the city.
  • Cultural Significance: A historic venue for important civic events.
