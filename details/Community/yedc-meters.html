YEDC Meter Theft Warning

Caution Against Unauthorized Meter Purchases

Important Notice: Only purchase meters from authorized YEDC channels to avoid legal issues and service disruption.

Situation Overview

The Yola Electricity Distribution Company (YEDC) has issued a stern warning to customers against purchasing meters from unauthorized sources, following an increase in meter theft cases.

Official Guidelines

  • Purchase Process: Only through YEDC offices
  • Documentation: Required paperwork
  • Installation: By authorized technicians
  • Verification: Official meter validation

Warning Signs

  • Unusually low prices
  • No official documentation
  • Unauthorized installers
  • Suspicious meter conditions

How to Report

  • YEDC Hotline: Direct reporting
  • Customer Service: Official channels
  • Online Portal: Digital reporting
  • Local Offices: In-person reports
