Female Child Education Program

Empowering Girls Through Education

Our Mission: To promote and support education for girls in Yola and surrounding communities.

Program Overview

The Female Child Education Awareness Program is a comprehensive initiative designed to increase access to education for girls and promote gender equality in education across Yola.

Key Initiatives

  • Scholarships: Financial support for education
  • Mentorship: Guidance from successful women
  • Community Outreach: Awareness campaigns
  • Skills Development: Additional training programs

Program Benefits

  • Free educational materials
  • Career guidance sessions
  • Parent support groups
  • Academic counseling

How to Participate

  • Registration: Open enrollment periods
  • Requirements: Basic eligibility criteria
  • Support: Available resources
  • Contact: Program coordinators
