About NTA Yola

NTA Yola is the local station of the Nigerian Television Authority (NTA), the largest television network in Africa. As a public service broadcaster, NTA Yola is mandated to provide balanced and objective news, educational content, and high-quality entertainment to the people of Adamawa. While part of a national network, our station is deeply rooted in the local community, ensuring that the unique stories, challenges, and successes of the state are given a national platform.

Our mission is to foster national unity and promote cultural harmony by providing programs that are both locally relevant and nationally integrated. We are the trusted source for government announcements, national news, and official communication, playing a critical role in informing and connecting citizens across Nigeria.

Our Programs

NTA Yola's programming is a mix of nationally syndicated shows and locally produced content, reflecting our dual mandate.

  • NTA News @ 9 (Daily): The network's flagship national news bulletin, broadcast live from our Abuja headquarters, with local inserts from our Yola studios.
  • Weekend File (Saturdays): A documentary-style program produced locally, highlighting significant developments, projects, and human-interest stories within Adamawa State.
  • Good Morning Nigeria (Daily): The popular breakfast show that features live discussions on current events, policy matters, and lifestyle segments, with contributions from our Yola studio.
  • Our People, Our Pride (Weekly): A cultural program that focuses on showcasing the traditional ceremonies, historical landmarks, and cultural festivals of the diverse ethnic groups in Adamawa.
  • Democracy in Motion (Weekly): A public affairs show that engages citizens in discussions about governance, electoral processes, and the performance of political leaders.
