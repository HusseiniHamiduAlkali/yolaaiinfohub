Bako Youth Organisation Yola

Uniting Youth for Community Development

Mission: To unite and empower youth in Bako community for positive social change and sustainable development.

Organization Overview

Bako Youth Organisation is a community-based youth organization working to empower young people and promote community development in the Bako area of Yola.

Programs and Activities

  • Youth Development: Leadership training
  • Community Service: Local projects
  • Sports Programs: Youth engagement
  • Educational Support: Academic assistance
  • Cultural Activities: Heritage preservation
