Empowering Youth for Universal Progress

Mission: To create a platform for comprehensive youth development through education, skills acquisition, and leadership training across all communities in Yola.

About UYDI

Universal Youth Development Initiative (UYDI) Yola is an inclusive youth organization dedicated to fostering comprehensive development among young people across all communities in Yola.

Core Programs

  • Education Support: Scholarships and mentoring
  • Skills Development: Vocational and technical training
  • Leadership Development: Youth leadership programs
  • Community Engagement: Social impact projects
  • Career Guidance: Professional development support
