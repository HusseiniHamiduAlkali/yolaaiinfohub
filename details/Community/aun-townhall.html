AUN Town Hall Meeting

Fostering Dialogue and Engagement with the Community

About the Meeting

The American University of Nigeria (AUN) regularly hosts town hall meetings to foster an open and transparent dialogue between the university leadership, faculty, students, and the local community. These meetings serve as a platform for sharing updates on AUN's community development initiatives, discussing local issues, and gathering feedback from residents. The AUN Town Hall Meeting is a testament to the university's commitment to being a responsible and engaged member of the Yola community, working together for collective progress.

Meeting Agenda

  • Updates: Sharing progress on AUN's community service projects.
  • Open Forum: An opportunity for residents to ask questions and share concerns.
  • Collaboration: Discussing potential partnerships for future projects.
