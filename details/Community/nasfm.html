About Us

Nas FM 99.1 Yola is the leading youth-oriented radio station in Adamawa State, dedicated to providing dynamic content that informs, educates, and entertains. Our name, Nas, is a reflection of the vibrant, forward-thinking spirit of the new generation. Launched to fill a void in youth-specific programming, we focus on music, current affairs, technology, and lifestyle, all while maintaining a strong connection to the local community.

Our mission is to be the authentic voice of Adamawa's young people, offering a platform for their ideas, talents, and aspirations. We are committed to high-quality broadcasting that is both engaging and responsible, fostering a sense of community and positive social change among our listeners.

Our Programming

Our broadcast schedule is a lively mix of music, interactive talk shows, and informative segments, designed to cater to diverse interests.

  • The Morning Rush (6am - 10am): Start your day with high-energy music, local news updates, and witty banter.
  • The Nas Top 10 Countdown (12pm - 1pm): Tune in to hear the hottest Afrobeats, Hip-Hop, and international hits, as voted by our listeners.
  • Youth Connect (3pm - 4pm): A vibrant talk show addressing topics relevant to young people, from career development and entrepreneurship to social issues and relationships.
  • Drive Time Mix (4pm - 7pm): The perfect soundtrack for your commute, featuring a mix of popular genres and traffic updates.
  • Weekend Live (Saturdays 10am - 2pm): A dedicated show for live music performances, interviews with local artists, and entertainment news.

Community Engagement

Beyond our broadcast, Nas FM is deeply involved in community development. We partner with local organizations for educational workshops, talent hunts, and public health campaigns. Our "Youth Voice Forum" is a monthly event that brings together young leaders and professionals to discuss solutions to community challenges, broadcast live on our station.
