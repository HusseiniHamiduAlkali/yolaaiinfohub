Yola Renewal Foundation

Building a Better Future for Yola

Mission: To empower young people through education and skill development while promoting community renewal and sustainable development.

Organization Overview

The Yola Renewal Foundation is a community-based organization dedicated to improving the lives of Yola residents through various development initiatives and youth empowerment programs.

Key Programs

  • Youth Empowerment: Skills training and development
  • Community Development: Infrastructure projects
  • Education Support: Scholarships and mentoring
  • Environmental Projects: Green initiatives
  • Economic Development: Small business support

Current Initiatives

  • Skills Development:
    • Digital literacy programs
    • Vocational training
    • Entrepreneurship workshops
  • Community Projects:
    • Infrastructure improvement
    • Environmental conservation
    • Public space renovation

Impact Areas

  • Social welfare and support
