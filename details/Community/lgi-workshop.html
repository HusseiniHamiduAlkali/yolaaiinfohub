LGI Workshop in Yola

Capacity Building for Local Government Officials

About the Workshop

The Local Governance and Innovation (LGI) Workshop in Yola is a specialized training program designed for local government officials. The workshop aims to enhance the skills and knowledge of participants in key areas of governance, including public administration, financial management, project implementation, and citizen engagement. By providing officials with modern tools and best practices, the workshop helps to improve the efficiency, transparency, and effectiveness of local government service delivery in the state.

Workshop Focus Areas

  • Good Governance: Principles of accountability and transparency.
  • Public Finance: Training on budget preparation and expenditure management.
  • Service Delivery: Strategies for improving public services and infrastructure.
