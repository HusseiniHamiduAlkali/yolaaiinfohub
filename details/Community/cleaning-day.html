Community Cleaning Day

A Collective Effort for a Healthier Environment

About the Event

Community Cleaning Day is a regular civic activity organized to promote sanitation and public health in neighborhoods across Yola. Residents, local leaders, and volunteers come together to clean streets, clear drainage systems, and dispose of waste. This collective effort not only improves the aesthetics of the community but also prevents the spread of diseases, especially during the rainy season. It is a powerful display of community solidarity and a commitment to a cleaner and healthier living environment.

What We Do

  • Drainage Cleaning: Unclogging gutters to prevent flooding.
  • Street Sweeping: Clearing debris and waste from public roads and walkways.
  • Waste Disposal: Proper collection and management of refuse.
