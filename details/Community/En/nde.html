National Directorate Of Employment (NDE), Yola Office

Government Agency for Employment and Skills Development

About NDE Yola

The National Directorate of Employment (NDE) Yola Office is a government agency tasked with employment generation, skills acquisition, and job creation programs in Adamawa State. Operating under the Federal Ministry of Labour and Employment, NDE plays a crucial role in reducing unemployment, combating underemployment, and equipping citizens with marketable skills for the job market.

Core Mission and Objectives

  • Generate employment opportunities for unemployed citizens
  • Provide skills acquisition and vocational training
  • Support youth entrepreneurship and business development
  • Implement poverty alleviation programs
  • Facilitate school-to-work transition for graduates
  • Promote sustainable job creation initiatives

Training Programs Offered

  • Vocational skills training in various trades
  • Entrepreneurship development programs
  • Information and Communication Technology (ICT) training
  • Agricultural skills and farm management courses
  • Artisan and craft training programs
  • Business management and financial literacy courses

Employment Services

  • Job placement and career counseling
  • Job matching services connecting employers and job seekers
  • Resume and interview preparation workshops
  • Labour market information and trends analysis
  • Employment fair organization and coordination
  • Apprenticeship and internship programs

Entrepreneurship Support

  • Business plan development assistance
  • Micro-credit and loan facilitation
  • Startup mentorship programs
  • Business registration guidance
  • Market access and networking opportunities
  • Business monitoring and growth support

Special Programs

  • Youth employment programs (N-Power, etc.)
  • Graduate internship schemes
  • Community development programs
  • Disability employment initiatives
  • Women empowerment and economic programs
  • Post-disaster employment rehabilitation

Eligibility and Application

  • Open to all Nigerian citizens
  • Various programs for different age groups and qualifications
  • Free or subsidized training courses
  • Registration and enrollment processes available
  • Support services for disadvantaged groups
  • Online and offline application options

Contact Information

Location: Yola, Adamawa State

The NDE Yola Office is dedicated to serving the employment needs of residents in Adamawa State. For inquiries about training programs, job placement services, or entrepreneurship support, visit the office during working hours or contact through official channels.
