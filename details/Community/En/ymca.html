About YMCA Yola Chapter

The YMCA (Young Men's Christian Association) Yola Chapter is a youth development organization committed to building strong communities and developing the full potential of young people. Through educational, recreational, and social programs, YMCA creates inclusive spaces where young people can learn, grow, and make positive contributions to society.

Core Mission and Values

  • Develop young people physically, mentally, and spiritually
  • Foster leadership and character development
  • Promote health and wellness among youth
  • Build inclusive and supportive communities
  • Encourage active citizenship and community service
  • Practice equity and serve all segments of society
