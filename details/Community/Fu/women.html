Maagal Macce

Tannugol e Caggal

Baɗal Maagal Amin

Maagal Macce ko yimande saajel wonde tannugol macce Yola nder bartagol, hayɓugo koofal, e caggal tabbugol. Amin waɗi lekkal girtirde macce wonde bartagol, fuddugol, e tannugol tabbugol nder waakandu.

Rewbaani Amin

  • Bartagol Koofal: Jattugol e koofal jom
  • Maagal Caggal: Tabbugol e jattugol sattinɗe
  • Projaam Faafaa: Kaɓbugal faafaa
  • Bartagol Koofal: Bartagol annde e jattugol koofal
  • Cayugol: Cayugol carrere e caggal

Wallu Amin

  • Juumbugol: Gonnaaji macce fof
  • Tabbugol: Tabbugol sattin\u0257e
  • Lekkal: Merkudo Yimande
  • Habooji: Koordinator maagal gonnaaji
