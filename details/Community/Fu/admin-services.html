Sarkinjaaje Nette Rewbe

Seɲto mawɗo Yola mo'o ko innde

Njilɗo Sariina: Janngo local government, esiisaade voter, NIN, e passport seɲto Yola.

Seara Council Services

  • Jagnaade Njikka: Njikka debbo ko innde rewbe. Koyɗe: IDs maɓɓe, certificat njikka hospital.
  • Jagnaade Njulaare: Njulaare aada e traditional. Koyɗe: proof identity, confirmation njulaare aada.
  • Certificate Indigene/Resident: Njikka tampere rewbe e status indigene. Koyɗe: attestation rewbe, proof address.
  • Permis Nattude Rewbe: Njikka naatude lowaama build rewbe e permit event.
  • Njikkagol Jelgo e Taakese Lokaal

Jagnaade Voter

  • Jasataade e: INEC (Komission Jawtoore Nijaari Banande) makko
  • Lekkale: Ofis Lokalu Council e polling units fooɗɗuko haa yaafaade njikka
  • Koyɗe Documents: Proof identity (kadi National ID, driver's license)

Jagnaade NIN

  • Seɲto: Jagnaade NIN, Njikkagol, Woppude/Update/Correction, Teeltaade
  • Ndaari: 07002255646 (NIMC General Enquiry)

Ofis Passport

  • Lekkale: Kofare, Numan Road, Jimeta Yola, Adamawa State
  • Naatnude: Online e Nigeria Immigration Service (NIS) website
  • Seɲto: Naatnude passport standard/official, capture biometric data, njikkagol
  • Waare Njilli: 6 lewruuji e 2 lewruuji (express seɲto woni)
