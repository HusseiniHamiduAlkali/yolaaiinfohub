Bartagol UNDP e Yola

Bartagol Hayɓugo Jattugol Hallal Suddantaa

Baɗal Bartagol

Proglamme Tatnugol Debbo (UNDP) waɗi bartagol e kaɓbugal oyoow e Yola wonde wayi jattugol hallal suddantaa Adamawa. Bartagol ngal waɗi hallal lokkal njilal, koɗal duufugol, e caggol njilal taƴta. UNDP waɗi tabbugol kayamba, NGO, e maagal yimande nder tabbugol baɓugo e koofal jaggude wonde mahmu jattugol caggal e banne jomre.

Fillal Bartagol

  • Jom e Girtirde: Kaɓbugal jattugol njilal e tabbugol yimande.
  • Tannugol Coofal: Bartagol duniyaajo e tatnugol koɔfal yimbe.
  • Suudu Duddal: Bartagol hallal duddal e maɗinki hallal.
