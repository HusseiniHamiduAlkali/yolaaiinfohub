Hana Mahmudu: Lolluugal meter nder merkudo YEDC hollude ko boona nder njilal njiikal e daawol tabbugol.

Kartal Njilal

Yola Electricity Distribution Company (YEDC) ina gabbugol hani gonnaaji duggugol meter nder merkudo mahmudu, nder baddugol deegde duggol meter.

Mahmudu Hollude

  • Njilal Lolluugal: Nder merkudo YEDC ko boona
  • Karamba: Doka mahmudu
  • Jattugol: Nder tekniki mahmudu
  • Enngel: Meter mahmudu jom

Warning Signs

  • Unusually low prices
  • No official documentation
  • Unauthorized installers
  • Suspicious meter conditions

How to Report

  • YEDC Hotline: Direct reporting
  • Customer Service: Official channels
  • Online Portal: Digital reporting
  • Local Offices: In-person reports
