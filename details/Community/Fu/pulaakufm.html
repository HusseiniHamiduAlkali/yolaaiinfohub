Tannugol Heerowe e Yillanke Fulɓe.

Baɗal Pulaaku FM

Pulaaku FM 95.3 Yola ko radiyo saajel wonde tannugol e caggitde mala, taƴta, e ngal Fulɓe. "Pulaaku" ko kodol seeso e Fulɓe, koƴal yillanke ko patiiri, tabbude e tapey. Radiyo amin ko tan tannugol mala e merkudo caggal ndab seelitde heerowe Fulɓe, nder tabbugol maagal Fulɓe Adamawa e nder winde fof.

Amin amin waa ko tan gabbugol koofal, tatnugol mala fof ko caggal nder Fulfulde, e gabbugal nder ngal lokkal taƴta kɔnaaɗe nde fosugol mayaɗe. Amin maa wayi tatnugol hakkille baɗe maɗinki heerowe Fulɓe, kɔndu mala seeso e maɗinki cayugol duniyaajo e jajle.

Gabbugal Amin

Gabbugal amin ko mooto taƴta woorde e gabbugal jomre, fof tabbugol nder lentol mala.

  • Haala Pulaaku (Tabbugol Fulɓe) - Daily: Projaam flagshi wonde tabbugol yillanke Pulaaku, koƴal seeso, e mahmudu yimande, fosugol tabbugol jom.
  • Wakaaji Fulani (Musiki Fulɓe) - Daily: Musiki Fulfulde seeso e jomre, koɓal Goge e Sere nder muusikiyanke caggal.
  • Ngol Danye (Tabbugol Sunnde) - Weekly: Projaam pastoralists, tabbugol jom jaafooji, tabnugol jom, koofal lolluudi, e forecast kaarungo.
  • Tarihi Fombina (Taƴta Fombina) - Weekly: Karamba taƴta taƴta Adamawa Emirate, fuddugol, e katyugol mahmudu golle.
  • Linguistics & Literature - Weekly: Segment Fulfulde bartagol e seelitde loodugol gonnaaji.

Miilo Amin e Jokkugol Yimande

Pulaaku FM ko jamma Fulɓe Adamawa e jom caggol. Radiyo ko jamma gabbugal wonde jom dunnde e dunnde gonnaaji, wonde tabbugol jaafooji e bartagol tatnugol. Radiyo amin ko bridjo wonde mahmu maagal gonnaaji seelitde mala Fulɓe e fosugol naabu nder tabbugol gonnaaji. Amin maa wayi uggugol niyam ko mahmu joom caggal hallal suddantaa.
