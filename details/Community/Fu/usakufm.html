Baɗal Usaku FM

Usaku FM 106.1 ko radiyo yimande wonde waɗi mooto e njilal yimande Adamawa. "Usaku," ngal lokkal kɔndu naabu e rewbaani tabbugol, ko amin njilal. Radiyo amin ko tabbugol yimande saajel wonde waɗi merkudo jaggude jaafooji nde tabbugol njilal lokkal, jattugal njilal gonnaaji, e seelitde mala yimbe. Radiyo amin ko jamma jom, jom fuddugol.

Amin amin waa ko fosugol lokkal jom e girtirde nder jaafooji gonnaaji e ndabbugal lokkal, nder watee tan kabar gonnaaji duuniaa lokkal. Amin maa wayi tatnugol gabbugal lokkal, musiki seeso, e tabbugol yimande jom fuddugol, wonde mahmu jokkugol yimande Adamawa fof.

Gabbugal Amin

Gabbugal amin ko root lokkal jiiwe e seeso.

  • The Community Report (Daily): Gabbugol lokkal jom gonnaaji e yillade, coƴal seeso, lolluudi gabbugol, e projaam yimande.
  • Stories of Our Land (Weekly): Tabbugol koƴal seeso, taƴta jaafooji, e koƴal maagal gonnaaji Adamawa, fosugol tannugol seeso.
  • Tradition Meets Today (Weekly): Tabbugol wonde yillanke seeso mahmu hallal njilal caggal, kɔndu jom e tatnugol yimande.
  • Local Music Showcase (Daily): Musiki muusikiyanke gonnaaji, seelitde musiki gonnaaji Adamawa.
  • Farmers' Corner (Daily): Caggal jom gonnaaji wonde jattugol, tannugol, e dukkale jom, e gabbugol kayamba tatnugol gonnaaji.
