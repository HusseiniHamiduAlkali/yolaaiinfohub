Yola Renewal Foundation

Jattugol Fosugol e Tatnugol Yola

Baɗal Foundation

Yola Renewal Foundation ko organisasiyon jeema saajel wonde tatnugol hallal jattugol e fosugol Yola. Foundation ngal maa waɗi projaam jaafooji yimbe, koƴal jattugol yammere, suudu duddal, e tannugol jom hakkille. Nder tabbugol kayamba lokkal e tabbugol yimande, foundation ina mahmu projaam wonde tatnugol tikki, coofal duuniaa, e tatnugol jiiwe yimande. Yola Renewal Foundation ina wayi visan Yola jomre, jom hallal, e jom coofal.

Projaamji Maɗɗi

  • Urban Greening: Sooyde ney e wujjude dukkale aade e parkji.
  • Waste Management: Naatore dudal e nder uddugol duuɗe leydi.
  • Skills Acquisition: Tatnugol yimɓe mbiyu e karannde vokasiyo e gasaaji.
