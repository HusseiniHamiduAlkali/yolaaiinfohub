Red Cross Society Saajo Wuram Yola

Tabbugol Maynu e Tabbugol Njilal

Baɗal Organisasiyon

Red Cross Saajo Wuram e Yola ko organisasiyon maynu saajel wonde tatnugol jummere e fosugol joom banne. Merkudo amin waɗi tabbugol yimbe, tatnugol njilal, e bartagol faafaa. Red Cross ko njilal jommere tatnugol njilal maajo, tabbugol yimande duggitaa, e tabbugol yimande fosugol lumpugol. Merkudo amin waɗi netwok duniyaajo tatnugol tabbugol jommere.

Rewbaani Njilal

  • Tatnugol Njilal: Tabbugol e gartol nder njilal taygol.
  • Bartagol Yimbe: Oyoowol jummere jom gonnaaji.
  • Ndabbugal Muryam: Caggitde muryam ndabbugol gonnaaji jummere.
