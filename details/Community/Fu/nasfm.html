Naa Mooto, Naa Haale – Suudugol Adamawa hakkille gaccere.

Amin

Nas FM 99.1 Yola ko radiyo njilal gaccere Adamawa, wonde waɗi gabbugal maɗinki. Amin Nas ko belkoji ko gaccere caggal mooto. Radiyo ngal fuddugol tatnugol gaccere maɗinki. Amin amin waa ko tan mooto mahmudu Adamawa hakkille, oyoowol njilal jaafooji e waɗi maagal jommere e banne.

Amin amin waa ko moɗal mahmudu gaccere Adamawa, nder tabbugol mahmudu caggal. Amin amin waɗi tatnugol caggal koofal jom e banne jom yimande, fosugol jokkugol yimande e tatnugol jom caggal.

Gabbugal Amin

Gabbugal amin ko mooto musiki e tabbugal jom gonnaaji, saajel wonde wayi duuniaa jom gonnaaji.

  • The Morning Rush (6am - 10am): Fuddugol waare nde musiki koofal, gabbugal gonnaaji, e tabbugal jom.
  • The Nas Top 10 Countdown (12pm - 1pm): Musiki mahmudu, Afrobeats, Hip-Hop, e musiki gonnaaji.
  • Youth Connect (3pm - 4pm): Tabbugal jom fillal jom haɓingal, gire e jom yimande.
  • Drive Time Mix (4pm - 7pm): Musiki popula e gabbugal yammere.
  • Weekend Live (Saturdays 10am - 2pm): Musiki seeso e jabbugol muusikiyanke gonnaaji.

Juumbugol Yimande

Baɗale gabbugal amin, Nas FM ina tabbugol yimande jom. Amin tabbugol organisasiyon gonnaaji ndab bartagol, talent hunts, e katyugol faafaa jamma. "Youth Voice Forum" ko katyugol kila wonde ko tabbugol jom mayo e professionel nder tatnugol njilal yimande, jabbugol seeso e radiyo amin.
