Projaam Jom Hakkille

Baɗal Amin

Youth Association ko saajel wonde tannugol jom Yola nder bartagol, rewbaani e tatnugol cayugol. Amin waɗi merkudo jom nde tatnugol hayɓugo, fuddugol lolluudi, e juumbugol tatnugol yimande.

Projaam Amin

  • Tatnugol Cayugol: Bartagol e caggal
  • Rewbaani Oyoowo: Oyoowo nder maagal e tatnugol
  • Caggal Dukkale: Bartagol e maagal tatnugol
  • Aadol Jom: Musiki, jokkogal, e tabbugal
  • Tabbugol Yimande: Lolluudi duniyaajo

Hol Naatore

  • Annde: 15-25 yimre
  • Jagnaade: Naatnude kala lewru
  • Taƴre Membership: Taƴre mawɗo
  • Koyɗe: Jawdi e jaaɓorgo mawniraaɗo so ɓe ndiyam 18
