YAWEF (Yola Area Women Empowerment Foundation)

Tannugol Macce e Gafu Ndab Caggal Jomre

Baɗal Foundation

YAWEF ko organisasiyon jeema saajel wonde tannugol coofal macce e gafu Yola. Foundation ngal waɗi jattugol njilal macce, wonde koofal dukkale daggel, caggol koɔfal, e caggal faafaa. Foundation ngal waɗi bartagol koofal macce nde mahmu tannugol jom, caggal jom e jokkugol jom yimande. Njilal foundation ngal ko mahmudu tatnugol cinnde jom jaafooji e jattugol suddantaa golle.

Projaam Amin

  • Hayɓugo Koofal: Bartagol jattugol fuufe, lolluudi, e kanfara.
  • Annde Mikro: Jjabbugol koofal koofe wonde mahmu macce koofal jaafooji jom.
  • Faafaa & Dukkale: Kaɓbugal aakugol faafaa macce e caggitde dukkale gafu.
