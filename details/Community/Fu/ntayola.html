Netwok Debbo, Dootal Gonnaaji Joomre e Duuniaa

Baɗal NTA Yola

NTA Yola ko merkudo gonnaaji NTA, netwok tilisu gita duuniaa. Ko radiyo jamma nder banne yimande, nde waɗi gabbugal coofal, bartagol e oyoow jommere gonnaaji Adamawa. Radiyo amin ko root gonnaaji, e amin merkudo ko root maɗinki, nder tabbugol mahmudu jaafooji Adamawa e banne gonnaaji.

Amin amin waa ko tan jirde naabu e tajnde mala ko caɓi banne gabbugol, tatnugol gabbugal jommere e banne jom yimande. Amin ko jamma mahmudu gabbugal kayamba, gabbugal jamma, e gabbugal hollude ofisiyal, e ko njilal mahmudu tatnugol Debbo jaafooji.

Projaam Amin

Gabbugal NTA amin ko mooto projaam nasyon tabbugol e projaam lokkal tatnugol, wonde koote amin waa ko root gonnaaji e root maɗinki.

  • NTA News @ 9 (Daily): Gabbugal nasyon, jabbugol seeso e caggal lokkal.
  • Weekend File (Saturdays): Karamba-style projaam tatnugol gonnaaji, highlights tatnugol, projaam, e jaafooji Adamawa.
  • Good Morning Nigeria (Daily): Tabbugol fuddugol ko tabbugol gonnaaji e lolluudi, nder caggal lokkal.
  • Our People, Our Pride (Weekly): Projaam mala wonde meggal seeso, taƴta jujubugo, e seelitde guurnde gonnaaji maagal Adamawa.
  • Democracy in Motion (Weekly): Tabbugol jamma wonde tabbugol jom jaafooji, njilal jamma, e tatnugol mayo.
