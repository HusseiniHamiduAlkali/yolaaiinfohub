Plaasa Mahmudu Ribadu

Lekkal Nyalol e Rewbaani Yola

Baɗal Plaasa

Plaasa Mahmudu Ribadu ko lekkal taƴta e mala Yola, ko merkudo nyalol jaafooji wonde ennge katyugol gita, wonde wan katyugol guurnde, katyugol jamma, katyugol mala, e katyugol aakugol jamma. Plaasa ko njilal jokkugol yimande e naabu, nder tabbugol lekkal e leppal baddaa wonde mahmu katyugol wonde waɗi duniyaajo taƴta gonnaaji.

Njilal Mahmudu

  • Leppal Baddaa: Ko boona wonde katyugol jamma e kayamba gita.
  • Lekkal Caggol: Joomaraa fof lekkal yola.
  • Mahmudu Mala: Lekkal taƴta wonde katyugol jamma mahmudu.
