Baɗal YPYDA

Yolde Pate Youth Development Association (YPYDA) ko organisasiyon jom caggal wonde tannugol jom e tatnugol yimande Yolde Pate Yola.

Projaam Njilal

  • Tannugol Jom: Bartagol koofal e tatnugol hayɓugo
  • Tatnugol Yimande: Tikki e projaam duddal
  • Caggal Dukkale: Ndabbugol e bartagol
  • Tatnugol Oyoowo: Projaam oyoowo jom
  • Rewbaani Mala: Njilal tannugol seeso
