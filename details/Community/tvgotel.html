About TV Gotel

TV Gotel is a leading private television broadcaster in northeastern Nigeria, offering a diverse range of high-quality news, documentary, and entertainment programming. As the visual arm of the Gotel media group, our station is built on a foundation of journalistic integrity and a commitment to showcasing the rich cultural, social, and economic life of Adamawa State and the wider region. Our state-of-the-art broadcasting facilities and professional team enable us to deliver compelling content that meets international standards.

Our mission is to be the go-to source for credible visual information and captivating entertainment. We provide a platform for local voices and talent, while also bringing national and global perspectives to our audience, serving as a trusted window to the world.

Our Programs

Our programming schedule is a rich tapestry of genres, designed to inform, educate, and entertain a wide viewership.

  • Gotel News at 7 (Daily): A flagship bulletin providing a comprehensive and in-depth look at local, national, and international news, with exclusive reports from our field correspondents.
  • Faces of Adamawa (Weekly): A documentary series that profiles inspiring individuals, traditional leaders, and community heroes who are making a significant impact in the state.
  • The Business Hub (Weekly): A program that explores local entrepreneurship, agribusiness, and investment opportunities in Adamawa, offering insights for both new and established businesses.
  • Cultural Showcase (Weekly): A vibrant show celebrating the unique dances, music, and festivals of the various ethnic groups in the state.
  • Weekend Gotel (Saturdays): A fun and interactive show featuring celebrity interviews, live performances, and entertainment news.
