Total Blackout in Yola

TCN Repair Work Underway

Current Status: Five-day power outage affecting Yola and surrounding areas during repair work.

Situation Overview

The residents of Yola and Jalingo, the capitals of Adamawa and Taraba States, are experiencing a five-day darkness due to essential repair work being carried out by the Transmission Company of Nigeria (TCN).

Areas Affected

  • Yola South: Complete outage
  • Yola North: Complete outage
  • Surrounding Communities: Limited power supply
  • Essential Services: Operating on backup power

TCN Repair Schedule

  • Duration: 5 days
  • Work Scope: Essential infrastructure repairs
  • Expected Completion: As scheduled
  • Progress Updates: Daily reports available

Community Advisory

  • Use alternative power sources where possible
  • Conserve water during the outage period
  • Keep emergency lighting ready
  • Check on vulnerable neighbors
