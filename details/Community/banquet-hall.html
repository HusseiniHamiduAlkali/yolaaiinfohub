Gov. House Banquet Hall

The Official Venue for State Events and Dignitaries

About the Hall

The Government House Banquet Hall is the official venue for high-profile state functions, including government dinners, award ceremonies, and diplomatic receptions. The hall is known for its elegant design, luxurious ambiance, and formal setting. It is not typically available for private events but serves as a symbol of prestige and a backdrop for key state activities. The hall is meticulously maintained to host guests of honor and state dignitaries.

Hall's Purpose

  • State Functions: Hosting official government and state events.
  • Official Dinners: For visiting dignitaries and foreign delegations.
  • Ceremonial Events: A grand setting for award presentations and ceremonies.
