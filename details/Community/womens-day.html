International Women's Day in Yola

Celebrating Women's Achievements and Empowerment

Event Focus: Recognizing and celebrating women's achievements while raising awareness about gender equality and women's rights.

Event Overview

International Women's Day is celebrated annually in Yola with various activities aimed at highlighting women's contributions to society and promoting gender equality.

Event Activities

  • Keynote Speeches: By prominent women leaders
  • Panel Discussions: On women's issues
  • Awards Ceremony: Recognizing achievements
  • Skills Workshops: Empowerment sessions
  • Cultural Performances: Celebrating women

Key Topics

  • Women's rights and advocacy
  • Economic empowerment
  • Education and career development
  • Health and well-being
  • Leadership and mentorship

Event Features

  • Exhibition: Women entrepreneurs showcase
  • Networking: Professional connections
  • Health Screenings: Free check-ups
  • Mentorship: Career guidance
  • Resource Fair: Support services

Impact Goals

  • Increased awareness of women's rights
  • Enhanced economic opportunities
  • Stronger support networks
  • Improved access to resources
  • Greater community engagement
