Youth Skills Development Program

Empowering Youth Through Practical Skills

Program Focus: Developing practical and technical skills in youth for sustainable employment and entrepreneurship.

Program Overview

The Youth Skills Development Program is a comprehensive initiative aimed at equipping young people in Yola with practical skills needed for employment and entrepreneurship in today's digital economy.

Available Training Programs

  • Digital Skills: Web development, graphic design, digital marketing
  • Technical Skills: Electronics repair, automotive maintenance
  • Soft Skills: Communication, leadership, teamwork
  • Entrepreneurship: Business planning, financial literacy

Program Benefits

  • Hands-on practical training
  • Industry-recognized certification
  • Internship opportunities
  • Mentorship from industry experts
  • Job placement assistance

Enrollment Information

  • Duration: 3-6 months depending on program
  • Requirements: Age 18-35, basic education
  • Schedule: Flexible timing options
  • Location: Multiple training centers in Yola

Success Stories

  • Over 500 youth trained annually
  • 75% employment rate after program completion
  • 30% of graduates start their own businesses
  • Strong industry partnerships
