Yola Wards & Proximity

Understanding the Social and Administrative Fabric of Yola

Section Focus: Breakdown of Yola North and Yola South wards, their proximity, and key features.

Yola North Local Government Area (Jimeta)

  • Alkalawa
  • Ajiya
  • Doubeli
  • Gwadabawa
  • Jambutu (Yola International Airport)
  • Karewa
  • Limawa
  • Luggere
  • Nassarawo
  • Rumde
  • Yelwa

Key Features: Centrality, Jimeta Market, Airport proximity, mixed residential/commercial, developed road network.

Yola South Local Government Area (Old Yola Town & Environs)

  • Adarawo
  • Bako
  • Bole/Yolde Pate
  • Namtari
  • Ngurore
  • Makama 'A'
  • Makama 'B'
  • Mbamba
  • Mbamoi
  • Toungo
  • Yolde Kohi

Key Features: Traditional core, Benue River proximity, rural-urban fringe, infrastructure development.

General Proximity & Relationship

  • "Twin Cities": Yola (Old Town) & Jimeta (Commercial Hub)
  • Distance: 5-9 km between Jimeta and Old Yola Town
  • Main connectors: Roads, public transport, new bye-pass
  • Transportation: Keke NAPEP, taxis, buses
