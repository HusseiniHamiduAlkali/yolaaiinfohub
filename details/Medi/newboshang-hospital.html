About Our Facility

New Boshang Clinic is a modern, multi-specialty healthcare facility providing a wide range of secondary care services. Our clinic is built on the principles of patient-centric care, integrating modern medical technology with compassionate service. We offer a holistic approach to health, from diagnostic services to specialized treatments, all under one roof.

Departments and Specialties

  • General Medicine & Diagnostics: For all general health concerns.
  • Maternity & Ante-Natal Care: Comprehensive services for expectant mothers.
  • Laboratory Services: Equipped with advanced testing capabilities.
  • Emergency Unit: Ready to handle urgent medical cases 24/7.
  • In-patient Care: For patients requiring continuous medical supervision.

Why Choose Us?

We are dedicated to providing a comfortable and reassuring environment for our patients. Our staff is trained to offer not just medical expertise but also emotional support, making the healthcare experience as stress-free as possible. We are committed to upholding the highest standards of ethics and professionalism.
