About Our Clinic

Galbose Specialist Clinic is a private secondary care facility renowned for its commitment to patient-centric and holistic healthcare. Our clinic provides a wide range of specialized medical services, focusing on accurate diagnostics and effective treatment plans. Our goal is to be a trusted medical partner for families and individuals, guiding them through their health journey with expertise and compassion.

Our Services

  • General Practice and Family Medicine
  • Comprehensive Medical Check-ups
  • Cardiology & Hypertension Clinic
  • Gastroenterology Consultations
  • Minor Surgical Procedures
  • 24-hour Emergency Services

Our Philosophy

At Galbose Specialist Clinic, we believe that health is a state of complete physical, mental, and social well-being. Our approach is to treat the root cause of illnesses while also considering the overall health of the patient. We are committed to building a lasting relationship with our patients, providing them with continuous and reliable care.
