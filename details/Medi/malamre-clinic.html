About Our Center

Malamre Clinic is a dedicated primary healthcare center serving the Malamre community and its environs. Our facility is equipped to handle essential medical needs, focusing on preventive care and routine treatments. Our compassionate team of health workers is committed to providing personalized care in a comfortable and friendly environment.

Core Services

  • General Medical Consultations
  • Minor Surgeries and Procedures
  • Family Planning Services
  • Health Screening and Diagnosis
  • Referral Services to Specialist Centers

Community Focus

We are more than just a clinic; we are a community partner. Malamre Clinic regularly organizes free health camps, vaccination drives, and public awareness seminars to address common health issues and promote healthy living. We strive to be the most trusted and reliable health resource for our neighbors.
