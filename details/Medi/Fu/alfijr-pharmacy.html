Al Fijr Pharmacy

Nana waɗde kawtal e jam e hakkil

E nder Farmasiya amen

Al Fijr Pharmacy on innde waɗiima e kawtal, o waɗi waɗtude laawolji e leeɗiiji laɓɓi. Min jogii daraja e jam e hakkil, kono min waɗiima ɓuri wonde ngoongu ngam yamiroowo e jam. Min waɗi laawolji yimɓe e feere e min jolde e nder ɓe e nder majji ɗiɗi.

Laawolji Muuyɗi

  • Laawol jokkere be kosɗe
  • Laawol ngoodi e ndiyam e keewal
  • Kisal e jam e maɓɓe
  • Nafoore kisal e laawol
