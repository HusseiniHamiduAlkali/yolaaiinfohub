Yola Dispensary

Providing Basic Medical Services to the Yola Community

Our Mission

The Yola Dispensary is a government-operated primary health facility dedicated to providing essential and accessible healthcare to the residents of Yola. Our core function is to handle everyday medical concerns, dispense necessary medications, and offer a first point of contact for healthcare in the community. We work tirelessly to ensure that basic medical attention is never out of reach for anyone.

Services at a Glance

  • Essential Medication Dispensing
  • Basic Clinical Diagnosis
  • Outpatient Consultations
  • Child and Adult Vaccinations
  • Health Education Seminars

Our Commitment

As a public health facility, we are committed to upholding the highest standards of care while ensuring our services remain free or very low-cost. We are an integral part of the state's healthcare network, working to improve public health one person at a time.
