About Our Clinic

Valli Clinic na primary healthcare wey serve local community. Our mission na provide accessible, affordable an good basic medical care. We focus on preventive medicine an health education to help people live healthier life.

Services Offered

  • Routine Check-ups & Consultations
  • Immunization for Children & Adults
  • Maternal & Child Health Services
  • Minor Wound Care & Dressing
  • First Aid & Emergency Response

Our Focus

We believe in proactive healthcare. Our staff dey work with community to do screenings, awareness campaigns an vaccination drives. We dey committed to be first point of contact for health concerns.
