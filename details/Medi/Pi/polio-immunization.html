Polio Immunization Drive

Public health program for community PHCCs

About the Campaign

This polio immunization campaign na important public health effort to wipe out polio for our communities. Health workers an volunteers dey give oral polio vaccine (OPV) to all pikin under five. Dem dey run am for PHCCs an door-to-door make sure no pikin miss am. This work important to build better, polio-free future for our pikin dem.

Campaign Objectives

  • Make sure all pikin under five dey vaccinated.
  • Raise awareness about routine immunization.
  • Give correct information an clear myths about polio vaccine.
  • Monitor progress to reach polio-free Adamawa State.
