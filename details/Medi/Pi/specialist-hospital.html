About Our Hospital

Specialist Hospital Yola na leading secondary healthcare for Adamawa. With skilled doctors an nurses, we dey offer many specialized medical an surgical services. Our hospital get modern diagnostic tools an facilities to handle complex cases.

Key Departments an Services

  • General Surgery: Including appendectomy an hernia repair.
  • Internal Medicine: Management of chronic diseases like hypertension an diabetes.
  • Pediatrics: Care for pikin health.
  • Obstetrics & Gynecology: Maternal care an delivery services.
  • Radiology: X-ray, Ultrasound an imaging.

Our Commitment

Our goal na to provide top medical care with focus on patient safety an comfort. We dey upgrade equipment an train staff to stay up-to-date. As referral center, we play big role for state health system.
