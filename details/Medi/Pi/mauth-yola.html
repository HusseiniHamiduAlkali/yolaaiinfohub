Modibbo Adama University Teaching Hospital (MAUTH), Yola

Big centre for tertiary healthcare, research an training

About MAUTH Yola

MAUTH na top tertiary hospital for Adamawa an north-east region. As teaching hospital we dey provide advanced medical care, carry out research an train future doctors, nurses an other health workers. We be final referral point for complex cases.

Special Services an Departments

  • Cardiothoracic Surgery: Complex heart an chest operations.
  • Neurosurgery & Neurology: Brain an nervous system care.
  • Oncology & Radiotherapy: Cancer treatment.
  • Endoscopy & Laparoscopy: Small-cut diagnostic an surgical procedures.
  • Training & Research: Clinical training for students an research programs.

Our Role

As teaching hospital our impact pass patient care. We dey champion medical innovation, research an professional development. Our facilities like OT, ICU an advanced labs make us important for the region.
