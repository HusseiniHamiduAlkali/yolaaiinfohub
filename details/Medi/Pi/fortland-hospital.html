Wetin Our Hospital Dey Do

Fortland Orthopedic Hospital na top place for bone care for Adamawa. We dey handle diagnosis, treatment, an rehabilitation for anything wey concern bones an muscles. From sport injury, break bone, to chronic joint pain, our team of surgeons an physiotherapists dey help people regain mobility an beta life.

Our Specialized Services

  • Orthopedic Surgery: Joint replacement an spinal surgery.
  • Physiotherapy & Rehabilitation: Post-op an non-surgical therapy to help recovery.
  • Trauma & Fracture Care: Emergency an long-term care for fractures.
  • Arthroscopy: Small-cut joint surgery (minimally invasive).

Focus on Rehabilitation

We no dey stop after surgery. Our physiotherapy center get skilled staff wey dey help patients recover full function an mobility. We dey use holistic approach make sure person fit return normal life.
