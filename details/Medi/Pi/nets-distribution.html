Mosquito Nets Distribution

Make we fight Malaria for Yola with preventive nets

About the Initiative

This program dey distribute Long-Lasting Insecticidal Nets (LLINs) to households for Yola. Malaria still plenty for the area, an treated mosquito nets na one of best low-cost way to prevent am. Government, local health agencies an partners join body for this campaign to reduce malaria for children an pregnant women.

Program Goals

  • Make plenty household get an use treated mosquito nets.
  • Teach people how to use an maintain the nets well.
  • Reduce malaria cases for our community.
  • Make families dey active for their health.
