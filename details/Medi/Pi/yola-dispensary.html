Yola Dispensary

We dey provide basic medical services to Yola community

Our Mission

Yola Dispensary na government primary health facility wey dey provide essential an accessible healthcare to Yola people. We dey handle everyday medical matters, dispense medicines an serve as first point of contact for healthcare. We dey try make sure basic medical help dey available to everybody.

Services at a Glance

  • Essential Medication Dispensing
  • Basic Clinical Diagnosis
  • Outpatient Consultations
  • Child an Adult Vaccinations
  • Health Education Seminars

Our Commitment

As public health facility, we dey committed to give high standard care an keep services free or cheap. We dey play important role for state health system, improving public health one person at a time.
