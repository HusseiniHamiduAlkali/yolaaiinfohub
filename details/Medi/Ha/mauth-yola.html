Cibiyar Sabis na Lafiya a Yola

Game da Cibiyar

Mauth Yola tana aiki don samar da kulawa mai inganci ga mazauna Yola. Muna ba da shawarwari, gwaje-gwaje, magunguna, da tallafin rigakafi don inganta lafiyar jama'a.

Ayyuka

  • Duba lafiya da gwaje-gwaje
  • Tallafin rigakafi
  • Shawarwari daga likitoci
