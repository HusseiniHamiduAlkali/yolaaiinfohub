Game da Asibitin

Specialist Hospital tana da ƙwararrun likitoci a fannoni daban-daban domin samar da kulawa ta musamman. Muna ba da shawarwari, gwaje-gwaje, da magani na musamman ga marasa lafiya tare da bukatar kulawa na musamman.

Sabis

  • Kwararrun likitoci
  • Gwaje-gwajen ƙwararru
  • Tiyata ta musamman
