Raba Kyamararriyar Kwari (Mosquito Nets)

Shirin Rigakafin Zazzabin Cizon Sauro don Kariya daga Maleriya

Game da Shirin

An raba kyamararriyar kwari ga iyalai domin rage haɗarin kamuwa da maleriya. Wannan shiri yana taimakawa wajen samar da kariya musamman ga yara da mata masu juna biyu. Muna ba da horo kan amfani da nets da yadda ake kiyaye su.

Makasudin Shirin

  • Kare iyalai daga cizon sauro
  • Rage yaduwar maleriya a cikin al'umma
  • Ilmantar da jama'a game da amfani da nets
