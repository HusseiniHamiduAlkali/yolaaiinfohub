Fortland Orthopedic Hospital

Specialized Care for Bones and Joints

About Our Hospital

Fortland Orthopedic Hospital is the premier orthopedic healthcare facility in Adamawa, exclusively dedicated to the diagnosis, treatment, and rehabilitation of all conditions affecting the musculoskeletal system. From sports injuries and fractures to chronic joint pain and congenital deformities, our hospital offers comprehensive care for bones, joints, ligaments, and tendons. Our team of orthopedic surgeons, physiotherapists, and support staff is committed to helping patients regain mobility and improve their quality of life.

Our Specialized Services

  • Orthopedic Surgery: Including joint replacement and spinal surgery.
  • Physiotherapy & Rehabilitation: Post-operative and non-surgical therapy.
  • Trauma & Fracture Care: Emergency and long-term care for all types of fractures.
  • Arthroscopy: Minimally invasive joint surgery.

Focus on Rehabilitation

Our commitment extends beyond surgery. We have a state-of-the-art physiotherapy and rehabilitation center, staffed with experts dedicated to helping patients recover full function and mobility. Our holistic approach ensures a complete return to health.
