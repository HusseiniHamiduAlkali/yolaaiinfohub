Modibbo Adama University Teaching Hospital (MAUTH), Yola

Ebe Akwụkwọ Ọgwụ, Nnyocha na Ọzụzụ

Banyere MAUTH Yola

MAUTH, Yola, bụ ụlọ ọgwụ tertiary kacha mkpa na steeti Adamawa na mpaghara ugwu ọwụwa anyanwụ. Dị ka ụlọ ọgwụ nkuzi nke Modibbo Adama University, ọrụ anyị bụ inye nlekọta dị elu, mee nchọpụta ọhụrụ, na zụọ ndị dọkịta na ndị nọọsụ na ndị ọrụ ahụike. Anyị na-anata ikpe mgbakwunye siri ike site na ebe ndị ọzọ.

Ọrụ Pụrụ Iche na Ụlọ Ọrụ

  • Ọgwụ obi na ahụ dị n'ígwé ahụ: Ọrụ obi na mgbakwunye.
  • Neurosurgery & Neurology: Nlekọta maka ụbụrụ na usoro akwara.
  • Oncology & Radiotherapy: Ọgwụ maka cancer na radiotherapy.
  • Endoscopy & Laparoscopy: Usoro nyocha na obere ọrụ.
  • Ịzụ & Nnyocha: Ọzụzụ ụmụ akwụkwọ ọgwụ na nnyocha postgraduate.

Ọrụ Anyi na Ahụike

Dị ka ụlọ ọgwụ nkuzi, mmetụta anyị gafere nlekọta onye ọrịa. Anyị bụ etiti maka ihe ọhụrụ n'ọrụ ọgwụ, nnyocha, na mmepe nke ndị ọrụ. Ihe nkasi obi na ụlọ nyocha dị elu na-arụ ọrụ iji kwado mkpa mpaghara nlekọta ahụike.
