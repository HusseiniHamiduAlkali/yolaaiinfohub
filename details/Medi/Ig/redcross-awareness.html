Mmemme Mgbasa Ozi Red Cross

Ịkwalite First Aid, nkwadebe mberede, na ike obodo

Banyere Mmemme

Red Cross Society na-eme mgbasa ozi n'obodo Yola iji kwalite nchekwa na nkwadebe. A na-akụziri ndị obodo ihe nchekwa ndụ, gụnyere First Aid, na otu esi edozi mberede na ihe mberede eke. Red Cross na-enyere obodo aka ịnwe ike izute ihe mberede ma nyere ndị nọ na mkpa aka.

Ebe A Na-elekwasị Anya

  • Ịzụ First Aid: Ọmụmụ ihe banyere otu esi edozi mmerụ na mberede.
  • Mgbochi Ihe Mberede: Ịkụziri ndị mmadụ otu esi edozi maka floods, ọkụ, na ihe mberede ndị ọzọ.
  • Ngosi Ọbara: Ịkpalite iwu inye ọbara n'enweghị ụgwọ iji nyere ụlọ ọgwụ.
  • Ịkwalite Ahụike na Nchekwa: Ikwu banyere nhicha na mgbochi ọrịa.
