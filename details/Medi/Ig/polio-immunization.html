Ngwa ọgwụ Polio

Mmemme ahụike ọha na ụlọ ọrụ Primary Healthcare Centers

Banyere Mmemme

Mmemme ọgwụ polio bụ mgbalị dị mkpa nke ebumnuche ya bụ iwepụ polio n'obodo anyị. Nke a gụnyekwuru ijikọta ndị ọrụ ahụike na ndị mgbasa ozi iji nyefee ọgwụ oriri polio (OPV) nye ụmụaka niile dị n'okpuru afọ ise. A na-eme mmemme a n'ụlọ ọgwụ PHCC na site n'ihu n'ụlọ iji jide n'aka na a naghị ahapụ ụmụaka ọ bụla.

Ebumnuche Mmemme

  • Iji hụ na ụmụaka niile n'okpuru afọ ise natara ọgwụ.
  • Ibulite mmata banyere mkpa ọgwụ mgbochi kwa oge.
  • Ikwu eziokwu na idozi amụma gbasara ọgwụ polio.
  • Ileba anya nke mmepe ruo n'iji mee Adamawa steeti gafere polio.
