Red Cross Society Awareness Campaign

Promoting First Aid, Disaster Preparedness, and Community Resilience

About the Campaign

The Red Cross Society, a globally recognized humanitarian organization, conducts regular awareness campaigns in Yola to promote a culture of safety and preparedness. These campaigns are designed to equip residents with essential life-saving skills, including basic first aid, and to educate them on how to respond to emergencies and natural disasters. The Red Cross is committed to building a more resilient community, capable of providing immediate assistance during crises and supporting those in need.

Key Areas of Focus

  • First Aid Training: Workshops on how to handle common injuries and medical emergencies.
  • Disaster Risk Reduction: Educating the public on how to prepare for floods, fires, and other disasters.
  • Blood Donation Drives: Encouraging voluntary blood donation to support local hospitals.
  • Health and Hygiene Promotion: Spreading awareness about sanitation and disease prevention.
