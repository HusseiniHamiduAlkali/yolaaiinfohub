Modibbo Adama University Teaching Hospital (MAUTH), Yola

A Center for Excellence in Tertiary Healthcare, Research & Training

About MAUTH Yola

MAUTH, Yola, is the premier tertiary healthcare institution in Adamawa State and the entire northeastern region. As a teaching hospital affiliated with the Modibbo Adama University, our primary functions are to provide highly specialized and advanced medical care, conduct cutting-edge medical research, and train the next generation of doctors, nurses, and other healthcare professionals. We are the final referral point for complex and life-threatening cases from primary and secondary healthcare centers across the region.

Specialized Services and Departments

  • Cardiothoracic Surgery: Complex heart and chest surgeries.
  • Neurosurgery & Neurology: Specialized care for the brain and nervous system.
  • Oncology & Radiotherapy: Cancer treatment and management.
  • Endoscopy & Laparoscopy: Minimally invasive diagnostic and surgical procedures.
  • Training & Research: Clinical training for medical students and postgraduate research.

Our Role in Healthcare

As a teaching hospital, our impact goes beyond patient care. We are a hub for medical innovation, research, and professional development. Our a and training programs ensure a continuous supply of skilled healthcare personnel for the region. Our extensive facilities, including multiple operating theaters, intensive care units, and advanced diagnostic labs, make us an invaluable asset to the health sector.
