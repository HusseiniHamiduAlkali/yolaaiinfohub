About Hajja Ammi Jumma'at Mosque

The Hajja Ammi Jumma'at Mosque is a welcoming and community-focused Islamic worship center in Yola. Known for its inclusive approach and friendly atmosphere, the mosque serves as a spiritual home for many members of the local Muslim community, emphasizing both religious practice and community solidarity.

Community Focus

  • Known for welcoming atmosphere
  • Inclusive approach to Islamic practice
  • Strong community engagement programs
  • Focus on family-centered activities
  • Social support services for community members
  • Regular community gatherings and events

Religious Services

  • Five daily prayers with consistent scheduling
  • Friday congregational prayers (Jumu'ah) with large congregation
  • Islamic education classes for all ages
  • Quranic study and recitation programs
  • Marriage and family counseling services
  • Eid celebrations and festival observances

Facilities Available

  • Spacious prayer halls for daily prayers
  • Separate prayer areas for men and women
  • Well-maintained ablution facilities
  • Comfortable prayer environment
  • Community meeting spaces
  • Adequate parking for visitors

Educational Programs

  • Women's Quranic study circles
  • Youth Islamic development programs
  • Islamic history and heritage courses
  • Arabic language classes
  • Islamic ethics and morality teachings

Community Services

  • Social welfare assistance programs
  • Interfaith community dialogue
  • Youth mentorship initiatives
  • Family counseling services
  • Community health awareness programs
  • Educational scholarship programs
