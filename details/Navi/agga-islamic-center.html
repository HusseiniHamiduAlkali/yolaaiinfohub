Agga Islamic Center, Yola

Islamic Educational and Worship Center

About Agga Islamic Center

The Agga Islamic Center is a prominent Islamic educational and worship facility serving the local Muslim community in Yola. Beyond providing a space for religious worship, the center is committed to Islamic education, community development, and fostering understanding of Islamic principles among its members.

Educational Programs

  • Quranic recitation classes for all ages
  • Islamic jurisprudence (Fiqh) study groups
  • Arabic language courses
  • Islamic history and heritage programs
  • Hadith study circles
  • Youth Islamic education initiatives

Worship Facilities

  • Well-equipped prayer halls for daily prayers
  • Separate prayer areas for men and women
  • Modern ablution facilities
  • Spacious courtyard for overflow prayers
  • Carpeted prayer areas for comfort
  • Climate-controlled prayer halls

Community Services

  • Islamic counseling and guidance services
  • Community outreach programs
  • Social welfare initiatives
  • Marriage and family counseling
  • Youth development programs
  • Interfaith dialogue activities

Visitor Information

  • Open for all daily prayers
  • Visitors welcome year-round
  • Respectful dress codes appreciated
  • Free ablution facilities available
  • Parking facilities provided
  • Refreshment areas for visitors

Contact Information

Location: Yola, Adamawa State

The center is strategically located to serve the local Muslim community with accessible facilities and educational opportunities.
