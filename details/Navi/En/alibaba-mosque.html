Alibaba Jumma'at Mosque, Yola

Local Community Prayer Center

About Alibaba Jumma'at Mosque

The Alibaba Jumma'at Mosque is a local mosque serving the residential neighborhoods of Yola. As a community-level prayer center, it provides daily prayer services and facilitates social gathering among local Muslim residents, maintaining strong bonds within the neighborhood.

Daily Prayer Services

  • Five daily prayers (Salat) with fixed timings
  • Friday congregational prayers (Jumu'ah)
  • Extended prayer hours during Ramadan
  • Taraweeh prayers during the holy month
  • Eid prayers and celebrations
  • Special Islamic occasion prayers

Community Gatherings

  • Daily prayers bringing neighborhood together
  • Friday congregation fostering community bonds
  • Neighborhood social events and gatherings
  • Community problem-solving discussions
  • Welfare assistance for community members
  • Collective celebration of Islamic festivals

Facilities and Features

  • Well-maintained prayer hall
  • Clean ablution facilities
  • Comfortable prayer environment
  • Separate prayer areas for men and women
  • Community meeting space
  • Safe parking area

Islamic Education

  • Quranic teaching for children
  • Islamic principles classes
  • Religious guidance for community members
  • Islamic ethics training
  • Youth religious education
  • Adult Islamic study circles

Community Support Services

  • Support for families in need
  • Marriage and family guidance
  • Youth mentorship programs
  • Community health initiatives
  • Neighborhood security cooperation
  • Social welfare assistance

Contact Information

Location: Residential Area, Yola, Adamawa State

The mosque serves the local neighborhood and is easily accessible to residents for daily prayer services and community activities.
