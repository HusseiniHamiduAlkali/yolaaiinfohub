About Items7 Restaurant

Items7 Restaurant is a modern casual dining establishment in Yola, offering a diverse menu of dishes to suit all tastes. The restaurant provides a relaxed atmosphere perfect for families, friends, and business associates.

Dining Options

  • Diverse international and local menu items
  • Casual and comfortable dining environment
  • Friendly and attentive service staff
  • Fast service options available
  • Family-friendly atmosphere
  • Takeaway and delivery services

Menu Variety

Choose from a wide selection of appetizers, main courses, and desserts. Items7 features both traditional Adamawa cuisine and popular international dishes to satisfy diverse palates.
