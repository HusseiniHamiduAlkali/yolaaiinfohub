About LCCN Church

The Lutheran Church of Christ (LCCN) No. 1 in Yola is a historic and significant Christian worship center serving the Christian community in Adamawa State. As one of the major Christian congregations, the church plays a vital role in spiritual guidance, community service, and Christian education. The church is known for its welcoming atmosphere and commitment to Christian fellowship and outreach.
