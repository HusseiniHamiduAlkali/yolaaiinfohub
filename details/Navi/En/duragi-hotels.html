About Duragi Hotels

Duragi Hotels Yola is a business-oriented accommodation provider in Yola, designed to meet the needs of corporate travelers and business professionals. The hotel offers comfortable rooms and comprehensive business amenities.

Business Facilities

  • Conference rooms with modern AV equipment
  • High-speed Wi-Fi in all areas
  • Business center with secretarial services
  • Meeting spaces for various group sizes
  • Parking facilities
  • Express check-in and check-out services

Guest Rooms

Comfortable rooms equipped with work desks, ergonomic chairs, flat-screen televisions, and complimentary Wi-Fi. All rooms feature air conditioning and modern bathroom facilities.
