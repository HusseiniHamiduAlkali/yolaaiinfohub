Keke Napep (Tricycle)

Local Transportation Services

About Keke Napep Service

Keke Napep tricycles are the most popular local transportation option in Yola for short-distance travel. These three-wheeled vehicles provide affordable and convenient transport throughout the city and surrounding areas.

Service Features

  • Short-Distance Transport
  • Affordable Fares
  • Available Throughout the Day
  • Multiple Routes Covered
  • Quick Pick-up and Drop-off
  • Accessible in All Areas

Common Routes

  • Jimeta to Yola Town
  • Motor Parks to Residential Areas
  • Markets and Shopping Centers
  • Hotels and Hospitality Venues
  • Educational Institutions

Contact Information

Location: Throughout Yola, Adamawa State

Find Keke Napep tricycles at designated stands throughout the city for convenient local transport.
