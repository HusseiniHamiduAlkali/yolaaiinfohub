Jabbama Jippu Jam (Yola Toll Gate)

Historic Entrance Point to Yola City

About Jabbama Jippu Jam Toll Gate

The Jabbama Jippu Jam, commonly known as the Yola Toll Gate, is a historic entrance point to Yola city serving as a major gateway for travelers and commerce. This iconic structure has been a recognizable landmark for decades, facilitating the movement of vehicles and goods into and out of the city. The toll gate plays a crucial role in revenue collection for road maintenance and development projects.

Contact Information

Location: Main Entrance to Yola City, Adamawa State

The Jabbama Jippu Jam Toll Gate serves as the primary entry point to Yola city. All vehicles entering or exiting the city must pass through this checkpoint. For toll inquiries, vehicle registration issues, or emergency assistance, contact the toll gate management office located at the facility.
