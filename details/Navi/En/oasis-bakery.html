About OASIS Bakery

OASIS Bakery is a beloved local bakery in Yola, famous for freshly baked bread, delicious pastries, and custom-made cakes. The bakery has been serving the community for years with consistent quality and traditional baking methods.

Bakery Products

  • Fresh bread baked daily
  • Assorted pastries and croissants
  • Custom cakes for special occasions
  • Donuts and sweet treats
  • Savory baked goods
  • Seasonal specialty items

Custom Orders

OASIS Bakery accepts custom orders for birthdays, weddings, and other special events. Our bakers can create personalized cakes and pastries to your specifications with advance notice.
