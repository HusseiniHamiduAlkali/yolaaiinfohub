Modibbo Zailani Mosque, Yola

Historic Islamic Center with Distinctive Architecture

About Modibbo Zailani Mosque

The Modibbo Zailani Mosque is a historic and culturally significant Islamic worship center in Yola, known for its distinctive architecture and religious importance. The mosque stands as a testament to the Islamic heritage of Adamawa State and serves as a major gathering place for the Muslim community.

Architectural Highlights

  • Distinctive architectural design reflecting Islamic traditions
  • Well-designed prayer halls with elegant interiors
  • Traditional dome and minaret structures
  • Spacious courtyard for congregation
  • Intricate decorative elements and Islamic art

Religious Services Offered

  • Five daily prayers (Salat) with fixed timings
  • Friday congregational prayers (Jumu'ah)
  • Quranic study circles and Islamic education
  • Eid prayers and celebrations
  • Islamic marriage ceremonies (Nikah)
  • Community religious gatherings

Community Impact

  • Serves hundreds of community members daily
  • Hosts major Islamic events and celebrations
  • Provides Islamic education for all age groups
  • Facilitates social cohesion within the Muslim community
  • Known for its welcoming and inclusive environment

Visiting Information

  • Open for all five daily prayers
  • Visitors are welcome during prayer times
  • Appropriate dress codes are respected
  • Clean ablution facilities available
  • Designated parking area for vehicles

Contact Information

Location: Yola, Adamawa State

The mosque is easily accessible from various parts of Yola and serves as a landmark for the local Muslim community.
