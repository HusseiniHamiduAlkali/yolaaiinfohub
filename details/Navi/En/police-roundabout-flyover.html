About Police Roundabout Flyover

The Police Roundabout Flyover in Jimeta, Yola is a modern elevated roadway structure designed to improve traffic flow in central Jimeta. This contemporary infrastructure project represents a significant development in Yola's transportation network, connecting key areas of the city and reducing congestion at one of the busiest intersections in Jimeta.
