About EYN Church

EYN Church Yola is a vibrant congregation of the Church of the Brethren, offering regular worship services and spiritual guidance to its members and the wider Christian community. The church is deeply rooted in Christian principles and actively engaged in community development, fellowship, and evangelism. EYN stands as a beacon of Christian faith and community service in Yola.
