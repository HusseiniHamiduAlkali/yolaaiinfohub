About Marwa Shawarma & Grills

Marwa Shawarma & Grills is a popular restaurant in Yola specializing in delicious shawarma and grilled specialties. The restaurant is known for its authentic Middle Eastern flavors and perfectly grilled meats.

Menu Specialties

  • Chicken shawarma with various marinades
  • Beef and lamb shawarma
  • Grilled meat platters
  • Mixed grill combinations
  • Shawarma wraps and sandwiches
  • Fresh salads and sides

Grilling Excellence

Our expert grillmasters prepare each dish with precision, ensuring perfectly cooked, flavorful meat every time. We use quality ingredients and traditional grilling techniques.
