About Madugu Rockview Hotel

Madugu Rockview Hotel is a contemporary accommodation establishment in Yola with stunning views of the surrounding landscape. The hotel combines modern amenities with a strategic location for business and leisure travelers.

Hotel Facilities

  • Comfortable guest rooms with modern furnishings
  • Scenic viewing areas with city views
  • Restaurant and bar services
  • Wi-Fi connectivity
  • Parking facilities
  • 24-hour reception desk

Room Amenities

All rooms feature air conditioning, satellite television, complimentary toiletries, and work desks for business travelers. Selected rooms offer panoramic city views.
