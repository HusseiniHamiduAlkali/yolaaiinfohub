Gorilla Park

Wildlife Park and Recreational Area

About Gorilla Park

Gorilla Park is a wildlife park in Yola dedicated to wildlife conservation and public education. It offers visitors the opportunity to observe diverse animal species in well-maintained habitats.

Park Features

  • Wildlife Habitats
  • Animal Enclosures
  • Viewing Areas
  • Educational Exhibits
  • Walking Paths
  • Rest Areas
  • Photography Opportunities

Activities

  • Wildlife Observation
  • Educational Tours
  • Photography
  • Family Visits
  • Nature Study
  • Recreational Activities
