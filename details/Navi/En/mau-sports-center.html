About MAU Yola Sports Center

The MAU (Modibbo Adama University of Technology) Yola Sports Center is a premier university sports complex featuring modern facilities for various athletic activities. Located on the campus of MAUTH, the sports center serves students, staff, and the wider community, providing excellent opportunities for sports training, competitions, and recreational activities.
