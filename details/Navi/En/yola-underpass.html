Ongoing Construction of Underpass at Fire Service Roundabout, Jimeta Yola

Infrastructure Development Project for Traffic Flow Improvement

About Fire Service Roundabout Underpass

The Fire Service Roundabout Underpass is an ongoing infrastructure development project in Jimeta, Yola, designed to significantly improve traffic flow at one of the busiest intersections in the city. This major construction initiative represents a commitment to modernizing Yola's transportation network and addressing congestion challenges at this critical junction.
