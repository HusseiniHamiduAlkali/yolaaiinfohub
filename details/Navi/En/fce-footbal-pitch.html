About FCE Yola Football Pitch

The Federal College of Education (FCE) Yola Football Pitch is a college football field located on the FCE Yola campus. The pitch serves as the primary venue for football activities within the college, hosting student matches, intramural competitions, and various sports events. It is an essential facility for developing athletic talent among students.
