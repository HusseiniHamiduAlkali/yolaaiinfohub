About Wetlands Park

Wetlands Park and Gardens is a beautiful natural park in Yola offering scenic landscapes, diverse flora and fauna, and peaceful recreational spaces. It's an ideal destination for nature lovers and families seeking outdoor relaxation.

  • Walking Trails and Pathways
  • Gardens and Landscaping

  • Photography Spots
  • Picnic Areas
  • Rest Facilities

  • Family Recreation
