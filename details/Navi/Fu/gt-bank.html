Saakaa GT Bank Yola

Guarantee Trust (GT) Bank Yola woni institusiyoona banki layaa bayannde joom-joomre banki guddina limmecooje, laaji-jamirooje, aski corporasiyoona Adamawa State. A sanla banki guddina Naaje Ginnde, GT Bank ina gaatu yamre daangal ummuummaaje, joom-joomre banki addaje, aski bayannde excellence joom-joomre banki.
