Marwa Shawarma Grills

Duuba njammere ngam shawarma e njaaji sudde mawnde

Baɗe Duuba

Marwa Shawarma Grills gida duuba njammere e Yola, njammere ngam shawarma ɓesnde e njaaji sudde mawnde. Duuba ndee ina falmeeri mawnde Araab e njaaji sudde mawnde jalnde.

Menu Njammereɓe

  • Chicken shawarma
  • Beef shawarma e lamb shawarma
  • Njaaji sudde duuɓe
  • Njaaji sudde jalkuɗe
  • Shawarma e roti gollooji
  • Salad feeɓiire e jallieɓe

Njaaji Sudde Ŋaaynaade

Jallbaɓe njammere ndee ina cariti gidinoore e njaaji sudde mawnde, kulli-kullee ɓesnde, e falmeeri sudde feeɓiire ndiyam ceɓɓe.

Humpito Gida

Lokkol: Yola, Adamawa State

Laybde: Njaaji Sudde, Shawarma, Duuba Njeewal; Saɗi: Fuɓaɓol Njeewal
