OASIS Bakery, Yola

Bakerie lokal waɗi buroda, pastry, e jalbaa cake feeɓiire

Baɗe OASIS Bakery

OASIS Bakery gida bakerie lokal e Yola, njammere ngam buroda jalnde, pastry ɓesnde, e cake feeɓiire. Bakerie ndee ina laybde gida taggal e duuɓe gidinoore e falmeeri lokal.

Bakerie Njulle

  • Buroda feeɓiire jalnde
  • Pastry e croissant yeeriiɓe
  • Cake feeɓiire ngam taggal duuɓe
  • Donuts e jallieɓe mawnde
  • Bakerie duuba duuɓe
  • Njulle taggal e jambare duuɓe

Jalbaa Duuɓe

OASIS Bakery waɗi jalbaa duuɓe ngam jecruma, weddings, e taggal duuɓe. Jallbaɓe bakerie waɗi cake duuɓe e pastry personalized ngam gidinoore.

Humpito Gida

Lokkol: Yola, Adamawa State

Laybde: Bakerie Jalnde, Cake Duuɓe, Jalbaa Duuɓe

Saɗi: Fuɓaɓol Njeewal
