Adamawa Sunshine Motor Park

Tereminaal Gaccol Jannde Addaje

Saakaa Adamawa Sunshine Motor Park

Adamawa Sunshine Motor Park woni tereminaal gaccol jannde addaje ina bayannde yelitere addaje gaccol mobine. Ina woni junction-junction guddina ina caaruu joofan baa dumaaje gaccol Naaje Ginnde ndeen ngal jabaral addaje aski caggal-caggal.

Joom-Joomre Ina Bata

  • Joom-Joomre Gaccol Mobine Addaje
  • Caggal-Caggal Coach Addaje
  • Tereminaal Maangal Aski Jamayngal
  • Jammecooje Jubbeere
  • Gaccol Waate-Waate Addaaje
  • Sahaya Amannga Aski Amannga
  • Gadi-Gadi Bayel-Bayel

Lekkaaje Ina Caaruu

  • Jannde Kodal
  • Jannde Fara
  • Jannde Masin
  • Jannde Jommere
  • Gida-Gidooje Guddina Naaje Ginnde

Joom-Joomre Kuutooral

Lekka: Yola, Adamawa State

Diga-ma Adamawa Sunshine Motor Park yamre joom-joomre gaccol mobine addaje layaa.
