Saakaa Access Bank Yola

Reshen Access Bank Yola woni banki jaddi joom-joom ina bayannde bata joom-joomre dijital aski aslitaade. A sanla jam jam mbaalooje maa nyummaa nibbude hakkonde annde banki, Access Bank ina daale joom-joomre addaje ngal feewde aski aslitaade payde Adamawa, ina sattiinde limmecooje, SMEs, aski laaji-jamirooje diga joom-joomre tawi banki.
