Mevish Cafe

Cafe gidinoore ngam kofe, duuba gondale, e buroda feeɓiire

Baɗe Mevish Cafe

Mevish Cafe gida cafe gidinoore e Yola, duuɓe ngam kofe jinnde e nder jeegom. Cafe ndee ina njammere kofe duuɓe e buroda feeɓiire jalnde.

Njammereɓe Cafe

  • Kofe duuɓe e espresso
  • Buroda feeɓiire jalnde
  • Duuba njeewal e gellina
  • Dege e duuba mawnde
  • Cake feeɓiire e jallieɓe
  • Jiddal wuute fuɓaɓol

Jeegom

Mevish Cafe ina jeegom mawnde e gidinoore, duuɓe ngam jalbaa, jabbindu, ko jeegom gondale. Soreteeri gida waɗi dawal njulle gidinoore.

Humpito Gida

Lokkol: Yola, Adamawa State

Laybde: Kofe Shop, Buroda, Duuba Gondale

Saɗi: Fuɓaɓol Njeewal
