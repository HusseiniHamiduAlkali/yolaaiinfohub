Chicken Cottage Yola

Gaccol mbuuti ina batuaje duujaaje kuki aski duujaaje mooyde

Saakaa Chicken Cottage Yola

Chicken Cottage Yola woni gaccol mbuuti ina batuaje duujaaje kuki addaaje aski duujaaje mooyde. Gaccol ina bata yaaje mooyde yamre jammecooje caggal-caggal sadde jaŋtugal.

Taggal Speciaal

  • Kuki faji ngal tawi addaaje
  • Duujaaje kuki aski rotis kuki
  • Sandwich kuki aski kaare
  • Raaya: Patata faji, coleslaw, rice
  • Aryumaaje gujjum aski aryumaaje
  • Taggal kombinee

Yaaje Mooyde

Chicken Cottage Yola bayannde yaaje mooyde aski jabaral addaje. Waawde mbuuti mooyde hesere hesere hesere yaaje, ina jahude yaaje mooyde aski jarki addaaje.
