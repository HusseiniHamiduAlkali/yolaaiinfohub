Catholic Secretariat, Yola

Caggal-Caggal Jabaral Diocese Kristiya

Saakaa Catholic Secretariat

Catholic Secretariat Yola woni caggal-caggal jabaral Diocese Kristiya Yola. Woni caggal-caggal gidi guddina yamre caaruu yelitere Kristiya, jaŋtugal pastoral, aski jaŋtugal jaabandiraawal ummuummaaje batuaje Diocese. Caggal-caggal layannde jaŋtugal spiritual aski jabaral ummuummaaje kristiya Adamawa State.

Caggal-Caggal Jabaral

  • Caggal-caggal jabaral Diocese
  • Caaruu yelitere jaŋtugal pastoral batuaje paruwa
  • Jabaral dowlaaje Diocese aski gajje
  • Jabaral Padre ngal gajje
  • Jabaral jilli rekorde aski dokumentasiyoona
  • Caaruu jaŋtugal jaarangol kristiya aski jabaral

Joom-Joomre Pastoral

  • Jabaral jaarangol aski jabaral spiritual
  • Jabaral sakrament-sakrament
  • Joom-joomre ummuummaaje aski ummuummaaje
  • Jabaral aski yelitere liibagal
  • Jabaral jilli rekorde aski dokumentasiyoona
  • Joom-joomre iyye aski sambe

Jaŋtugal Ummuummaaje

  • Katekesimo aski jaarangol kristiya
  • Jaŋtugal jaabandiraawal jaylaaji-jamirooje aski jabaral
  • Joom-joomre gerembu aski mahad
  • Jaŋtugal jaabandiraawal ummuummaaje
  • Joom-joomre sikooli aski taggal beere
  • Jaŋtugal ummuummaaje aski ndeje beere

Caggal-Caggal Layannde

  • Caggal-caggal jabaral yamre jammecooje
  • Caggal-caggal konferans aski jaabandiraawal
  • Gida aski caggal-caggal resiorse
  • Caggal-caggal duba yamre jammecooje aski dawal
  • Caggal-caggal jaarangol
  • Caggal-caggal dawal sahaya dawal

Joom-Joomre Dawal

  • Jabaral jaarangol kristiya aski jabaral
  • Imaamaaje paruwaje kristiya batuaje kodal maa
  • Joom-joomre rekorde aski dokumentasiyoona
  • Jabaral sakrament caggal-caggal
  • Resiorse jaŋtugal aski maatere
  • Joom-joomre caaruu ummuummaaje

Joom-Joomre Kuutooral

Lekka: Yola, Adamawa State

Catholic Secretariat woni caggal-caggal sahaya yamre delli ina waawde jabaral jaarangol kristiya, jabaral jabaral, hesere ummuummaaje. Dawal daajire ina waawde yamre ndeje yelitere kristiya aski jaŋtugal Diocese.
