Keke Napep

Jabbindu moota gida ngam hakkunde e gondale gooto

Baɗe Keke Napep

Keke napep gida mootaa ndee jabbindu moota gooto mooto e Yola. Hay tawa goɗe hakkunde kam jabbindu, anndude keewol gogol, kam ndiyam gondale duuɓe e ngondal mawnde. Maa hay gida jabbindu moota duuɓe e ngondal.

Cariitinaange Jabbindu

  • Jabbindu duuɓe gondale gooto
  • Yiteende takaagal
  • Gida jabbindu e njeewal
  • Gondale moɓɓe yeeriiɓe
  • Dawal e nder sarreen ngoo
  • Dawal e nder gida alla

Gondale Yeeriiɓe

  • Jimeta ngam Yola Town
  • Motor park ngam ndiyam gida
  • Markeejo e ndiyam gollooji
  • Hotele e ndiyam haande
  • Sukkaagal e ndiyam seɓɓe

Humpito Gida

Lokkol: E gondale alla Yola, Adamawa State

Keke napep gida mootaa waɗi nder kayu gondale duuɓe e ngondal. Hay waɗi keno gondale keewol e kam jabbindu njeewal.
