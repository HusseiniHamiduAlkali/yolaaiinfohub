Wetlands Park

Ogige mmiri na ebe nchebe gburugburu

Banyere Wetlands Park

Wetlands Park bụ ebe nchekwa mmiri na ebe ntụrụndụ nke na-echekwa gburugburu na ụdị anụ ọhịa. Ọ bara uru maka nlegharị anya na mmụta banyere gburugburu ebe obibi.

Atụmatụ

  • Ụlọ nlegharị anya na ntụgharị
  • Mmemme mmụta gburugburu ebe obibi
  • Ụzọ ije na ebe nnọchi anya

Ozi Kọntaktị

Ebe: Yola, Steeti Adamawa

Nweta mmụta gbasara gburugburu na-anọ na Wetlands Park.
