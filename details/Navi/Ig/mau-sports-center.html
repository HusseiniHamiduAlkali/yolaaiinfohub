Banyere MAU Yola Sports Center

MAU (Modibbo Adama University of Technology) Yola Sports Center bụ ebe egwuregwu mahadum nwere akụrụngwa oge a maka egwuregwu dị iche iche. Dị na campus MAUTH, ebe egwuregwu a na-eje ozi ụmụ akwụkwọ, ndị ọrụ, na obodo sara mbara.

  • Ụlọ basketball
  • Ụlọ volleyball
  • Ụlọ tennis
  • Ụlọ football/soccer
  • Track na field stadium
  • Indoor sports hall

Mmemme na Mmụta

  • Asọmpi interuniversity
  • Mmemme ntorobịa na ọmụmụ
  • Nkuzi na mmepe egwuregwu

Ozi Kọntaktị

Ebe: Campus MAUTH, Yola, Steeti Adamawa

MAU Sports Center nabata ndị egwuregwu, ndị hụrụ ahụike n'anya, na ndị na-akwado egwuregwu. Kpọtụrụ njikwa maka ndebanye, ọzụzụ, na ịgbazite ebe.
