Jabbama Jippu Jam (Yola Toll Gate)

Mkpụrụ ụzọ mbata akụkọ ihe mere eme nke Yola

Banyere Jippu Jam Toll Gate

Jabbama Jippu Jam bụ ụzọ mbata akụkọ ihe mere eme na Yola nke na-eje ozi dị ka ụzọ isi banye n'obodo maka njem na ahia. Ọ bụ akara ama ama nke obodo.

Atụmatụ

  • Bọtụ ịnakọta ụgwọ ụdị ụgbọala
  • Ụzọ dị iche iche maka ụgbọala
  • Nlekọta nchekwa na achịkwa okporo ụzọ

Ozi Kọntaktị

Ebe: Nzughachi Mbata Yola, Adamawa State

Contact toll gate management for inquiries and assistance.
