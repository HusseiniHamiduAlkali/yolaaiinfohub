About Agga Islamic Center

Agga Islamic Center na important Islamic education and worship place wey dey serve Muslim community for Yola. Dem dey do Quran lessons, Arabic classes and community work.

Educational Programs

  • Quran recitation classes for all ages
  • Fiqh (Islamic law) study groups
  • Arabic language courses
  • Islamic history programs
  • Hadith study circles
  • Youth Islamic education

Worship Facilities

  • Prayer halls for daily prayers
  • Separate prayer areas for men and women
  • Clean ablution facilities
  • Big courtyard for overflow prayers
  • Carpeted prayer areas
  • Climate-controlled halls

Community Services

  • Islamic counseling
  • Community outreach programs
  • Social welfare initiatives
  • Marriage and family counseling
  • Youth development programs
  • Interfaith dialogue activities

Visitor Information

  • Open for daily prayers
  • Visitors welcome anytime
  • Respectful dress codes appreciated
  • Free ablution facilities
  • Parking available
  • Refreshment areas for visitors

Contact Information
