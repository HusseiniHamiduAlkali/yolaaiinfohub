About Keke Napep

Keke Napep na common local transport for Yola wey dey flexible for short distance travel. Dem quick and cheap for inside town movement.

Services

  • Door-to-door pick-up
  • Flexible routes
  • Affordably priced fares

Safety Tips

  • Negotiate fare before boarding
  • Use well-lit pickup points at night
  • Share rides where possible
