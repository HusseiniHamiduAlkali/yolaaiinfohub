Catholic Secretariat, Yola

Administrative center for the Catholic Diocese

About Catholic Secretariat

The Catholic Secretariat na the administrative headquarter for Catholic Diocese for Yola. Dem dey coordinate religious activities, pastoral services and community programs.

Administrative Functions

  • Diocesan administration
  • Coordinate pastoral activities
  • Manage diocesan finances
  • Oversee clergy assignments
  • Handle marriage records
  • Coordinate religious education

Pastoral Services

  • Spiritual guidance and counseling
  • Sacrament administration
  • Marriage and family services
  • Burial and funeral arrangements
  • Baptism records
  • Confession and reconciliation

Community Programs

  • Catechism and religious education
  • Youth development programs
  • Women empowerment activities
  • Social welfare and charity
  • Healthcare support
  • Scholarship programs

Contact Information

Location: Yola, Adamawa State

The Secretariat dey open for people wey need Catholic spiritual guidance or administrative help.
