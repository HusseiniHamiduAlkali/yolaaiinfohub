Game da Adamawa Waste To Wealth Recyclers

Adamawa Waste To Wealth Recyclers Limited wani kamfani ne mai sabon salo wanda ke haɗa ayyukan sake amfani da sharar gida na masana'antu da na kasuwanci tare da ayyukan muhalli masu tsabta. Ƙungiyar ta himmantu wajen mayar da sharar gida zuwa damar tattalin arziki yayin kiyaye muhalli.

Ayyukan Tsakiya

  • Sake amfani da sharar masana'antu: Sarrafa sharar daga wuraren masana'antu
  • Gudanar da sharar kasuwanci: Mafita ga kasuwanci da ofisoshi
  • Maido da kayayyaki: Cire kayayyaki masu ƙima daga rafukan sharar
  • Rarraba da rarraba sharar: Rarraba kayayyaki masu sake amfani da su
  • Gudunyar da kayayyakin inganci: Shirya kayayyakin da aka maido don sake siyarwa

Kayayyakin da aka sarrafa

  • Filastik da kayayyakin polythene
  • Karafa da guntun karafa
  • Takarda da kwali
  • Gilashi da yumbu
  • Sashen sharar lantarki

Damar Tattalin Arziki

  • Damar aiki a fannin gudanar da sharar
  • Lalacewa mai adalci ga kayayyakin sharar da aka tattara
  • Shirye-shiryen haɓaka ƙwarewa ga ma'aikata
  • Kasuwa ga kayayyakin sake amfani da aka maido
  • Taimako ga 'yan kasuwa na gida

Himma ga Muhalli

  • Rage sharar da ake zuba a wuraren sharar sosai
  • Rage gurbatar muhalli
  • Gudanar da albarkatu masu dorewa
  • Shirye-shiryen rage alamun carbon
  • Shirye-shiryen ilimin muhalli na al'umma
