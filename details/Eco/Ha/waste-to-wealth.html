Shirin 'Shara Zuwa Albarkatun' na Yola

Maida Sharar Zuwa Damar Tattalin Arziki

Game da Shirin

Shirin 'Shara Zuwa Albarkatun' na Yola wani sabis ne mai sababbin hanyoyi wanda ke juya kayan sharar zuwa albarkatu masu ƙima yayin samar da damar tattalin arziki ga jama'a. Shirin yana nuna yadda sharar da aka zama matsanancin batutuwa, zai iya zama tushen kuɗi da inganta rayuwa.

Manufofin Shirin

  • Juya sharar zuwa ƙima ta tattalin arziki
  • Samar da damar kuɗi ga jama'a
  • Rage gurbatar muhalli
  • Inganta kasuwancin dorewa
  • Ƙarfafa jama'oyin masu wahala

Tsarin Samar da Ƙima

  • Tattarawa: Tara sharar cikin tsari
  • Rarraba: Rarrabe kayan bisa nau'i da inganci
  • Sarrafa: Sauya sharar zuwa kayan matsakaici
  • Ƙirƙira: Samar da kayayyaki na gama don kasuwa
  • Siyarwa: Hadin kai tsakanin kayayyaki da masu siyan

Kayayyakin da aka Ƙirƙira

  • Kayan filastik da aka sake amfani (kwasan, jakunkuna, kwantena)
  • Abubuwan ƙirƙira daga guntun kayan
  • Kayan gine-gine daga sharar
  • Abinci don dabbobi daga sharar halittu
  • Maida da compost daga kayan halittu

Fa'idodin Masu Shiga

  • Kuɗi kai-tsaye daga tattara sharar
  • Ayyuka a sarrafa da ƙirƙira
  • Horar da ƙwarewa da gina ƙwarewa
  • Samun kasuwa ga kayayyaki
  • Taimakon al'umma da sadarwa

Tasirin Muhalli

  • Rage sharar zuwa wuraren zubar da shara
  • Rage gurbatar muhalli
  • Kare albarkatu ta sake amfani
  • Rage buƙatan albarkatun sabbin
  • Tallafawa ka'idojin tattalin arziki mai zagaye

Nasarorin Shirin da aka Samu

  • Samar da kuɗi ga mutane 500+
  • Naɗa ton 1,000+ na sharar daga wuraren zubar da shara
  • Samar da ayyuka 50+ masu dorewa
  • Samar da kayayyaki 10,000+ da aka gama
  • Gina kasuwancin tushen jama'a mai ƙarfi
