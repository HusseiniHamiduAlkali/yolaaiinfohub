Benue Valley Reserve

Yankin Tsaro na Yanayi tare da Shirye-shiryen Kiyaye Bambancin Halittu

Game da Benue Valley Reserve

Benue Valley Reserve wani yanki ne na tsaro na yanayi da aka keɓe don kiyaye bambancin halittu da kiyaye muhalli. Yana a gefen kwarin Benue, yankin yana ƙunshe da muhallin yanayi daban-daban kuma yana zama mazaunin tsire-tsire da dabbobi da yawa yayin bayar da damar ilimi ga baƙi.

Maƙasudin Kiyaye

  • Kiyaye nau'ikan tsire-tsire da dabbobi na asali
  • Kiyaye muhallin yanayi na asali
  • Saka idanu kan lafiyar bambancin halittu da mazauni
  • Shigar da al'umma cikin kiyaye
  • Taimaka ilimin muhalli
  • Inganta yawon shakatawa na muhalli mai dorewa

Muhallin Yanayi da Mazauni

  • Dajin Kwarin: A gefen Kwarin Benue
  • Dajin Savanna: Yankunan ciyayi buɗe
  • Wetlands na Kwarin: Yankunan fadama da swamps
  • Ruwa Buɗe: Magudanan kwarin da tabki

Nau'ikan Dabbobi da aka Kiyaye

  • Manyan Dabbobi: Buffalo, antelope, dabbobi masu ƙafa daban-daban
  • Primates: Birai da sauran nau'ikan primates
  • Tsuntsaye: 200+ nau'ikan tsuntsaye da aka rubuta
  • Masarautu da Amphibians: Bambancin halittun masarautu
  • Nau'ikan Kifi: Kifi na asali na ruwa mai tsabta

Shirye-shiryen Kiyaye

  • Saka idanu kan nau'ikan: Binciken dabbobi na yau da kullum
  • Gudanar da Mazauni: Ayyukan maido da muhalli
  • Patrols na Anti-Poaching: Kiyaye daga farauta mara doka
  • Ayyukan Bincike: Nazarin muhalli da rubutu
  • Shirye-shiryen Al'umma: Shigar da gida cikin kiyaye

Ayyukan Baƙi

  • Tafiye-tafiyen Yanayi masu Jagora: Tafiye masu jagora na ƙwararru
  • Kallo Tsuntsaye: Kallo na ornithological
  • Ɗaukar Hoto: Ɗaukar hoton dabbobi da yanayi
  • Shirye-shiryen Ilimi: Ziyarar makaranta da ƙungiyoyi
  • Samun Bincike: Ga masu bincike masu izini

Kididdigar Bambancin Halittu

  • Sama da 200 nau'ikan tsuntsaye
  • 50+ nau'ikan dabbobi
  • Nau'ikan masarautu da amphibians da yawa
  • Yawan kifin a tsarin kwaraye
  • Nau'ikan tsire-tsire da ciyayi daban-daban

Bayanin Baƙi

  • Wuri: Kwarin Benue Valley, kusa da Yola
  • Shiga: Ta hanyar tafiye masu jagora kawai
  • Lokacin Mafi Kyau: Lokacin bushewa (Nuwamba - Maris)
  • Izin Shiga: Ana buƙata ga duk baƙi
  • Aikin Jagora: Ana bayar da jagora na ƙwararru
