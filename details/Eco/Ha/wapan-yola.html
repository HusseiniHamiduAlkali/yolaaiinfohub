Ƙungiyar Masu Tattara Sharar Najeriya (WAPAN), Yola

Ƙungiyar Tushen Jama'a na Kare Muhalli

Game da WAPAN

Ƙungiyar Masu Tattara Sharar Najeriya (WAPAN) ƙungiya ce na tushen jama'a da ke aiki a kare muhalli da wayar da kai a Yola. WAPAN tana wakiltar masu tattara shara da masu tara sharar mara tsari, tana samar da goyon bayan, fadakarwa, da damar ci gaba ga membobi.

Kilancin Ƙungiyar

  • Fadakarwa ne game da hakkin masu tattara shara da yanayin aiki
  • Kare muhalli ta hanyar gudanar da sharar
  • Wayar da kan al'umma game da batutuwan sharar
  • Samar da kuɗi ga masu tattara shara
  • Tallafawa tsarin na doka ga sashin sharar mara tsari

Ayyukan Goyon Bayan ga Membobi

  • Shirye-shiryen Horo: Haɓaka ƙwarewa a gudanar da sharar
  • Hadin Gwiwar Kasuwa: Hadin kai zuwa masu siyan kayan da aka tattara
  • Kayan Tsaro: Samar da kayan kariya na jiki
  • Taimakon Lafiya: Ayyukan lafiya da jama'a
  • Fadakarwa: Wakilta bukata na membobi

Alhakin Muhalli

  • Tattara da sarrafa kayan sharar masu ƙima
  • Rage nauyin wuraren zubar da shara
  • Samar da ayyukan sake amfani ga al'umma
  • Wayar da kan al'umma game da gudanar da sharar
  • Tallafawa ci gaban tattalin arziki mai zagaye

Tasirin Tattalin Arziki da Jama'a

  • Samar da kuɗi ga ɗaruruwan masu tattara shara
  • Inganta yanayin aiki a sashin sharar
  • Samar da damar aiki ga ƙungiyoyi marasa karfi
  • Taimaka ga rayin iyali
  • Tallafawa rage talauci
