Shirin Kiyaye Muhalli na Jama'a, Yola

Shirin Tushen Jama'a don Kare Muhalli da Ayyukan Dorewa

Game da Shirin

Shirin Kiyaye Muhalli na Jama'a wani shiri ne na tushen jama'a da ke shigar da al'umma cikin ƙoƙarin kiyaye muhalli da ayyukan dorewa a Yola. Shirin yana ƙarfafa al'umma don mallakar muhallinsu da aiwatar da mafita na kiyaye.

Ka'idodi na Tsakiya

  • Yanke shawara ta al'umma
  • Ƙarfafawa ta hanyar shiga
  • Gudanar da albarkatu mai dorewa
  • Haɗa ilimin gargajiya
  • Kiyaye muhalli na dogon lokaci

Ayyukan Kiyaye

  • Maido da Mazauni: Kare da maido da muhallin yanayi
  • Gonakin Jama'a: Noman birni don tsaron abinci
  • Gudanar da Sharar: Shirye-shiryen rage sharar na jama'a
  • Kare Ruwan: Kare tushoshin ruwa
  • Kare Dabbobi: Kiyaye dabbobin gida

Shigar da Jama'a

  • Taro na jama'a na yau da kullum da tsare-tsare
  • Ilimin muhalli da horo
  • Shigar da matasa cikin kiyaye
  • Shirye-shiryen ƙarfafa mata
  • Taimako ga kasuwancin jama'a

Ayyukan Dorewa da aka Inganta

  • Noma ta kwayoyin halitta da noma mai dorewa
  • Kare ruwa da tara
  • Karɓar makamashi mai sabuntawa
  • Rage sharar da sake amfani
  • Agroforestry da dasa itace

Sakamakon Shirin

  • Shigar da membobin jama'a 1,500+
  • Maido da hekta 50 na mazauni
  • Kafa gonakin jama'a 10
  • Horar da mutane 200+ a ayyukan dorewa
  • Inganta rayuwar jama'a

Fa'idodi ga Jama'a

  • Inganta ingancin muhalli
  • Tsaron abinci ta hanyar gonakin jama'a
  • Samun kuɗi daga ayyukan kiyaye
  • Inganta lafiya da walwala
  • Ƙarfafa haɗin gwiwa da haɗin kai na jama'a
