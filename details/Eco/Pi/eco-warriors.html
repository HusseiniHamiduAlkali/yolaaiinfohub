Green Environment Solutions Initiative, Yola

Community Organization Wey Dey Fight To Protect Di Ground

About GESOL

Green Environment Solutions Initiative (GESOL) na community organization wey work hard make di ground better for Yola. Di organization focus on ground protection wey people do together, teach people about environment, and make sure everybody do thing wey go make future good.

Di Purpose and Di Dream

Di Purpose: Make ground better and keep am fresh through di work wey people do together, teach about environment, and make sure everybody do better thing.

Di Dream: Yola where everybody go say "di ground na wetin we must protect" and green practice go be normal for everyday life.

Di Main Work Dem Do

  • Teach About Environment: Go tell community people about di ground problem and solution
  • Plant Tree: Plant tree for Yola to make ground green again
  • Take Care Di Throw Out: Help community reduce throw out and recycle am
  • Save Water: Make program to protect di water wey people use
  • Make Green Space: Build and keep garden for community

Community Get Involved

  • Every month dem go clean di place together
  • Dem go teach people how to live way wey no spoil di ground
  • Dem go teach small pikin for school about environment
  • Youth go get club where dem all talk about environment
  • Anybody wey like can come help for free

What Dem Don Accomplish

  • Plant more than 10,000 tree for Yola
  • Organize more than 50 place cleaning event
  • Teach more than 500 people how to take care throw out
  • Build 5 garden for community
  • Get more than 1,000 community people for di program dem
