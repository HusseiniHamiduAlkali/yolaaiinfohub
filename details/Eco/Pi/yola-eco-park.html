Yola Eco Park

Sustainable Park With Local Plant and Environmental Display

About Yola Eco Park

Yola Eco Park na sustainable park wey feature local plant and environmental display. Di park work as place for people relax and also place for learn about environment. E show how do good practice and teach people say di ground important.

Di Park Wey Dem Have

  • Local Plant Garden: Show di tree and plant wey come from Yola area
  • Butterfly and Bee Area: Support different animal stay alive
  • Walk Path: Nice path for person walk around and explore
  • Learn Display: Interactive thing to understand di nature and animal
  • Picnic Area: Place for family sit down and eat
  • Bird Look Area: Place to watch bird and other animal

Local Plant Wey Di Park Feature

  • Neem tree (Azadirachta indica)
  • Baobab tree (Adansonia digitata)
  • Acacia tree type
  • Local wild plant and grass
  • Local medicine plant and small tree

Learn Program

  • Expert Walk: Person wey know nature show you around and explain
  • School Program: Teach small pikin about environment and nature science
  • Teach Session: Teach how live di way wey no spoil ground
  • Animal Watch: Track di different animal wey live for di park
  • Learn Display: Thing wey you touch and learn from at di park

Good Practice Dem Do

  • Collect di rain water and use am
  • Use natural design for di plant
  • No use poison chemical
  • Take waste from plant and make dirt
  • Use power wey efficient

Di Thing For Visitor

  • Walk path and ride bicycle path
  • Picnic and relax place
  • Good bathroom place
  • Information center
  • Parking place for car
  • Small place to buy food and drink
