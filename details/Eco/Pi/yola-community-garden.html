Yola Community Garden

Public Garden Place For Community Farm and Learn About Environment

About Yola Community Garden

Yola Community Garden na public garden place wey help community people farm and learn about environment. Di garden give place for people grow food dem sef, learn how farm di good way, and know other people for community while make di place better.

Di Garden Wey Dem Have

  • Vegetable Patch: Individual place for community people grow vegetable
  • Herb Area: Plant wey you use for medicine and cooking
  • Fruit Tree: Mango, cashew, and orange tree
  • Dirt Make Place: Reduce throw out and make ground good
  • Learn Place: Where dem teach about farm and other good thing

How You Can Join

  • Rent Small Place: Get small garden place for your sef
  • Come Help: Help keep di public place for free
  • Learn New Skill: Learn how farm di good and sustainable way
  • Join Event: Join activity wey community do for di garden
  • Give Money: Help di garden improve di place

Learn Program

  • Farm Di Good Way: Teach about how farm di good and natural way
  • Make Dirt: Learn how manage throw out and make ground good
  • Save Water: Learn how use water di smart way
  • Good Food: Learn about good food and how e keep you healthy
  • Science of Nature: Learn about how nature work from real life

Di Rule Dem Have For Di Garden

  • No use poison chemical - use natural way only
  • Take care di water and tool wey everybody share
  • Keep your place and public place clean clean
  • Come help keep place when dem organize di day
  • Share di extra food you grow with community

Good Thing Di Garden Do For Community

  • Make fresh cheap food for people
  • Make people health better
  • Make community people know each other well
  • Teach people about environment and nature
  • Make green place for people sit and relax
  • Make people mental health become better

Member Information

  • Plot Size: 20m² to 40m² for pick
  • Yearly Money: Affordable price to join
  • Season: Farm whole year
  • Tool: Dem give tool for everybody to share
  • Water: Water system for farm
