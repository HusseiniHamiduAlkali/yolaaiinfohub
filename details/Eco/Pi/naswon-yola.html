About NASWON

National Association of Scrap and Waste Picker Nigeria (NASWON) work hard make city clean clean and better development for tomorrow. Di organization represent people wey pick scrap and waste, fight for dem to get better work, protect di ground, and help community grow.

Di Main Purpose

  • Make city keep clean and fresh fresh
  • Make development wey go last forever
  • Fight for waste picker people to get better life
  • Help waste picker people become formal business
  • Work for protect di ground

Clean City Work Dem Do

  • Collect Waste: Dem clean street and place regular time
  • Get Valuable Thing: Dem collect good thing from di waste
  • Community Work: Dem tell community say dem too must help keep place clean
  • Teach Public: Dem teach how throw waste di proper way
  • Keep Infrastructure Good: Dem make sure public place stay clean

Better Development Work

  • Bring waste picker people into di official waste system
  • Build proper place for recycle work
  • Teach people new skill wey go help dem
  • Help people start green business
  • Make plan to protect di ground

Good Thing For Member

  • Dem fight for your right and support you
  • You get access for new skill learning
  • You get connection to person wey go buy wetin you collect
  • Dem help you if anything happen
  • You get to know other people wey work like you
