Yola Tree Plant Project

Make Green Activity Normal For Yola

About Yola Tree Plant Project

Yola Tree Plant Project na organization wey work hard make people understand say green practice good good through plant tree for big number and make Yola more green. Dem work make Yola get plenty tree cover, fight climate change wey make ground hot, and make di air fresh.

Di Purpose of Di Project

  • Plant 50,000 tree for Yola for 5 year
  • Make forest cover and green place grow
  • Fight climate change and bad air
  • Make place where animal dem can live
  • Get community people do ground work

Kind of Tree Dem Plant

  • Fruit Tree: Mango, cashew, guava for eat
  • Wood Tree: Teak, iroko for wood business
  • Tree For Di Place: Local tree for make animal happy
  • Nice Tree: Tree wey make Yola pretty and clean di air

Di Work Dem Do

  • Community Plant Event: Every month dem go plant tree together together
  • Learn How Keep Tree: Dem teach people how keep tree alive
  • Grow Small Tree First: Dem make small tree for give away
  • Teach Small Pikin: Dem teach young people about environment
  • Make City Green: Dem plant tree for street and public place

Di Good From Tree

  • Make di air better and clean
  • Make hot place cool down
  • Stop water run away and dirt go place
  • Make green space for people sit and relax
  • Make place where animal can live
  • Help save di world from hot hot

What Dem Accomplish

  • Plant 15,000 tree since dem start
  • Get more than 2,000 community people join
  • Teach 300 person how keep tree
  • Make small tree farm make 5,000 small tree every year
  • Make green place bigger by 10 percent for where dem work

How You Can Join
