About Plastic Waste Collector Yola

Plastic Waste Collector Yola na special organization wey work hard collect plastic waste di whole Yola and recycle am. Dem focus make sure plastic no spoil di ground by make am easy for people give plastic waste and dem handle am proper.

Di Work Dem Offer

  • Collect Plastic: Dem go your house or business and collect di plastic waste
  • Sort Plastic: Dem put different plastic apart (PET, HDPE, PVC and all)
  • Recycle Work: Dem take di plastic wey dem collect and make am new again
  • Factory Plastic Waste: Dem collect plastic waste from big business and factory
  • Teach About Plastic: Dem teach people how reduce plastic waste

Kind of Plastic Dem Accept

  • PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate) - bottle and container
  • HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) - bag and container
  • PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) - pipe and connection piece
  • LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene) - film and bag
  • PP (Polypropylene) - motor car part and container
  • PS (Polystyrene) - foam package and throw away thing

Di Good and Good Thing

  • Stop plastic from spoil di ground
  • Make material wey people go use make new plastic product
  • Create work for people wey know waste work
  • Help make di whole circle business work proper
  • Stop so much pressure on nature

Di Price and How Dem Collect

  • House Collection: Dem come for free if you get 5kg and more
  • Big Order Price: Dem give better price for business amount
  • How Often: Dem collect one time every week
  • Minimum: You need 2kg for dem come
