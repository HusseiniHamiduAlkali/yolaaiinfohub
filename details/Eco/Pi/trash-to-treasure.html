'Throw Out To Gold' Initiative, Yola

Make Art and Good Thing From Old Throw Out

About Throw Out To Gold

'Throw Out To Gold' Initiative na creative work wey take di throw out wey people no want no more and use brain make am art and something useful while teach people say environment important. Di program show say if you think smart, you can make throw out thing become something nice and something people go use.

Di Purpose of Di Initiative

  • Take throw out and make am art
  • Make people think creative way
  • Teach people say environment important
  • Make work for people wey know creative work
  • Help people buy thing wey no spoil ground

Product and Creative Thing Dem Make

  • Big Art: Dem make big big art from throw out for public place
  • Thing You Use: Furniture and thing for house from recycle material
  • Fashion: Bag, jewelry, cloth from recycle material
  • Nice Thing: Art and thing to put for house
  • School Material: Thing for teach small pikin for school

Di Work Dem Do

  • Teach Session: Dem teach how make thing from throw out
  • Community Project: People work together make art
  • Show Di Work: Dem show di product dem wey people done make
  • Teach Young Person: Dem teach young people about environment
  • Sell Di Product: Dem go to market and sell di thing dem

Material Dem Use

  • Plastic bottle and container
  • Cloth piece and old cloth
  • Metal and wire piece
  • Paper and cardboard
  • Old thing from house wey no use again

Di Good For Ground Awareness

  • Show say you can reduce throw out
  • Change how people think about throw out - say e valuable
  • Make people want buy thing wey no spoil ground
  • Get community people understand environment matter
  • Show creative way to solve ground problem

Good Thing For Community

  • Create work for artist and people wey know craft
  • Get money from sell di product dem
  • Learn new skill wey go help for life
  • Make community culture and art grow
  • Make people understand environment important
