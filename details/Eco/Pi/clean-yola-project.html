National Environmental Standards and Regulations Enforcement Agency (NESREA), Yola

Authority Wey Dey Ensure Environmental Compliance and Enforcement

About NESREA Yola

National Environmental Standards and Regulations Enforcement Agency (NESREA) na Federal regulatory body wey get presence for Yola, wey focus on city cleanliness and sustainable urban development. NESREA dey work to ensure compliance with environmental standards and regulations while e dey promote sustainable practices.

Mandate and Objectives

  • Enforce environmental standards and regulations
  • Promote sustainable urban development
  • Ensure city cleanliness and sanitation
  • Monitor environmental compliance
  • Educate the public on environmental responsibility

Key Services

  • Environmental Inspections: Regular monitoring of businesses and facilities
  • Compliance Verification: Ensuring adherence to environmental standards
  • Waste Management Oversight: Regulating proper waste disposal
  • Air and Water Quality Monitoring: Testing and reporting on environmental quality
  • Environmental Education: Public awareness and training programs

Cleanliness Initiatives

  • Regular street cleaning and sanitation programs
  • Public space maintenance
  • Market and commercial area regulation
  • Enforcement of waste disposal rules
  • Community sanitation awareness campaigns

Urban Development Focus

  • Green space development and maintenance
  • Sustainable city planning participation
  • Environmental impact assessments for projects
  • Integration of green technologies
  • Climate action initiatives
