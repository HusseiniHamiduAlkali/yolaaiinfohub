Yola 'Throw Out To Rich' Initiative

Turn Di Throw Out Into Money and Good Living

About Di Initiative

Yola 'Throw Out To Rich' Initiative na smart program wey take di throw out wey people no want and turn am to something valuable wey go make people get money. Di program show say wetin people throw out no be problem alone - e be opportunity to make better money and better life.

Di Purpose of Di Initiative

  • Turn di throw out to money wey people can use
  • Create opportunity for people to make money
  • Make di ground clean, no more dirty
  • Make business model wey go last forever
  • Give poor people power to do something

How Dem Make Money From Throw Out

  • Collect: Dem go collect all di throw out nicely nice
  • Sort: Dem go put di throw out separate base on wetin e be
  • Work On Am: Dem go change di throw out to something good wey go sell
  • Make New Thing: Dem go make final product wey ready for people buy
  • Sell Am: Dem go find buyer and sell di product dem

Product Dem Make

  • Plastic product wey dem recycle (bucket, bag, container)
  • Nice thing wey dem make from old scrap
  • Building material wey dem make from throw out
  • Animal food from di throw out wey na from plant
  • Soil food and something to make ground good from rotten thing

Good Thing Wey Dem Get

  • Direct money from collect throw out
  • Work for people wey know how to work di throw out
  • Learn new skill wey go help dem for life
  • Find buyer wey go buy di product dem make
  • Community support and people wey work together

Di Good For Di Ground

  • No more throw out go to di ground dump
  • Di ground go become more clean
  • We go recycle material instead make new one
  • No need to bring so much new material from nature
  • Di whole circle of business go work proper

What Dem Don Accomplish

  • Make money for more than 500 people for community
  • Put 1,000 ton throw out away from di big dump
  • Make more than 50 work for people
  • Make more than 10,000 finished product
  • Build strong business for community
