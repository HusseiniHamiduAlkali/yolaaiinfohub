Water Conservation Project

Program For Save Water and Sustainable Water Management

About Di Water Conservation Project

Water Conservation Project work hard make and run program for save water and sustainable water management for Yola. Di project focus on keep water safe, reduce water wastage, and make sure everybody get good water for use.

Di Purpose of Di Project

  • Make community people know say water important to save
  • Reduce water wastage for house and business
  • Protect and make water good again
  • Make water better and safe to use
  • Make sustainable water management way

Conservation Program

  • Water Check: See how much water building use
  • Technology To Save: Put di thing wey save water inside building
  • Teach People: Community teach make people know about water saving
  • Rain Water Collect: Make system to collect and keep rain water
  • Protect Natural Water: Protect di wet place where water come from

Technology To Save Water

  • Faucet wey no use so much water
  • Shower wey no use so much water
  • Toilet wey dey efficient
  • Drip farm system
  • System wey collect rain water
  • System wey reuse di water wey come from shower
