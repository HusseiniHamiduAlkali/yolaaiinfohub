Association of Plastic Recyclers, Yola

Non-Profit Organization Wey Dey Teach People About Environment and Conservation

About the Association

Association of Plastic Recyclers Yola na non-profit organization wey focus on environmental education and conservation, especially for plastic waste management. The organization dey bring together recycling professionals, businesses, and community members to improve plastic recycling practices.

Mission and Goals

  • Promote reduction of plastic waste from the source
  • Improve plastic recycling technology and practices
  • Educate communities about plastic pollution
  • Build sustainable circular economy for plastics
  • Advocate for environmental policies wey dey support

Programs and Activities

  • Recycler Training: Professional development for workers in recycling industry
  • Community Education: Awareness campaigns about plastic waste
  • Industry Collaboration: Networking among recyclers and businesses
  • Research and Innovation: Find new solutions for recycling
  • Policy Advocacy: Promote environmental regulations wey dey support

Membership Benefits

  • Access to training and professional development
  • Networking with other recyclers and businesses
  • Information about market trends and opportunities
  • Representation in industry discussions
  • Support for business development

Environmental Impact

  • Divert thousands of tons of plastic from landfills
  • Train hundreds in professional recycling practices
  • Engage thousands in environmental awareness
  • Establish sustainable business models
  • Contribute to policy improvements
