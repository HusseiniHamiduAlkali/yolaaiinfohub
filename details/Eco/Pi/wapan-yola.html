Waste Picker Association of Nigeria (WAPAN), Yola

Community Organization Wey Work For Protect Di Ground

About WAPAN

Waste Picker Association of Nigeria (WAPAN) na community organization wey work hard make ground better and teach people about environment for Yola. WAPAN represent people wey pick waste, give dem help, fight for di right, and make opportunity for dem improve life.

Organization Focus

  • Fight for waste picker people right and better work place
  • Protect di ground through waste management
  • Teach community about waste matter
  • Make money for waste picker people
  • Help waste picker people become formal business

Help Dem Give To Member

  • Teach New Skill: Help people learn new waste management skill
  • Find Buyer: Connect di member to person wey go buy wetin dem collect
  • Safety Thing: Give equipment to protect di person when dem work
  • Health Help: Give health service and social help
  • Fight For Right: Speak for di member when dem get problem

Di Good For Ground

  • Dem collect and work di valuable waste material
  • Dem reduce throw out wey go to di ground dump
  • Dem give recycle service for community
  • Dem teach about waste and environment matter
  • Dem help make di whole circle business work proper

Economic and Social Good Thing

  • Give money for hundred and hundred waste picker people
  • Make waste picker work place better better
  • Make opportunity for poor people group
  • Help family money survive
  • Help reduce poor people problem
