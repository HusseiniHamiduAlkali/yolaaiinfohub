UNDP Go Green Initiative

Better Development Wey Go Last and Environmental Action Program

About UNDP Go Green Initiative

UNDP Go Green Initiative na big big program wey work make ground better and sustainable wey focus on green practice and better development for Yola and Adamawa State. Di initiative work with community people, government, and organization wey help people make climate action project, promote sun power and alternative power, and make sure ground stay good. Di program follow di United Nations Sustainable Development Goal and help Nigeria fulfill commitment to fight climate change.

Di Main Program Area

  • Climate Action: Make project to reduce bad air and get ready for climate change
  • Alternative Power: Make sun power and other power normal in community
  • Ground Protection: Help protect animal variety and how we use natural thing
  • Green Work: Make work opportunity for people in green business
  • Teach Community: Make people know about ground matter and good practice
  • Fight For Rule: Help make better rule for di ground

Di Project Initiative

  • Sun Power Project: Put sun panel for government building and community
  • Waste Management: Community work wey help reduce and recycle throw out
  • Plant Tree Project: Plant tree and protect forest
  • Water and Cleanness: Make sure people get clean water and good sanitation
  • Farm Better: Make farm way wey work with climate and natural farm
  • Teach About Ground: Teach and build skill for community and young people

Good Thing For Community

  • Make air and water better for community place
  • Make people get clean power for house and business
  • Make work for people in green business and sun power
  • Make more food production through better farm
  • Teach young person and woman new skill
  • Make hospital work cost small because ground become better

How You Can Join

  • Community Participation: Join local organization wey do ground work
  • Learn New Skill: Go to school wey teach environment and green work
  • Come Help: Help dem with tree plant, waste manage, and conservation
  • Business Partner: Partner with di initiative make your business better
  • Support Di Rule: Push dem to make better rule for di ground
