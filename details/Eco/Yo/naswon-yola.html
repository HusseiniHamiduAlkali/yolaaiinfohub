Ilojana Sisagbe Imulẹ Ìlú ati Agbaye Sisagbe

Nipa NASWON

The National Association of Scraps and Waste Pickers of Nigeria (NASWON) jẹ ilojoona ti o lo ara ninu sisagbe imulẹ ìlú ati agbaye sisagbe. Ile ajumọ yii n lo ara lori awon olugbe ẹkó, sisagbe ipo iṣẹ, ati agbaye ile aje ati ijoba.

Awon Ilosiwaju Keyyi

  • Ṣiṣi imulẹ ìlú ati agbaye
  • Sisagbe agbaye ile aje sisagbe
  • Sisagbe ipo ati iju awon olugbe ẹkó
  • Atilẹyin sisagbe imulẹ ẹkó alailẹka
  • Idiwọ ninu asigbe agbaye

Awon Iṣe Imulẹ Ìlú

  • Gbigbe Ẹkó: Gbigbe ìlú ati aaye nilo
  • Gbigbe Ohun Iye: Gbigbe nkan iye lati ara ẹkó
  • Idiwọ Ará Ile: Sisagbe ара ile nipa ijo ẹkó
  • Ikẹkọ Olumọ: Agbaye nipa imulẹ ẹkó
  • Sisagbe Aaye Olumọ: Ṣiṣi imulẹ awon aaye ènìyàn

Awon Ilosiwaju Agbaye Ile Aje

  • Sisagbe olugbe ẹkó sinu imulẹ alailẹka
  • Sisọ ile ero yiyipada
  • Awon iṣẹ ikẹkọ ati sisagbe iṣẹ
  • Atilẹyin para sisagbe ile aje sisagbe
  • Awon ọna asigbe agbaye

Awon Anfani Eyin Alabọde

  • Sisagbe ipo ati atilẹyin ibeere
  • Ṣiṣi awon ọna ikẹkọ ati sisagbe iṣẹ
  • Idiwọ pelu aje nkan iye
  • Atilẹyin iju ati alaibalẹ
  • Idiwọ ará ile ati idiwọ miiran
