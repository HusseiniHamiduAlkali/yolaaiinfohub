National Environmental Standards and Regulations Enforcement Agency (NESREA), Yola

Ụlọ ọrụ nrube isi gburugburu ebe obibi na mmanye iwu

Banyere NESREA Yola

National Environmental Standards and Regulations Enforcement Agency (NESREA) bụ ụlọ ọrụ gọọmentị etiti nwere ọnụnọ na Yola lekwasịrị anya na ịdị ọcha obodo na mmepe obodo na-adịgide adịgide. NESREA na-arụ ọrụ iji hụ na nrube isi na ụkpụrụ gburugburu ebe obibi na iwu ma kwalite omume na-adịgide adịgide.

Ọrụ enyere na ebumnuche

  • Mmanye ụkpụrụ gburugburu ebe obibi na iwu
  • Kwalite mmepe obodo na-adịgide adịgide
  • Hụ na ịdị ọcha obodo na ịdị ọcha
  • Nyochaa nrube isi gburugburu ebe obibi
  • Kuzie ọha na eze banyere ọrụ gburugburu ebe obibi

Ọrụ isi

  • Nnyocha gburugburu ebe obibi: Nnyocha mgbe niile nke azụmahịa na ụlọ ọrụ
  • Nnwale nrube isi: Ịhụ na nrube isi na ụkpụrụ gburugburu ebe obibi
  • Nlekọta njikwa mkpofu: Ịhazi mkpofu kwesịrị ekwesị
  • Nnyocha ikuku na mmiri: Ịnwale na ịkọ akụkọ banyere ikuku gburugburu ebe obibi
  • Nkuzi gburugburu ebe obibi: Mmemme mmata ọha na eze na ọzụzụ

Mmemme ịdị ọcha

  • Ịsacha ụzọ na mmemme ịdị ọcha mgbe niile
  • Nlekọta oghere ọha
  • Iwu ahịa na mpaghara azụmahịa
  • Mmanye iwu mkpofu
  • Mgbasa ozi mmata ịdị ọcha obodo

Ilekwasị anya na mmepe obodo

  • Mmepe na nlekọta oghere ndụ ndụ
  • Ntinye aka na atụmatụ obodo na-adịgide adịgide
  • Nnyocha mmetụta gburugburu ebe obibi maka ọrụ
  • Njikọta teknụzụ ndụ ndụ
  • Mmemme ime ihe banyere ihu igwe
