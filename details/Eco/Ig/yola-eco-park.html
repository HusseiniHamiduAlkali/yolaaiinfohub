Yola Eco Park

Sustainable Park with Native Plants and Environmental Exhibits

About Yola Eco Park

Ogige Eco Yola bụ ogige na-adịgide adịgide na-egosipụta osisi obodo na ihe ngosi gburugburu. Ogige a bụ ebe ntụrụndụ na ebe agụmakwụkwọ, na-egosi omume na-adịgide adịgide ma na-akwalite mmata gburugburu n'etiti ndị ọbịa niile.

Park Features

  • Ogige osisi obodo: Egosi ụdị osisi mpaghara
  • Ebe nnụnụ na pollinators: Na-akwado iche iche ụdị ndụ
  • Ụzọ ije: Ụzọ eke maka nchọpụta
  • Ihe ngosi gburugburu: Ihe ngosi interaktịv gbasara ecology
  • Ebe picnic: Oghere ntụrụndụ maka ezinụlọ
  • Ebe ile nnụnụ: Ebe nlele maka ụdị nnụnụ

Native Plants Featured

  • Osisi neem (Azadirachta indica)
  • Osisi baobab (Adansonia digitata)
  • Ụdị Acacia
  • Afọ na ahịhịa mpaghara
  • Herb na shrubs obodo

Educational Programs

  • Njem ndu: Ije eke ndu nke ndị ọkachamara na-edu
  • Mmemme ụlọ akwụkwọ: Nlekọta sayensị gburugburu
  • Ụlọ ọrụ ngosi: Ọmụmụ ndụ ndu na-adịgide adịgide
  • Nyochaa anụ ọhịa: Ndetu iche iche ụdị ndụ
  • Ihe ngosi gburugburu: Ihe ọmụmụ interaktịv

Sustainability Practices

  • Usoro nchịkọta mmiri ozuzo
  • Imewe ala eke
  • Enweghị ọgwụ ntupu kemịkal
  • Composting mkpofu organic
  • Ebe ọrụ na-eji ike nke ọma
