Banyere Ndị Na-anakọta Mkpofu Ụlọ Yola

Ndị Na-anakọta Mkpofu Ụlọ Yola bụ ọrụ nwere ikike na-ahụ maka nchịkọta na iwepụ mkpofu ụlọ n'obodo Yola niile. Ụlọ ọrụ ahụ na-enye ngwọta njikwa mkpofu a pụrụ ịdabere na ya, rụọ ọrụ nke ọma, ma na-ejide ụzọ enyi gburugburu maka ezinụlọ niile.

Site na ọtụtụ afọ nke ahụmịhe na njikwa mkpofu, ha aghọwo onye mmekọ a pụrụ ịtụkwasị obi maka ezinụlọ na-achọ ụzọ ziri ezi iji wepụ mkpofu ha nke kwekọrọ na ụkpụrụ gburugburu.

Ọrụ enyere

  • Nchịkọta mkpofu ụlọ mgbe niile: Nchịkọta kwa izu ma ọ bụ kwa izu abụọ site na mpaghara ibi
  • Nkewa mkpofu: Enyemaka n'ikewa mkpofu organic na inorganic maka nrụgharị ka mma
  • Wepụ nnukwu mkpofu: Nchịkọta pụrụ iche maka ihe buru ibu dị ka akpụkpọ ụlọ na igwe nnukwu
  • Iwepụ enyi gburugburu: Ụzọ e si ewepụ mkpofu nke na-ebelata mmetụta gburugburu
  • Mmụmụ mkpofu: Mmemme mmata obodo banyere omume njikwa mkpofu

Ebe ọrụ

Ndị Na-anakọta Mkpofu Ụlọ na-arụ ọrụ n'ofe mpaghara Yola metropolitan, gụnyere:

  • Mpaghara azụmahịa Central Yola
  • Ụlọ obibi na agbataobi
  • Obodo suburb
  • Mpaghara semi-ala mgbago Yola

Nhazi nchịkọta

  • Mpaghara ibi: Ugboro abụọ kwa izu (Tues & Fri)
  • Mpaghara azụmahịa: Ugboro atọ kwa izu (Mon, Wed, Sat)
  • Nchịkọta pụrụ iche: Dị mgbe arịrịọ maka ụgwọ mgbakwunye
  • Ụbọchị ezumike ọha: Ụbọchị nhazi gbanwere ga-akọwaa tupu ya erute

Ngwa na ebe

  • Ụgbọ nchịkọta mkpofu oge a nwere usoro nrụpụ
  • Akpa mkpofu kwesịrị ekwesị maka ụlọ obibi
  • Ndị ọrụ nwere ọzụzụ na njikwa mkpofu
  • Ebe a họpụtara maka iwepụ mkpofu nwere nchebe gburugburu
  • GPS maka imeziwanye ụzọ
