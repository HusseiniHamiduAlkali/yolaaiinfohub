Green Environment Solutions Initiative, Yola

Ụlọ ọrụ nchekwa gburugburu ebe obibi obodo

Banyere GESOL

Green Environment Solutions Initiative (GESOL) bụ ụlọ ọrụ obodo na-arụ ọrụ na nchekwa gburugburu ebe obibi na mmata na Yola. Ụlọ ọrụ ahụ lekwasịrị anya na nchekwa gburugburu ebe obibi grassroots, ntinye aka obodo, na omume mmepe na-adịgide adịgide.

Ọrụ na ọhụụ

Ọrụ: Ichebe na mweghachi gburugburu ebe obibi site na ime ihe obodo, nkuzi, na omume na-adịgide adịgide.

Ọhụụ: Yola ebe nchekwa gburugburu ebe obibi bụ ọrụ onye ọ bụla na omume ndụ ndụ agbakwunyere na ndụ kwa ụbọchị.

Mmemme isi

  • Mgbasa ozi mmata gburugburu ebe obibi: Nkuzi obodo banyere okwu gburugburu ebe obibi
  • Mmemme ịkụ osisi: Ọrụ reforestation na Yola
  • Mmemme njikwa mkpofu: Mbelata mkpofu obodo na nrụgharị
  • Nchekwa mmiri: Mmemme ichebe na nchekwa akụrụngwa mmiri
  • Mmepe oghere ndụ ndụ: Ịmepụta na nlekọta ububa obodo

Ntinye aka obodo

  • Ịsacha gburugburu ebe obibi kwa ọnwa
  • Nkuzi banyere omume ndụ na-adịgide adịgide
  • Mmemme nkuzi gburugburu ebe obibi ụlọ akwụkwọ
  • Klọb gburugburu ebe obibi ntorobịa
  • Ohere ọrụ afọ ofufo

Mmezu nso nso a

  • Kụrụ karịrị osisi 10,000 na Yola
  • Haziri ihe omume ịsacha obodo 50+
  • Zụrụ mmadụ 500+ na njikwa mkpofu
  • Guworo ububa obodo 5
  • Sonyere ndị obodo 1,000+ na mmemme
