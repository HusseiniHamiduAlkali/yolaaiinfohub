Community-Led Environmental Conservation Project, Yola

Mmemme grassroots maka nchekwa gburugburu ebe obibi na omume na-adịgide adịgide

Banyere ọrụ ahụ

Community-Led Environmental Conservation Project bụ mmemme grassroots na-etinye aka na obodo na mbọ nchekwa gburugburu ebe obibi na omume na-adịgide adịgide na Yola. Ọrụ ahụ na-enye obodo ikike inwe gburugburu ebe obibi ha ma mejuputa ngwọta nchekwa.

Ụkpụrụ isi

  • Mkpebi obodo etiti
  • Inye ikike site na ntinye aka
  • Njikwa akụrụngwa na-adịgide adịgide
  • Njikọta ihe ọmụma ọdịnala
  • Nlekọta gburugburu ebe obibi ogologo oge

Ọrụ nchekwa

  • Mweghachi ebe obibi: Ichebe na mweghachi gburugburu ebe obibi eke
  • Ububa obodo: Ọrụ ugbo obodo maka nchekwa nri
  • Njikwa mkpofu: Mmemme mbelata mkpofu obodo
  • Nchekwa mmiri: Ichebe isi mmiri
  • Nchekwa anụ ọhịa: Ichebe anụmanụ obodo

Ntinye aka obodo

  • Nzukọ obodo na atụmatụ mgbe niile
  • Nkuzi gburugburu ebe obibi na ọzụzụ
  • Ntinye aka ntorobịa na nchekwa
  • Mmemme inye ikike ụmụ nwanyị
  • Nkwado maka azụmahịa obodo

Omume na-adịgide adịgide akwalitere

  • Ọrụ ugbo organic na ọrụ ugbo na-adịgide adịgide
  • Nchekwa mmiri na ịkọ mmiri
  • Nnwere onwe ike ọhụrụ
  • Mbelata mkpofu na nrụgharị
  • Agroforestry na ịkụ osisi

Nsonaazụ ọrụ

  • Sonyere ndị obodo 1,500+
  • Mweghachitere hectares 50 nke ebe obibi
  • Guworo ububa obodo 10
  • Zụrụ mmadụ 200+ na omume na-adịgide adịgide
  • Mewaa ndụ obodo

Uru maka obodo

  • Mewaa ikuku gburugburu ebe obibi
  • Nchekwa nri site na ububa obodo
  • Ịmepụta ego site na ọrụ nchekwa
  • Mmeziwanye ahụike na ọdịmma
  • Wusie njikọ obodo na nkwado
