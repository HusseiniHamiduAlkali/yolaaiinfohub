Adamawa Waste To Wealth Recyclers Limited

Na-agbanwe mkpofu ka ọ bụrụ uru na ohere

Banyere Adamawa Waste To Wealth Recyclers

Adamawa Waste To Wealth Recyclers Limited bụ ụlọ ọrụ njikwa mkpofu nwere ọhụrụ nke na-ejikọta ọrụ nrụgharị ụlọ ọrụ na azụmahịa na omume enyi gburugburu. Ụlọ ọrụ a na-agba mbọ ịgbanwe mkpofu ka ọ bụrụ ohere akụ ma na-echebe gburugburu ebe obibi.

Ọrụ Isi

  • Nrụgharị mkpofu ụlọ ọrụ: Nhazi mkpofu sitere n'ụlọ ọrụ nrụpụta
  • Njikwa mkpofu azụmahịa: Ngwọta maka azụmahịa na ụlọ ọrụ
  • Mgbake ihe: Wepụta ihe bara uru site na iyi mkpofu
  • Nhazi na nhazi mkpofu: Nkewa ọkachamara nke ihe ndị a na-enwegharị
  • Ndozi ngwaahịa dị mma: Ikwado ihe ndị eweghachitere maka ire

Ihe ndị a na-ahazi

  • Plastik na ihe polythene
  • Ígwè na ibe ígwè
  • Akpụkpọ akwụkwọ na kaadiboodu
  • Iko na seramik
  • Ihe mkpofu elektrọnik

Ohere akụ

  • Ohere ọrụ na njikwa mkpofu
  • Ụgwọ ziri ezi maka ihe mkpofu ndị a chịkọtara
  • Mmemme mmepe nkà maka ndị ọrụ
  • Ahịa maka ihe ndị eweghachitere ndị a na-enwegharị
  • Nkwado maka azụmahịa obodo

Nkwa gburugburu ebe obibi

  • Ibelata mkpofu ebe a na-ekpofu ihe nke ukwuu
  • Ibelata mmetọ gburugburu ebe obibi
  • Njikwa akụrụngwa na-adịgide adịgide
  • Mmemme mbelata nzọụkwụ carbon
  • Mmemme nkuzi gburugburu ebe obibi obodo
