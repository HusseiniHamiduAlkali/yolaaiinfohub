Orụ Ịkụ Osisi Yola

Ịkwalite omume enyi gburugburu na Yola

Banyere Orụ Ịkụ Osisi Yola

Orụ Ịkụ Osisi Yola raara onwe ya nye ịkwalite omume enyi gburugburu site na ịkụ ọtụtụ osisi na mmemme ọhịa obodo. Ọrụ a na-arụ iji bulie mkpuchi ndụ ndụ na Yola, gbaa ume megide mgbanwe ihu igwe, ma melite ịdị ọcha ikuku na ahụike gburugburu.

Ebumnuche oru

  • Ịkụ osisi 50,000 na Yola n'ime afọ 5
  • Bulie mkpuchi ọhịa na oghere ndụ
  • Gbaa ume megide mgbanwe ihu igwe na mmetọ ikuku
  • Mepụta ebe obibi maka anụmanụ na nnụnụ
  • Jikọta obodo n'ịchekwa gburugburu

Ụdị osisi

  • Osisi mkpụrụ: Mango, kasụ, guava maka mmepụta nri
  • Osisi osisi: Teak, iroko maka ọrụ ọhịa na-adịgide adịgide
  • Ụdị obodo: Osisi mpaghara maka mmezi ebe obibi
  • Osisi mma: Maka mma obodo na imeziwanye ikuku

Ihe omume oru

  • Mmemme ịkụ osisi obodo: A na-eme mmemme ịkụ osisi kwa ọnwa
  • Ọzụzụ nlekọta osisi: Atụmatụ maka nlekọta na iyi osisi
  • Ụlọ mkpụrụ osisi: Ikwu ụmụ osisi maka nkesa
  • Mmemme ụlọ akwụkwọ: Nkuzi gburugburu maka ntorobịa
  • Ịmepụta ndụ ndụ n'obodo: Ịkụ osisi n'akụkụ ụzọ na oghere ọha

Uru gburugburu

  • Melite ịdị ọcha ikuku ma belata mmetọ
  • Hazie okpomọkụ n'ime obodo
  • Zere ntapu ala na ntapu mmiri
  • Mepụta oghere ndụ na ebe ntụrụndụ
  • Nyere ebe obibi anụmanụ
  • Gbaa ume megide ntupu ụwa

Mmezu

  • Kụrụ osisi 15,000 kemgbe mmalite oru
  • Sonyere ndị obodo 2,000+
  • Zụrụ mmadụ 300+ na nlekọta osisi
  • Mepụta ụlọ mkpụrụ na-emepụta 5,000 ụmụ osisi kwa afọ
  • Bulite mkpuchi ndụ site na 10% na mpaghara ebumnuche

Otu esi sonye

  • Sonyere n'ime mmemme ịkụ osisi obodo
  • Kwado mmepe ịkụ osisi
  • Nabata osisi maka nlekọta ogologo oge
  • Bụ onye ọrụ afọ ofufo n'ọrụ oru
  • Kwalite mmata banyere gburugburu
