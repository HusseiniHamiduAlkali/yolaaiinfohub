Ndị Na-anakọta Mkpofu Plastik, Yola

Ọrụ nrụgharị mkpofu plastik pụrụ iche

Banyere ndị na-anakọta mkpofu plastik

Ndị Na-anakọta Mkpofu Plastik Yola bụ ọrụ pụrụ iche nke na-akwụsi ike n'ịnakọta na nrụgharị mkpofu plastik n'obodo Yola. Ha lekwasịrị anya na mbelata mmetọ plastik site n'inye ọrụ nchịkọta rụọ ọrụ na ngwọta nrụgharị ziri ezi.

Ọrụ enyere

  • Nchịkọta plastik: Ịnakọta plastik site n'ọnụ ụzọ ruo n'ọnụ ụzọ
  • Nhazi plastik: Ịkewa ụdị plastik dị iche (PET, HDPE, PVC, wdg)
  • Nrụgharị: Ịgbanwe plastik a chịkọtara ka ọ bụrụ ihe jiri ọzọ
  • Mkpofu plastik ụlọ ọrụ: Ntakọta site n'ụlọ ọrụ na ụlọ mmepụta
  • Mmemme mmata: Mụta obodo banyere mbelata mkpofu plastik

Ụdị plastik a na-anabata

  • PET - karama na akpa
  • HDPE - akpa na akpa siri ike
  • PVC - ọkpọkọ na mgbanaka
  • LDPE - akwa na akpa dị mfe
  • PP - akụkụ ụgbọala na akpa
  • PS - mkpuchi foam na ihe ị na-eji otu oge

Mmetụta na uru

  • Na-ebelata mmetọ plastik na gburugburu Yola
  • Na-enye ihe mbu maka ngwaahịa plastik ọhụrụ
  • Na-emepụta ohere ọrụ na njikwa mkpofu
  • Na-akwado ụkpụrụ akụnaụba okirikiri
  • Na-ebelata nrụgide na akụrụngwa eke

Ọnụahịa na nchịkọta

  • Nchịkọta ụlọ: Nnabata n'efu maka 5kg ma ọ bụ karịa
  • Nchịkọta nnukwu: Ọnụahịa belatara maka ibu azụmahịa
  • Ugboro: A na-enwe nchịkọta kwa izu
  • Arọ kacha nta: 2kg maka nchịkọta
