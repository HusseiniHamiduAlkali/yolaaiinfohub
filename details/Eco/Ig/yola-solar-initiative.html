Banyere Yola Solar Initiative

Yola Solar Initiative raara onwe ya nye ịkwalite nnwere onwe ike anyanwụ na ngwọta ike ọhụrụ na Yola. Ụlọ ọrụ ahụ na-arụ ọrụ iwelie mmata na nnweta teknụzụ anyanwụ ma belata ndabere na mmanụ ụgbọala na ịkwado mgbanwe ike na-adịgide adịgide.

Ọrụ

Ịkwalite nnwere onwe ike anyanwụ na Yola site na nkuzi, nkwado, na mmejuputa ngwọta ike ọhụrụ maka ezinụlọ, azụmahịa, na ụlọ ọrụ.

  • Nkuzi anyanwụ: Ọzụzụ na nkuzi banyere teknụzụ anyanwụ

  • Nkwado nrụnye: Ọrụ nrụnye ọkachamara
  • Mmemme nlekọta: Ọrụ na nkwado mgbe niile
  • Enyemaka ego: Enyemaka na nhọrọ itinye ego anyanwụ
