Njiytaaji Leydi Plastik, Yola

Golle topirde plastik juuti

Ko fii Njiytaaji Plastik

Njiytaaji Leydi Plastik Yola ko gollal juutiiɗo ngam njiytaade e topirde plastik e Yola. Fedde ngal naatnoyaa ƴettude ndiyam plastik e ɗowlaare topirde mum.

Golle ɗe ɗon waɗi

  • Plastic Collection: Door-to-door collection of plastic waste
  • Plastic Sorting: Classification of different plastic types (PET, HDPE, PVC, etc.)
  • Recycling Processing: Converting collected plastic into reusable materials
  • Leydi plastik kaŋko: Njiyta e suuduji gaynaako e laamuji njaare
  • Awareness Programs: Community education on plastic waste reduction

Laamu Plastik ɗi njiyata

  • PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate) - bottles and containers
  • HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) - bags and containers
  • PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) - pipes and fittings
  • LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene) - films and bags
  • PP (Polypropylene) - automotive parts and containers
  • PS (Polystyrene) - foam packaging and disposables

Ngaana e Faaydii

  • Reduces plastic pollution in Yola environment
  • Provides raw materials for new plastic products
  • Creates employment opportunities in waste management
  • Supports circular economy principles
  • Reduces pressure on natural resources

Laawol Njiyta e Dole

  • Household Collection: Free pickup for 5kg or more
  • Bulk Collection: Discounted rates for commercial volumes
  • Frequency: Weekly collections available
  • Minimum Weight: 2kg for pickups
