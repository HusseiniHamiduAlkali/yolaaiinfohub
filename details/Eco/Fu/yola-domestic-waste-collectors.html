Njiytaaji Leydi Suudu Yola

Golle topirde leydi suudu juuti e Yola

Ko fii Njiytaaji Suudu

Njiytaaji Leydi Suudu Yola ko gollal topirde leydi suudu okkitaaɗo e Yola. Fedde ngal waɗi gollal moƴƴi, juuti e jokkondiral leñol ngam suuduji kalaa.

E kala jaaramaaji gollal, fedde ngal waɗi ngon e ɓeyduɗo ngam suuduji ɓe waɗi so ɗon njiytude leydi ɗi e laawol leñol.

Golle ɗe ɗon waɗi

  • Regular Domestic Waste Collection: Scheduled weekly or bi-weekly collection from residential areas
  • Waste Segregation: Assistance in separating organic and inorganic waste for better recycling
  • Bulk Waste Removal: Specialized collection of bulky items like furniture and large appliances
  • Eco-Friendly Disposal: Responsible waste disposal methods that minimize environmental impact
  • Waste Education: Community awareness programs on proper waste management practices

Mbaydi Golle

Yola Domestic Waste Collectors operate throughout Yola metropolitan area, including:

  • Central Yola business districts
  • Residential neighborhoods
  • Suburban communities
  • Semi-rural areas around Yola

Laawol Njiyta

  • Suuduji: Njiyta gannii-je (Talaata & Juma)
  • Suuduji gaynaako: Njiyta tattaalii-je (Altine, Alarba, Sabti)
  • Special Collections: Available on request for additional charges
  • Public Holidays: Modified schedule communicated in advance

Jamii e Suudu

  • Motooji njiytaaji modern waɗi system compact
  • Proper waste containment bins for households
  • Trained waste management personnel
  • Designated waste disposal site with environmental safeguards
  • GPS tracking for route optimization
