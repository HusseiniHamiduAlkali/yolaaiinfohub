Projektii Hawtude Ndiyam

Golle nde naatataa hawtude ndiyam e laawol tawaangal ndiyam

Ko fii Projektii Hawtude Ndiyam

Projektii ɗee naatataa nanndude e waɗtude golluuji hawtude ndiyam e tawaangal laawol ndiyam e Yola. Ngal projektii naatnoyaa hawtude jayngol ndiyam, ƴettude leydi e heɓtude ndiyam safaaɗo ɗe ɓe ɗon.

Maƴɗe Projektii

  • Promote water conservation awareness in communities
  • Reduce water waste in households and businesses
  • Protect and restore water resources
  • Improve water quality and safety
  • Develop sustainable water management practices

Golle Hawtude

  • Water Audits: Assessing water usage in buildings
  • Technology Solutions: Installing water-saving fixtures
  • Educational Campaigns: Community awareness on conservation
  • Rainwater Harvesting: Systems for water collection and storage
  • Wetland Restoration: Protecting natural water sources

Teknolooji Hawtude Ndiyam

  • Low-flow faucets and showerheads
  • Water-efficient toilets
  • Drip irrigation systems
  • Rainwater collection tanks
  • Greywater recycling systems

Ngaana Rewɓe

  • Trained 300+ households on water conservation
  • Saved 2 million liters of water annually
  • Installed 100+ water-saving devices
  • Protected 5+ water sources
  • Improved water quality in target areas

Hol no a waawi jokkondiral

  • Participate in conservation programs
  • Install water-saving devices at home
  • Support wetland and water source protection
  • Reduce outdoor water use
  • Jokkondiral e golluuji projektii
