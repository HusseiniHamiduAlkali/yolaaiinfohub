Ko fii 'Leydi e Yowre'

Projektii 'Leydi e Yowre' ko gollal ngonaande nde waɗi leydi to yowre, waɗa art e nder faydeji, tee naatnoyaa ɓeydude leñol. Projektii ngal waɗi holli hol njamaare woni waɗi heɓtude leydi e yowre mawɗe.

Maƴɗe Projektii

  • Transform waste into artistic creations
  • Promote creativity and innovation
  • Raise environmental awareness
  • Create employment opportunities
  • Support sustainable consumption

Golluuji e Ndaari

  • Art Installations: Large-scale public art from waste
  • Functional Items: Furniture and home accessories
  • Fashion: Bags, accessories, clothing from recycled materials
  • Decorative Items: Artwork and ornaments
  • Educational Tools: Learning materials for schools

Golle Program

  • Workshops: Training in upcycling techniques
  • Community Projects: Collaborative creative work
  • Art Exhibitions: Showcasing finished creations
  • School Programs: Youth environmental education
  • Market Events: Selling upcycled products

Kaye ɗi waɗi naatɗo

  • Plastic bottles and containers
  • Fabric scraps and old clothing
  • Metal and wire scraps
  • Paper and cardboard
  • Worn-out household items

Ngaana e Janngol Leñol

  • Demonstrates waste reduction possibilities
  • Changes perception of waste as a resource
  • Inspires sustainable consumption
  • Engages communities in environmental issues
  • Promotes creative sustainability solutions

Faaydii Rewɓe

  • Employment for artists and craftspeople
  • Income generation from product sales
  • Skills development and training
  • Community cultural enrichment
  • Environmental consciousness raising
