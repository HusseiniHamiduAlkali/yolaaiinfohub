Green Environment Solutions Initiative, Yola (GESOL)

Fedde jokkondiral ngam ɓeydude leñol

Ko fii GESOL

Green Environment Solutions Initiative (GESOL) ko fedde jokkondiral ngoodi e ɓeydude leñol e Yola. Fedde ngal naatnoyaa ɓeydude leñol joomiraaɗi, jokkondiral rewɓe, e tawaangal jaafunɗe.

Naatnude e Ndeer

Naatnude: Jokkude e wurtinde leñol e nder jokkondiral rewɓe, janngol e tawaangal jaafunɗe.

Ndeer: Yola ɗe ɓeydude leñol ɗon e maaɓɓe kalaa e tawaangal ɗi jooɗi e nder ɗeere ɗi.

Golleji Kala

  • Janngol ɓeydude: Janngol rewɓe e ɗowle leñol
  • Wurtinde lanndu: Golle wurtinde laanaaji e Yola
  • Topirde leydi: Golle ƴettude leydi e topirde
  • Hawtude ndiyam: Golle hawtude e jeyaaɓe ndiyam
  • Baadi rewɓe: Njaare e waɗtude baadi rewɓe

Jokkondiral Rewɓe

  • Wadde njunndu leñol kala lewru
  • Seminaaji e njoɓdi tawaangal
  • Prograamii janngol leñol e suuduji
  • Kulɓe ɓeerndu ɓeydude leñol
  • Hol no waawi jokkondiral

Jumɗe Jiɗɗi

  • Wurtinii 10,000+ laanaaji e Yola
  • Mbaytii 50+ golluuji ndokkude leydi
  • Jannginii 500+ yimɓe e topirde leydi
  • Yahanii 5 baadi rewɓe
  • Jokkondirii 1,000+ yimɓe e golluuji
