Ko fii Nganndu Metal

Nganndu Metal e Scrap Yola ko gollal topirde metal, nde njiyataa ngoni scrap metal e fow e Yola. Fedde ngal wondi e heɓtude metal maaɗa e ɓeydude leñol.

Golle ɗe ɗon waɗi

  • Scrap Metal Collection: Pickup of metal scraps from homes and businesses
  • Metal Sorting: Classification and separation of different metal types
  • Industrial Metal Waste: Bulk collection from factories and workshops
  • Demolition Waste: Collection of metal from construction site debris
  • Fair Market Pricing: Competitive rates for collected metals

Laamu metal ɗi njiyta

  • Ferrous Metals: Steel, iron, and cast iron
  • Non-Ferrous Metals: Aluminum, copper, brass, zinc
  • Stainless Steel: High-grade stainless steel items
  • Electronics Scrap: Metals from electronic waste
  • Automotive Scrap: Metal parts from vehicles
