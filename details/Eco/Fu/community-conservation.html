Projektii Ɓeydude Rewɓe e Leñol, Yola

Projektii so tawii joomiraaɗi, ngoodi e ɗowre rewɓe ɗe ngam ɓeydude leñol e tawaangal jaafunɗe

Ko fii Projektii

Projektii ɗee wonaa joomiraaɗi ngam ɓeydude leñol, nde rewɓe e leydi ɗi waɗi jokkondiral, jogii e nder Yola. Projektii ngal naatnoyaa rewɓe ngam hulnude leñol e huuwde so wonaa tawaangal.

Teelte Kala

  • Jokkondiral rewɓe e ɗimmiɗi
  • Naatnude rewɓe e jokkondiral
  • Wakkilaare jayngol e mawɗe
  • Hooliɗe laatugol sappoore ɗi
  • Laawol ɓeydude leñol maaɗa

Golle ɓeydude

  • Wurtinde habitat: Jokkude e wurtinde laanaaji ɗi
  • Baadi rewɓe: Njaare dow suudu ngam luumo ndiyam
  • Topirde leydi: Golle rewɓe ñaamɗo ngam ƴettude leydi
  • Hawtude ndiyam: Jokkude jeyaaɓe ndiyam
  • Hawtude dowji: ɓeydude horɗe leñol

Jokkondiral Rewɓe

  • Wadde jokkondiral e yimɓe e yeesoore
  • Janngol ɓeydude leñol e wullude
  • Saare maɓɓe ɓe yahi ɓeydude
  • Golle nde ngoodi rewɓe debboɓe
  • Naatnude huuwde ndiyam e jeyaaɓe rewɓe

Tawaangal jaafunɗe waɗɗi

  • Njaare organik e tawaangal njaare
  • Hawtude ndiyam e ƴeewtude
  • Suuɗtude lewru woni laawol
  • Ƴettude leydi e topirde fayde
  • Njaare e gollal ɗee nannduɗo laanaaji

Jumɗe Projektii

  • Jokkondirii 1,500+ yimɓe rewɓe
  • Wurtinii 50 hektaar laanaaji
  • Yahanii 10 baadi rewɓe
  • Jannginii 200+ yimɓe e tawaangal jaafunɗe
  • Nokka fii dumɗe dow rewɓe

Faaydii ɗe rewɓe

  • Taggude laawol leñol
  • Luumo ndiyam ngam baadi rewɓe
  • Heddital kalaaɗe e golluuji ɓeydude
  • Yamiroore e mawnirde
  • Taggude laawol e jokkondiral rewɓe
