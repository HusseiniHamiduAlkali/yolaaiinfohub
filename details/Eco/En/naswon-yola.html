About NASWON

The National Association of Scraps and Waste Pickers of Nigeria (NASWON) is focused on city cleanliness and sustainable urban development. The organization represents scrap and waste pickers, advocating for better working conditions, environmental protection, and community development.

Key Objectives

  • Maintain and improve city cleanliness standards
  • Promote sustainable urban development
  • Advocate for waste pickers' rights and welfare
  • Support formalization of informal waste sector
  • Engage in environmental conservation activities

City Cleanliness Programs

  • Waste Collection: Regular street and area cleaning
  • Material Recovery: Collecting valuable items from waste
  • Community Engagement: Promoting community responsibility
  • Public Education: Awareness on waste disposal
  • Infrastructure Maintenance: Keeping public spaces clean

Sustainable Development Initiatives

  • Integration of waste pickers into formal waste management
  • Development of recycling infrastructure
  • Training and skills development programs
  • Support for green business development
  • Environmental protection strategies

Member Benefits

  • Representation and advocacy support
  • Access to training and development programs
  • Connection to market opportunities
  • Welfare and safety support
  • Community and networking opportunities
