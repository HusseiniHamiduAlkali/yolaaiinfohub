About Yola Plastics and Polythenes

Yola Plastics and Polythenes Waste Recycling Company is the leading plastic recycling facility in Yola, specializing in processing and recycling all types of plastics and polythene materials. The company combines modern technology with environmental responsibility to transform waste into valuable resources.

Recycling Services

  • Plastic Waste Processing: Advanced recycling of various plastic types
  • Polythene Film Recycling: Specialized handling of plastic films and bags
  • Plastic Granule Production: Creating raw materials for new plastic products
  • Bulk Waste Handling: Processing large quantities of plastic waste
  • Quality Assurance: Rigorous testing of recycled materials

Materials Processed

  • Single-use plastic bags and packaging
  • Plastic bottles and containers
  • Polythene films and sheets
  • Industrial plastic waste
  • Commercial plastic scraps

Production Capabilities

  • Daily Processing Capacity: Up to 5 tons of plastic waste
  • Granule Output: High-quality plastic granules for manufacturing
  • Product Range: Various grades for different applications
  • Quality Standards: ISO certified recycling processes

Environmental Impact

  • Diverts tons of plastic from landfills annually
  • Reduces demand for virgin plastic production
  • Conserves petroleum resources
  • Minimizes environmental pollution
  • Supports sustainable development goals
