Waste Pickers Association of Nigeria (WAPAN), Yola

Community-Based Environmental Protection Organization

About WAPAN

The Waste Pickers Association of Nigeria (WAPAN) is a community-based organization working on environmental protection and awareness in Yola. WAPAN represents waste pickers and informal waste collectors, providing support, advocacy, and development opportunities for its members.

Organization Focus

  • Advocacy for waste picker rights and working conditions
  • Environmental protection through waste management
  • Community awareness on waste issues
  • Income generation for waste pickers
  • Support for informal waste sector formalization

Member Support Services

  • Training Programs: Skills development for waste management
  • Market Linkages: Connections to buyers for collected materials
  • Safety Equipment: Provision of personal protective equipment
  • Welfare Support: Health and social services
  • Advocacy: Representing members' interests

Environmental Contributions

  • Collects and processes valuable waste materials
  • Reduces landfill waste burden
  • Provides recycling services to communities
  • Raises awareness about waste management
  • Contributes to circular economy development

Economic and Social Impact

  • Provides income for hundreds of waste pickers
  • Improves working conditions in waste sector
  • Creates opportunities for marginalized groups
  • Supports family livelihoods
  • Contributes to poverty reduction
