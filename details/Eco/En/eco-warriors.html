Green Environment Solutions Initiative, Yola

Community-Based Environmental Protection Organization

About GESOL

Green Environment Solutions Initiative (GESOL) is a community-based organization working on environmental protection and awareness in Yola. The organization focuses on grassroots environmental conservation, community engagement, and sustainable development practices.

Mission and Vision

Mission: To protect and restore the environment through community action, education, and sustainable practices.

Vision: A Yola where environmental conservation is everyone's responsibility and green practices are integrated into daily life.

Key Programs

  • Environmental Awareness Campaigns: Community education on environmental issues
  • Tree Planting Initiatives: Reforestation projects in Yola
  • Waste Management Programs: Community waste reduction and recycling
  • Water Conservation: Programs to protect and conserve water resources
  • Green Spaces Development: Creating and maintaining community gardens

Community Involvement

  • Monthly environmental cleanup drives
  • Workshops on sustainable living practices
  • School environmental education programs
  • Youth environmental clubs
  • Volunteer opportunities

Recent Achievements

  • Planted over 10,000 trees in Yola
  • Organized 50+ community cleanup events
  • Trained 500+ people on waste management
  • Established 5 community gardens
  • Engaged 1,000+ community members in programs
