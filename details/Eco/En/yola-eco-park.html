Yola Eco Park

Sustainable Park with Native Plants and Environmental Exhibits

About Yola Eco Park

Yola Eco Park is a sustainable park featuring native plants and environmental exhibits. The park serves as both a recreational space and an educational facility, showcasing sustainable practices and promoting environmental awareness among visitors of all ages.

Park Features

  • Native Plant Gardens: Showcasing local flora species
  • Butterfly and Pollinator Zones: Supporting biodiversity
  • Walking Trails: Nature paths for exploration
  • Environmental Exhibits: Interactive displays on ecology
  • Picnic Areas: Family-friendly recreational spaces
  • Bird Watching Areas: Observation spots for avian species

Native Plants Featured

  • Neem trees (Azadirachta indica)
  • Baobab trees (Adansonia digitata)
  • Acacia species
  • Local wildflowers and grasses
  • Indigenous herbs and shrubs

Educational Programs

  • Guided Tours: Expert-led nature walks
  • School Programs: Environmental science classes
  • Workshops: Sustainable living practices
  • Wildlife Monitoring: Biodiversity tracking
  • Environmental Exhibits: Interactive learning displays

Sustainability Practices

  • Rainwater harvesting systems
  • Natural landscape design
  • No chemical pesticides
  • Organic waste composting
  • Energy-efficient facilities

Visitor Amenities

  • Walking and cycling paths
  • Picnic and relaxation areas
  • Clean restroom facilities
  • Information center
  • Parking facilities
  • Small café with sustainable practices
