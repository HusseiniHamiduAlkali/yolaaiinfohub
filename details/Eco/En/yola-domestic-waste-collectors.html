About Yola Domestic Waste Collectors

Yola Domestic Waste Collectors is a licensed waste management service specializing in domestic waste collection and disposal throughout Yola. The company provides reliable, efficient, and environmentally responsible waste management solutions for households of all sizes.

With years of experience in waste management, the organization has established itself as a trusted partner for families seeking proper waste disposal services that meet environmental standards.

Services Offered

  • Regular Domestic Waste Collection: Scheduled weekly or bi-weekly collection from residential areas
  • Waste Segregation: Assistance in separating organic and inorganic waste for better recycling
  • Bulk Waste Removal: Specialized collection of bulky items like furniture and large appliances
  • Eco-Friendly Disposal: Responsible waste disposal methods that minimize environmental impact
  • Waste Education: Community awareness programs on proper waste management practices

Service Areas
