Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC), Yola Office

Government agency wey dey register businesses an' regulate companies

Wetin CAC Yola Office dey do

Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) Yola Office na government agency wey dey register an' regulate businesses, corporate entities, an' associations for Adamawa State. CAC dey make sure say companies follow corporate laws an' dem dey help people register businesses, partnerships an' organizations properly.

Main functions an' services

  • Business name registration an' renewal
  • Company incorporation an' registration
  • Registration for trustees (NGOs)
  • Business information an' document retrieval
  • Company compliance an' filings
  • Corporate governance an' regulatory oversight

Business registration services

  • Business name registration - For sole proprietors an' partnerships
  • Company registration - For limited liability companies
  • Registration of trustees - For non-profit organizations
  • Registration of foreign companies - For companies wey dey operate for Nigeria
  • Branch registration - For businesses wey dey expand
  • Certificate of incorporation - Official proof say business register

Compliance an' regulation

  • Annual returns an' financial reporting requirements
  • Directors an' shareholders information updates
  • Audit reports an' corporate governance compliance
  • Enforcement of corporate laws an' regulations
  • Investigation of corporate violations
  • Legal advisory services on corporate matters

Documentation an' records

  • Issuance of certificates of registration
  • Certified true copies of registered documents
  • Company search an' verification services
  • Name availability checking
  • Document authentication services
  • Historical records an' archive services

Support for entrepreneurs

  • Guidance on business registration processes
  • Information on corporate regulatory requirements
  • Business name suggestions an' approval
  • Registration fee clarification an' payment options
  • Assistance with compliance documentation
  • Customer care an' support services

Operating hours an' access

  • Monday to Friday business hours
  • Many service counters for quick processing
  • Online registration an' filing options dey available
  • Walk-in services an' appointment scheduling
  • Accessible facilities for persons with disabilities
  • Customer information center with guides an' forms

How to reach dem

Where dem dey: Yola, Adamawa State

CAC Yola Office dey provide full business registration an' corporate regulation services to entrepreneurs, businesses, an' organizations for Adamawa State. Visit the office make dem help you for business registration, compliance matters, or corporate questions.
