Red Cross Society Yola Branch

International humanitarian organization for emergency relief an' community support

Wetin Red Cross Society Yola Branch dey do

Red Cross Society Yola Branch na humanitarian organization wey dey provide emergency relief, healthcare services, an' community support to vulnerable people for Adamawa State. As part of the International Red Cross Movement, dem dey follow principles of humanity, impartiality, neutrality, independence, voluntary service, unity, an' universality.
