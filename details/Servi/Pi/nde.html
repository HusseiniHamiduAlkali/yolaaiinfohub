National Directorate Of Employment (NDE), Yola Office

Government agency for employment an' skills development

Wetin NDE Yola dey do

National Directorate of Employment (NDE) Yola Office na government agency wey dey work for employment generation, skills acquisition, an' job creation programs for Adamawa State. Under the Federal Ministry of Labour an' Employment, NDE dey help reduce unemployment an' give people marketable skills.

Main mission an' objectives

  • Generate employment opportunities for unemployed people
  • Provide skills acquisition an' vocational training
  • Support youth entrepreneurship an' business development
  • Implement poverty alleviation programs
  • Help graduates move from school to work
  • Promote sustainable job creation initiatives

Training programs wey dem dey give

  • Vocational skills training for different trades
  • Entrepreneurship development programs
  • ICT training
  • Agricultural skills an' farm management courses
  • Artisan an' craft training programs
  • Business management an' financial literacy courses

Employment services

  • Job placement an' career counseling
  • Job matching services wey connect employers an' job seekers
  • Resume an' interview preparation workshops
  • Labour market info an' trends analysis
  • Employment fair organization an' coordination
  • Apprenticeship an' internship programs

Entrepreneurship support

  • Business plan development assistance
  • Micro-credit an' loan facilitation
  • Startup mentorship programs
  • Business registration guidance
  • Market access an' networking opportunities
  • Business monitoring an' growth support

Special programs

  • Youth employment programs (N-Power etc.)
  • Graduate internship schemes
  • Community development programs
  • Disability employment initiatives
  • Women empowerment an' economic programs
  • Post-disaster employment rehabilitation

Eligibility an' application

  • Open to all Nigerian citizens
  • Programs for different age groups an' qualifications
  • Free or subsidized training courses
  • Registration an' enrollment processes available
  • Support services for disadvantaged groups
  • Online an' offline application options

How to reach dem

Where dem dey: Yola, Adamawa State

NDE Yola Office dey ready to serve the employment needs of people for Adamawa State. For questions about training programs, job placement, or entrepreneurship support, visit the office during working hours or contact dem through official channels.
