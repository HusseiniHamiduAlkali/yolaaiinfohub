Maßßi Duuji CAC Yola

Duuji CAC Yola woni koroporeysun jokkorde ma sariyya hakkaa duuji e haakoore kampo, koroporeysun e kaymu a Adamawa. Sorta sowtere ko ndoowa ndee fow nder Naajeria, CAC faataane taje e faataane duuji, keew kaytey e kaymu.
