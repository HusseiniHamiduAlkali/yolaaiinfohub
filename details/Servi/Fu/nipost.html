Hewlude Postal Resmi Naaje Jambugol

Baɗte NIPOST Yola

Hewlude Postal Nijiriya (NIPOST) Yola Branch ja hewlude postal resmi ko Adamawa State. Ko ji fuddika seere mayle, sakka mawɓe e hewlude postal ko saaji e kaasale. Ko gollan Federal Ministry Kommyunikaasyon e Diital Economy. NIPOST ko saaji hewlude kommyunikaasyon moƴƴugol Nijiriya.

Hewlude Mawɓe Hokki

  • Seere mayle Naaje - Mayle e dokumo
  • Hewlude sakka e kasale
  • Hewlude mayle Teekere (EMS)
  • Mayle e sakka International
  • Hewlude mayle Sagal
  • Hewlude mayle Garantini e sakka Muuɓe

Seere e Hewlude Postal Mawɓe

  • Mayle e Baruwa Jowɓe

  • Mayle Sagal - Teekere e sagal seere muuɓe
  • Sakka International - Jiitol nde-naaje
  • Hewlude Filateli - Timbe e sakka postal muuɓe

Hamma Tiiɗnde

Nooto: Yola, Adamawa State

NIPOST Yola ko saaji hewlude postal moƴƴugol ko saaji kumarɓe e kaasale Adamawa State. Tiim bransa ko nooto ko seere mayle, sakka, ko sagal postal. Kumarɓe teknik ko hakkil jamaa mayle mawɓe.
