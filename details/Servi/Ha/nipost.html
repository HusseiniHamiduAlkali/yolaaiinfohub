Game da NIPOST Yola

Manyan Ayyuka da muke Bayarwa

  • Sabis na jigilar kaya da parcels
  • Sabis na gaggawa (EMS)
  • Aika wasiƙu da kaya zuwa ƙasashen waje
  • Wasiƙun da aka yi wa rijista
  • Kula da kaya masu daraja

Lokutan Aiki

  • Asabar: Sabis kaɗan
