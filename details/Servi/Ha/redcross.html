Kungiyar Red Cross Ofishin Yola

Kungiyar Jin Kai ta Duniya don Taimakon Gaggawa da Tallafin Al'umma

Game da Kungiyar Red Cross Reshen Yola

Kungiyar Red Cross reshen Yola ƙungiya ce ta jin kai da ke sadaukarwa don samar da agajin gaggawa, sabis na lafiya, da tallafi ga mutanen da ke cikin mawuyacin hali a Jihar Adamawa. Muna aiki akan ƙa'idodin jin kai, rashin nuna bambanci, da sa kai.

Manyan Ayyukan Jin Kai

  • Agajin gaggawa da martani ga bala'i
  • Horar da taimakon gaggawa (First Aid)
  • Shirye-shiryen lafiyar al'umma
  • Ilimin kiwon lafiya a matakin al'umma
  • Gudunmawar jini don ceton rai
  • Neman dangin da suka ɓace

Yadda za ku Tallafa

  • Ba da gudunmawar kuɗi
  • Ba da jini don ceton rai
  • Kasance mai sa kai
  • Wayar da kan mutane game da ayyukan Red Cross

Hanyoyin Sadarwa

Wuri: Yola, Jihar Adamawa

Red Cross tana maraba da tallafin al'umma. Don tambayoyi ko neman taimako, ziyarci ofishinmu.
