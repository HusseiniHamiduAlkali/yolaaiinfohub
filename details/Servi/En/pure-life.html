About Purelife Foundation

The Ahmad Uthman Purelife Foundation is a non-profit, community-based organization dedicated to improving the quality of life for vulnerable and marginalized populations in Adamawa State. The foundation operates with a mission to provide humanitarian services, promote education, healthcare, and community development initiatives that address pressing social challenges and foster sustainable development.

Mission and Vision

  • Improve living standards for disadvantaged communities
  • Provide quality education and skills training
  • Promote health and wellness initiatives
  • Build sustainable development programs
  • Foster social cohesion and community unity
  • Empower individuals and families for self-reliance
