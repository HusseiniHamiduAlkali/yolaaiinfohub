Red Cross Society Yola Branch

International Humanitarian Organization for Emergency Relief and Community Support

About Red Cross Society Yola Branch

The Red Cross Society Yola Branch is a humanitarian organization dedicated to providing emergency relief, healthcare services, and community support to vulnerable populations in Adamawa State. As part of the International Red Cross Movement, the organization operates with principles of humanity, impartiality, neutrality, independence, voluntary service, unity, and universality.
