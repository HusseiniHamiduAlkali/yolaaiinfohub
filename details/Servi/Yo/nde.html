Ajọ Àkbùkùn Ìgbẹ̀púpọ̀ Olòmímó Àwọjọ́ (NDE), Ilé 1ẹ̀wọkfé Yola

Ajọ ilé-ijobá fun awon isẹ ati ẹko awon ogó

Nipa NDE Yola

NDE Yola na ajọ ilé-ijobá ti a sẹ lati fọfẹ awon isẹ wọpọ, n pẹsẹ ẹko awon ogó, ati n sẹ awon imọ-isẹ awon olòmímó óre Adamawa State. Ni pé Ajọ 1ẹ̀tógó Wọ́lúfù Iyalode ati Awon Isẹ, NDE n rán ènyán lati dinu alẹ òmímó ai-isẹ, lö ilẹ òmímó ai-ló, ati n pẹsẹ awon ogó ti ọ lẹ tẹ nipa oju isẹ òmímó.

Main Activities and Goals

  • Create job opportunities for unemployed people
  • Provide vocational skills training
  • Support youth businesses and enterprise development
  • Run poverty reduction programs
  • Ease school-to-work transition for graduates
  • Promote sustainable job creation initiatives

Training Programs We Dey Offer

  • Vocational skills training in various trades
  • Business development programs
  • Information and Communication Technology (ICT) training
  • Agricultural skills and farm management
  • Technical and artisanal skills training
  • Business management and financial literacy

Employment Services

  • Job placement and career advice
  • Workforce matching services between employers and job seekers
  • Resume preparation and interview training workshops
  • Job market information and trend analysis
  • Job fair and labour market coordination
  • Apprenticeship and internship programs

Business Support

  • Help with business plan development
  • Ease access to micro-credit and loans
  • Start-up mentorship programs
  • Guidance on business registration
  • Market access and networking opportunities
  • Business coaching and growth support

Key Programs

  • Youth employment programs like N-Power
  • Graduate internship schemes
  • Community development programs
  • Job initiatives for persons with disabilities
  • Women empowerment and economic programs
  • Post-disaster employment recovery

Who Fit Apply and How

  • Open to all Nigerians
  • Programs for different age groups and needs
  • Free or subsidized training options
  • Registration procedures and available forms
  • Support services for disadvantaged groups
  • Online and offline application options

Contact Info

Location: Yola, Adamawa State

NDE Yola dey serve the employment needs of people in Adamawa State. For questions about training programs, job placement services, or business support, visit the office during working hours or contact through official channels.
