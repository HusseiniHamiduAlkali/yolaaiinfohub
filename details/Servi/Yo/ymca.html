YMCA Yola Chapter

Empowering young men through education, health, and community service

About YMCA Yola Chapter

YMCA (Young Men's Christian Association) Yola Chapter na organization working to build strong community and develop young men properly. Through education, sports, and community service, YMCA create safe space where young men learn, grow, and contribute to society.

Main Work And Main Idea

  • Develop young men for body, mind, and spirit
  • Promote good behavior and character development
  • Promote health and well-being for young men
  • Build strong community with support and cooperation
  • Engage young men in skills training and community service
  • Work with all sectors of the community

Education Programs

  • Develop skills and teach vocational training
  • Computer and mathematics education
  • Support with academic learning and tutoring
  • Discuss life skills and career guidance
  • Learn how to live and how to get employment
  • Provide school scholarships and educational support
