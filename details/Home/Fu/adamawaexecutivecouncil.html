Hukumta Leɗɗe Adamawa e Majalisa Laamu

Jillal hollarangal laamu e laamorgo leɗɗe ndee

Hukumta Leɗɗe Adamawa: Jilal

Leɗɗe Adamawa ko gooto e leɗɗe 36 Nigeria, joodii e fedde fuɗnaange leɗɗe ndee. Laamu mum ko fannu ko waɗii ko fannu laamu Nigeria fuɗnaange, e leɗɗe ndee ko joodii e Yola. Hukumta leɗɗe ndee ko koɗol laamu, ɓeydinde, e moƴƴere yimɓe mum. Gollal mum ko ɓeydinde ekonoma, sappaade, kiɗɗe, laamu, ngesa, e kisal.

Gollal mum ko huɓɓi ko fannu laamu Nigeria e sarɗiiji leɗɗe Adamawa.

Gobbo: Laamorgo Maa

Gobbo ko laamorgo resu laamu leɗɗe Adamawa e koɗol laamu resu. Ko o laamii duuɓi mawɗi, o waɗii ko laamii duuɓi ɗiɗo. Gollal o ko:

  • Fannu daɗol e huɓɓinde: Fannu daɗol leɗɗe ndee e huɓɓinde gollal laamu e ofisiiji mum.
  • Lollinde e reende: Lollinde komisiijoɗo, ɗaɗoɓe, e hooreeɓe goɗɗe ngam laamu fannuuji keewɗi.
  • Ceɗe e kiɗɗe: Addude ceɗe leɗɗe ndee e reende kiɗɗe mum.
  • Kisal: Ko o kisalorgo leɗɗe ndee, ko o hoolii laamu e kisal.
Natal: Hoto Gobbo Leɗɗe Adamawa

Majalisa Laamu Leɗɗe: Kabinetto Gobbo

Majalisa Laamu Leɗɗe, kadi ko Kabinetto Gobbo, ko majalisa laamu mawɗa e leɗɗe resu. Ko majalisa ɗaɗol e laamu, ko o hoolii Gobbo ngam laamu leɗɗe ndee. Majalisa ndee ko Gobbo laamii, e o waɗii ko Deputi Gobbo e komisiijoɗo fof. Komisiijoɗo kala ko o hoolii ofis o, o jaɓɓorgo e majalisa. Naatnaade majalisa ndee ko naatnaade e lewru, ɗo laamuuji mawɗi leɗɗe ndee, pinal e huɓɓinde ko ɗaɗol.

Gollal Majalisa Laamu Leɗɗe:

  • Ɗaɗol laamu: Jaɓɓorgo e huɓɓinde laamuuji kesi e sarɗiiji ngam addude e Suudu Laamu Leɗɗe Adamawa.
  • Fannu ceɗe: Huɓɓinde ko fannu ceɗe leɗɗe ndee ngam pinal e gollal keewɗi.
  • Reende pinal: Reende ɓeydinde pinal mawɗi leɗɗe ndee e huɓɓinde mum ko ɓeydi e saasi.
  • Laamu yimɓe: Waɗude majalisa ngam laamu yimɓe, ɗo komisiijoɗoɓe jaɓɓorgo ko gollal ofisiiji mum.

Gobbo e Kabinetto Makko: Hooreeɓe Maaɓɓe

Kabinetto ndee ko renndo hooreeɓe e politiikoɓe, kala ko o lollinaa ngam laamu daɗol leɗɗe ndee. Les ɗee ko hooreeɓe kabinetto keewɗi, kala ko o hoolii fannu mawɗu e laamu leɗɗe ndee.

Gobbo

Gollal: Laamorgo resu, Hooree Majalisa Laamu. Ko o fannuɗo laamuuji leɗɗe ndee fof, ko o duɗal e laamuuji resu.

Deputi Gobbo

Gollal: Ko o laamii ɗum e hooree Gobbo, o waɗii ko o hoolii fannuuji keewɗi e laamii e ɗum Gobbo woodi.

Sikritara Leɗɗe (SSG)

Gollal: Ko o sikritara mawɗo e leɗɗe ndee. O hoolii gollal ofisiiji e hukumta leɗɗe ndee, o waɗii ko sikritara Majalisa Laamu.

Komisiijo Ceɗe

Gollal: O hoolii ceɗe leɗɗe ndee, fannu ceɗe, addude ceɗe, e reende ceɗe. Ko o hoolii ngam laamu ceɗe leɗɗe ndee.

Komisiijo Gollal e Suudu

Gollal: O hoolii fannu, mahinde, e reende laawol leɗɗe ndee fof, laawol, ɗate, e suudu yimɓe.

Komisiijo Kiɗɗe

Gollal: O hoolii kiɗɗe leɗɗe ndee, suudu kiɗɗe yimɓe, e laamu kiɗɗe.

Komisiijo Laamu

Gollal: O hoolii laamu leɗɗe ndee, dungu laamu, e sarɗiiji laamu.

Komisiijo Ngesa

Gollal: O hoolii ɓeydinde ngesa, kisal ngesa, e wallitorde ngesaɓe. Ko o hoolii ngam ekonoma ngesa leɗɗe ndee.
