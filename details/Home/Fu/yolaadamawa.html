Jilal: Jokkuɗo Yola Koɗol

Joodii e ɓaleeɗe Benue mawɗo, Yola ko koɗol laamu Leɗɗe Adamawa Nigeria, kono ko ɓernde laamu, neɗɗo, e laamu Laamu Fombina, ko ina anndaa ko Emiraat Adamawa. Fuɗnaange duuɓi leyɗe pahal, koɗol ndee ko fuɗɗorde ko Modibbo Adama mawɗo, fuɗɗorde fedde fuɗnaange Emiraat Sokoto. Yola ko jokkuɗo koɗol laamu Lamido Adamawa. Jooni, Yola ko ɓernde urban mawɗo, ko jokkuɗo asli mum mawɗo e ɓeydinde jooni, ko seɗa neɗɗo mawɗo e jokkuɗo leñol keewɗe, ekonoma, e neɗɗo intellectual.

Fuɗɗorde mum ko fannu koɗol kisal e moƴƴere, Yola ko ɓeydii koɗol ɓernde neɗɗo Islamic, njulaagu, e laamu, ko jokkuɗo leyɗe koloniyal e Nigeria jooni. Jokkuɗo mum ko jokkuɗo crossroads natural, ko woodi leñol keewɗe e jokkuɗo socio-neɗɗo mawɗo.

Fuɗɗorde mum, Modibbo Adama ko fuɗɗii suudu mum, jamirooru maa, e kuugal hooreeɓe mum e geldo mum. Koɗol mum ko ɓeydii koɗol multicultural mawɗo, ko woodi Fulani settlers, leñol indigenous, njulaaguɓe mawɗe, e geldo Islamic, ko waylii Yola koɗol ɓernde neɗɗo Islamic e diina.
