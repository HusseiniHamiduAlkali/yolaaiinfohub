Mmụta Mbụ: Mmalite Alakụmụ Fombina

Alakụmụ Fombina, nke a maara taa dịka Amanomi Adamawa, bụ ihe àmà nke mmegide 19th n’ala Fulani Jihad n’Ụrọ̀ Afrịka. Ọ na-ejikọta mpaghara sara mbara gafee steeti Adamawa na Taraba nke Nigeria, na-aga n’etiti ugwu Cameroon, Chad, na ọbụna Central African Republic.

Aha ya, “Adamawa,” sitere na onye guzobere ya, Modibbo Adama, onye bụ onye ukwu na onye isi ọha n’ụlọ nka Fulani. Ọ kpọrọ mpaghara a "Fombina", pụtara "ụsọ" n’Igbo.
