About GTC Yola

Government Technical College Yola is a public technical institution dedicated to providing quality technical and vocational education. The college focuses on developing skilled technicians and craftsmen who can contribute to the industrial and technological development of Nigeria.

Technical Programs

  • Mechanical Engineering Trades:
    • Automobile Mechanics
    • Welding and Fabrication
    • Machine Shop Practice
    • Refrigeration and Air Conditioning
  • Electrical/Electronic Trades:
    • Electrical Installation
    • Electronics
    • Computer Hardware
    • Solar Installation
  • Construction Trades:
    • Building Construction
    • Carpentry and Joinery
    • Plumbing and Pipefitting

Facilities

  • Technical Workshops:
    • Mechanical Workshop
    • Electrical Workshop
    • Construction Workshop
    • Computer Laboratory
  • Learning Resources:
    • Technical Library
    • Drawing Studio
    • Demonstration Rooms
  • Support Facilities:
    • Student Hostel
    • Sports Ground
    • Cafeteria

Admission Requirements

  • Basic Requirements:
    • Junior Secondary School Certificate
    • Credits in Mathematics and English
    • Entrance Examination
    • Technical Aptitude Test
  • Additional Requirements:
    • Medical Fitness Certificate
    • Age Requirement (14-20 years)
    • Character Reference

Training Approach

  • Practical Training: 70% of curriculum
  • Theoretical Classes: 30% of curriculum
  • Industrial Attachment: Mandatory
  • Skills Certification: NABTEB examination

Career Opportunities

  • Industrial Employment
  • Self-Employment
  • Further Technical Education
  • Apprenticeship Training
