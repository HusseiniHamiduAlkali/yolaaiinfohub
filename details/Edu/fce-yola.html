About FCE Yola

The Federal College of Education, Yola is a premier teacher training institution committed to producing qualified and competent teachers. The college plays a vital role in developing the educational workforce of Adamawa State and Nigeria as a whole.

Academic Programs

FCE Yola offers various NCE programs in:

  • Arts and Social Sciences: English, History, Social Studies
  • Sciences: Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Biology
  • Vocational and Technical Education: Business Education, Agricultural Science
  • Education: Primary Education, Early Childhood Care
  • Languages: Arabic, Hausa, French

Facilities

  • Library: Well-stocked with educational resources
  • ICT Center: Modern computer laboratories
  • Micro-Teaching Laboratory: For practical teaching experience
  • Sports Facilities: For physical education and recreation
  • Student Accommodation: Safe and comfortable hostels

Admission Requirements

  • Five O'Level credits including English and Mathematics
  • JAMB score (for NCE programs)
  • Direct Entry qualification for degree programs
  • School screening test performance

Student Life

  • Student Union: Active student representation
  • Clubs and Societies: Academic and social organizations
  • Sports: Inter-collegiate competitions
  • Cultural Activities: Annual cultural festivals
