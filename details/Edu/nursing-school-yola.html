About the School

The School of Nursing Yola is a premier healthcare training institution dedicated to producing qualified and competent nursing professionals. The school maintains high standards in nursing education and practical training, preparing students for the challenges of modern healthcare delivery.

Programs Offered

  • Basic Nursing Program:
    • 3-year General Nursing program
    • Leads to RN qualification
    • Approved by Nursing and Midwifery Council
  • Post-Basic Programs:
    • Midwifery
    • Public Health Nursing
    • Pediatric Nursing

Facilities

  • Clinical Facilities:
    • Demonstration Laboratories
    • Simulation Rooms
    • Skills Laboratory
    • Hospital Attachments
  • Academic Facilities:
    • Modern Classrooms
    • Library
    • Computer Laboratory
    • Study Areas

Admission Requirements

  • Basic Requirements:
    • Five O'Level Credits including English, Mathematics, Biology, Chemistry, and Physics
    • Pass in School Entrance Examination
    • Successful Interview Performance
  • Additional Requirements:
    • Medical Fitness Certificate
    • Age Requirements (17-35 years)
    • Character References

Training Approach

  • Theoretical Training:
    • Classroom Lectures
    • Group Discussions
    • Case Studies
  • Practical Training:
    • Laboratory Practice
    • Hospital Rotations
    • Community Health Experience

Student Life

  • Accommodation: Available on campus
  • Healthcare: Access to medical services
  • Student Associations: Nursing Students Association
  • Mentorship: Professional guidance program
