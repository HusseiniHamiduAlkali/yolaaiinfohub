Nipa AMC

Aliyu Musdafa College, ti o wa ni Yola, jẹ́ ile-ẹ̀kọ́ kejì ti o ga pẹ̀lú itan niyonu ti ẹ̀kọ́ dara. Jango yi jẹ́ ipilẹ̀ ti ẹ̀kọ́ ni Adamawa, ti o ti lo omọ wa ti o jẹ́ pataki ni ara ẹ̀kọ́.

Àwọn Ẹ̀kọ́ Àgbàṣe

  • Junior Secondary School (JSS 1-3)
  • Senior Secondary School (SSS 1-3)
  • Science Classes
  • Arts Classes
  • Commercial Classes

Àwọn Ohun Elo

  • Àwọn Daftare Tuntun: Àwọn Inu-ile-ekọ́ ti o pẹ́kẹ́
  • Àwọn Ile-Ẹ̀kọ́ Ẹkọ Sayensi: Physics, Chemistry, ati Biology labs
  • Ile-Ẹ̀kọ́ Kọ̀ńpíútà: ICT training facility
  • Ile-Ẹ̀kọ́: Comprehensive collection of academic resources
  • Àwọn Ohun Elo Eré: Football field, basketball court, etc.
  • Àwọn Ohun Elo Boarding: Comfortable dormitories

Àwọn Ibeere Wọ Sinu

  • Successful completion of Primary School
  • Passing score in entrance examination
  • Interview performance
  • First School Leaving Certificate
  • Birth Certificate

Ndiyam Jango

  • Academic Activities: Regular classes, tutorials, and prep
  • Extra-Curricular: Sports, clubs, and societies
  • Boarding Life: Structured daily routine
  • Cultural Activities: Annual cultural day, inter-house sports

Àwọn Iṣẹ́ Pataki

  • Consistent excellent performance in WAEC/NECO examinations
  • Multiple awards in state and national competitions
  • Recognition for academic excellence
  • Strong alumni network: Aliyu Musdafa Old Students' Association (AMOSA)
