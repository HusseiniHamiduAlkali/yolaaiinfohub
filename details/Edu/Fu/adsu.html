Ko ADSU

Adamawa State University (ADSU), ɗiɗo e Mubi, ko jango ndiyam ɗe haɗi e leydi noon ngun wara ina yiɗi huutoraade jango mawɗo e waɗataa am hoore leydi Adamawa e Nijeriya kala. Universiti o fotta haɓugo tan ngol, o waɗi jango ɗiɗiima e baŋŋe jango leñol e jango gunki ɗiɗi.

Daadaa Jango

ADSU huutora daadaa jango mabɓe e kala fakultiti:

  • Fakultiti Scienze: Physique, Chimie, Biologie, Mathematiques, Informatique
  • Fakultiti Arts: English, Histoire, Etudes Religieuses, Langues
  • Fakultiti Scienze Sociales: Economics, Science Politique, Sociologie
  • Fakultiti Education: Gestion Education, Etudes Curriculaires
  • Fakultiti Agriculture: Science Agricole, Science Animale, Science des Cultures

Cuɓɓi e Leeftere

  • Leeburari Jawdi: Ko fiɗɗa hassaa e ngurndanɗe akademiike e e-leeburari
  • Centre ICT: Laboretoorɗi kumbuutar cokkirde e intarneet
  • Centres de Recherche: Cuɓɓi maaɓɓe e nder haɓugo rikoore yumma
  • Complex Sport: Cuɓɓi sporti jawdi e nder njangorde
  • Maɗo Ɓeyngu: Hokkere kidde e leydi fii moƴƴi

Laawol Anndaade

Anndaade e ADSU ko hakkunde ɗiɗi:

  • Scores JAMB UTME
  • Jawdi O'Level (WAEC/NECO)
  • Détinere Post-UTME
  • Ñaawedum fakultiti
