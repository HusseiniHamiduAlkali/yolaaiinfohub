State Polytechnic Yola (SPY)

Hesɗe Technical ngam Njuɓɓe

E SPY

State Polytechnic Yola jango naange state-owned njiɗi hokka education technical e treninge vocational majjuɗi. Jango ngal ɗaɓɓitii ley ko njuɓɓe skilled workforce fii industrial e technological advancement Adamawa State e Nijeriya.

Programmi Anndude

SPY wiyaa National Diploma (ND) e Higher National Diploma (HND) programmi ko:

  • Engineering Technology: Civil, Electrical, Mechanical
  • Business Studies: Accountancy, Banking e Finance
  • Science e Technology: Computer Science, Statistics
  • Environmental Studies: Architecture, Building Technology
  • Agriculture: Agricultural Technology

Cuɓɓi

  • Workshops Technical: Wiyaade don ngam treninge practical
  • Laboratories Kumbuutar: Cuɓɓi ICT modern
  • Leeburari: Resources technical e academic hoottitooɗo
  • Centru Entrepreneurship: Fii development skills business
  • Housing Students: Accommodation e campus

Laawol Yamiroo

Yamirooji daɗi ko programmi ko njoɓɓe e:

  • Creditji njande O'Level njande ɗi humondiri English e Mathematics
  • Score JAMB (fii programmiji ND)
  • ND e haɓɓorgo Lower Credit fii programmiji HND
  • Certificate pre-ND ngam programmiji ɓe

Treninge Industrial

  • Programmi SIWES ko foti
  • Partnerships industry ngam placement
  • Development skill practical
  • Supervision industry-based

Ndiyam Students

  • Union Students: Government students don
  • Clubs Technical: Groups development professional
  • Sporte: Activitie sporte daɗi
  • Events Cultural: Activitie cultural kala lewru
