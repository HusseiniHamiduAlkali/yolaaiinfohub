American University of Nigeria (AUN)

Jango Bikkore ɗee Afrik

Ko AUN

American University of Nigeria (AUN) ko jango jawdi fuƴƴiɗo e Yola, Adamawa State. Tawi ɓuri ɗooɗɗo 2003, o ko jango bikkore on Afrik. AUN huutora jahangal daange America noon fota e laawol Afrik, o neɗɗo ɓe woni lannge e maɓɓe e ɗee kawe ɗe ɓe huutora ko waɗa ko ngam bikkore Afrik.

Daadaa Jango

AUN huutora daadaa jango ballal e ballal lannge makko nde ɗe:

  • Saaro Business & Entrepreneurship: Accounting, Business Administration, Finance
  • Saaro IT & Computing: Computer Science, Software Engineering, Information Systems
  • Saaro Arts & Sciences: Natural & Environmental Sciences, Petroleum Chemistry
  • Saaro Law: Program LLB
  • Daadaa Jango Ballal: MBA, MSc, e degreji ballal ɗe ɓuri

Cuɓɓi Ɓikkele Donya

  • Leeburari Digital: Ko leeburari ɗi jawdi laatnude nder West Africa
  • Ajuutal Smart: Suudu jango ɓen e technology
  • Centres de Recherche: To Centre for Development Studies
  • Complex Sport: Cuɓɓi indoor e outdoor jawdi
  • Maɗo Ɓeyngu: Suudu moƴƴi waɗi security 24/7

Ñaawedum Pinal

  • Jango daange America
  • Hokkude ndekti e business e bikkore ndiyam
  • Programmi ɓikkore fiyaama
  • Jangoɓe janngo e eɓe kala
  • Klasi ndiyam baalii

Laawol Anndaade

Laawol anndaade AUN ɗi:

  • Anndaade ɗe online
  • Scores SAT/ACT (ko jangoɓe international)
  • Scores JAMB (ko jangoɓe Nijeriyan)
  • Laawol Fiyaama
  • Test proficient English (ko neɗɗo on ngoodi)
