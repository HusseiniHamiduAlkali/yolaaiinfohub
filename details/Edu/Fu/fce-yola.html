Hesɗe e Anndude Teacher

E FCE Yola

Federal College of Education, Yola jango ngol moƴƴi e treninge teachers ngam ummoraangi teachers kamɓe moƴƴi e waɗi. Jango ngal ɗaɓɓitii no Mbodi e udditorde educational workforce Adamawa State eɓe welo e Nijeriya waɗɗo mabbiti.

Programmi Anndude

FCE Yola wiyaa programmiji NCE ɓe laawol:

  • Arts e Social Sciences: English, History, Social Studies
  • Scienze: Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Biology
  • Vocational e Technical Education: Business Education, Agricultural Science
  • Education: Primary Education, Early Childhood Care
  • Liggoggooji: Arabic, Hausa, French

Cuɓɓi

  • Leeburari: Wonndude e resources educational
  • Centru ICT: Laboretoorje kumbuutar modern
  • Laboretoor Micro-Teaching: Fii treninge teaching practical
  • Cuɓɓi Sporte: Fii physical education e recreation
  • Accommodation Students: Hostels safe e woodi leydi

Laawol Yamiroo

  • Creditji O'Level njande ɗi ɓe humondiri English e Mathematics
  • Score JAMB (fii programmiji NCE)
  • Qualification Direct Entry fii programmiji degree
  • Performance test screening jango

Ndiyam Students

  • Clubs e Societies: Organizations academic e social
  • Sporte: Competitions inter-collegiate
  • Activities Cultural: Festivals cultural kala lewru
