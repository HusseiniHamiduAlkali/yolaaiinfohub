About ADSU

Adamawa State University (ADSU), located in Mubi, is a state-owned tertiary institution established to provide quality education and contribute to the development of Adamawa State and Nigeria as a whole. The university has grown significantly since its establishment, offering a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs across various disciplines.

Academic Programs

ADSU offers comprehensive programs through its various faculties:

  • Faculty of Science: Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics, Computer Science
  • Faculty of Arts: English, History, Religious Studies, Languages
  • Faculty of Social Sciences: Economics, Political Science, Sociology
  • Faculty of Education: Educational Management, Curriculum Studies
  • Faculty of Agriculture: Agricultural Science, Animal Science, Crop Science

Facilities and Resources

  • Modern Library: Well-stocked with current academic resources and e-library facilities
  • ICT Center: State-of-the-art computer laboratories with internet access
  • Research Centers: Specialized facilities for advanced research
  • Sports Complex: Modern sporting facilities for student recreation
  • Student Housing: Safe and comfortable accommodation options

Admission Information

Admission to ADSU is competitive and based on:

  • JAMB UTME scores
  • O'Level results (WAEC/NECO)
  • Post-UTME screening
  • Faculty-specific requirements
