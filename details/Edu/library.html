About the Library

The National Library of Nigeria, Yola Branch, serves as a vital educational and cultural resource center for Adamawa State. It provides access to a vast collection of books, journals, and digital resources, supporting academic research, lifelong learning, and community development.

Library Resources

    • Reference materials

  • Digital Resources:
