Cikawa a Ilimin Shari'a

Game da Kwalejin

College For Legal Studies a Yola wata cibiya ce ta farko da aka sadaukar da ilimin shari'a da horarwa. An kafa ta don biyan buƙatar masu ƙarfi a fannin shari'a a jihar Adamawa da waje, kwalejin tana ba da shirye-shirye mai cikakken a cikin karatun shari'a da ilimin paralegal.

Shirye-shiryen Ilimi

  • Shirye-shiryen Diploma:
    • Diploma a Karatun Shari'a
    • Diploma na Ci Gaba a Karatun Shari'a
    • Diploma a Karatun Paralegal
  • Kwas na Takardar:
    • Takardar a Aikin Shari'a
    • Takardar a Gudanar da Ofishin Shari'a
    • Takardar a Binciken Shari'a

Kayan Aiki

  • Kayan Aikin Ilimi:
    • Laburare na Shari'a
    • Kotun Moot
    • Dakunan Kwamfuta
    • Cibiyar Bincike
  • Albarkatun Koyo:
    • Bayanai na Dijital na Shari'a
    • Taskokin Nazarin Shari'a
    • Mujallu da Wallafe-wallafen Shari'a

Abubuwan Mahimman

  • Kwararrun Masana Shari'a a matsayin Malamai
  • Shirye-shiryen Horarwa na Aiki
  • Damar Internship
  • Cibiyar Taimakon Shari'a
  • Taron Bita na Bunkasa Kwararru

Abubuwan da ake Bukata don Shiga
