Polytechnic na Jihar Yola (SPY)

Ƙwarewar Fasaha don Ci gaba

Game da SPY

Polytechnic na Jihar Yola kamfani ne na gwamnati wanda ya himmatu wajen samar da ingantaccen ilimin fasaha da horon sana’o’i. Wannan cibiyar na ɗaukar nauyi wajen horar da kwararru masu fasaha domin ci gaban masana’antu da fasaha na Jihar Adamawa da Najeriya baki ɗaya.

Shirye‑Shiryen Karatu

SPY na bayar da shirye‑shirye na Diploma na Ƙasa (ND) da Higher National Diploma (HND) a fannoni:

  • Fasahar Injiniya: Muhalli, Wutar Lantarki, Injin
  • Darussan Kasuwanci: Lissafi, Banki da Kuɗi
  • Kimiyya da Fasaha: Kimiyyar Kwamfuta, Kididdiga
  • Darussan Muhalli: Gine‑gine, Fasahar Ginawa
  • Agriculture: Fasahar Noma

Ababen more rayuwa

  • Dakin Ayyukan Fasaha: Cikakke don horo na aikace‑aikace
  • Dakin Kwamfuta: Cibiyar ICT ta zamani
  • Laburare: Sabbin kayan karatu na fasaha da ilimi
  • Cibiyar Ƙwarewar Kasuwanci: Don haɓaka ƙwarewar kasuwanci
  • Matsugunin Dalibai: Masauki a cikin harabar makaranta

Sharuɗɗan Karɓa

Sharuɗɗan suna bambanta da shirin amma gaba ɗaya sun haɗa da:

  • Credit biyar na O'Level ciki har da Turanci da Lissafi
  • Makaran JAMB (don shirye‑shiryen ND)
  • ND da ƙasa da ƙananan ƙima don shirin HND
  • Sahihiyar takardar Pre‑ND don wasu shirye‑shirye

Horar da Masana'antu

  • Shirin SIWES tilas ne
  • Haɗin gwiwa da masana'antu don samun guraben aiki
  • Haɓakar ƙwarewar aikace‑aikace
  • Sarrafa haɗin kai ta hanyar masana'antu

Rayuwar Dalibai

  • Ƙungiyar Dalibai: Hukumar dalibai mai ƙarfi
  • Ƙungiyoyin Fasaha: Ƙungiyoyin haɓaka ƙwarewar hannu
  • Wasanni: Ayyukan wasanni daban‑daban
  • Al'adun Gargajiya: Ayyukan al'adu na shekara‑shekara
