Cikawa a Ilimin Kiwon Lafiya

Game da Kwalejin

Central College of Health Sciences and Technology, Yola wata cibiya ce ta gaba da aka sadaukar da samar da ilimi mai cikakken a cikin kimiyyar kiwon lafiya da fasahar likita. Kwalejin tana mai da nufin samar da kwararrun kwararrun kiwon lafiya waɗanda suka shirya da ilimin ka'ida da kwarewar aiki.

Shirye-shiryen Ilimi

  • Shirye-shiryen Diploma:
    • Fasahar Laburare na Likita
    • Fasahar Pharmacy
    • Fasahar Dental
    • Gudanar da Bayanin Lafiya
    • Fasahar Kiwon Lafiyar Al'umma
  • Kwas na Takardar:
    • Gudanar da Kiwon Lafiya
    • Gudanar da Rikodin Likita
    • Aikin Kiwon Lafiyar Jama'a
    • Taimakon Farko da Kiwon Lafiya na Gaggawa

Kayan Aiki

  • Kayan Aikin Ilimi:
    • Dakunan Gwaji na Zamani
    • Laburare na Likita
    • Cibiyar Kwamfuta

    • Cibiyar Bincike
  • Kayan Aikin Clinical:

    • Cibiyar Bincike
    • Cibiyar Taimakon Farko
