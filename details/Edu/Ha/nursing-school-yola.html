Makarantar Jinya ta Yola

Kulawa da Ilimi da Tausayi

Game da Makarantar

Makarantar Jinya ta Yola ita ce babbar cibiyar horon lafiya da ke ƙoƙarin samar da ƙwararrun ma’aikatan jinya masu ƙwarewa. Makarantar tana kiyaye matakaita na ilimi da horo na aikace‑aikace, tana shirya ɗalibai don ƙalubalen harkar kiwon lafiya na zamani.

Shirye‑Shiryen da ake Bayarwa

  • Shirin Jinya na Asali:
    • Shirin Jinya na shekaru 3
    • Yana ba da cancantar RN
    • An amince da shi daga Hukumar Jinya da Majalisar Kiwon Lafiya
  • Shirye‑shirye na Bayan‑Asali:
    • Jinyar Haihuwa
    • Jinyar Lafiya ta Jama’a
    • Jinyar Yara

Ababen more rayuwa

  • Ababen Kiwon Lafiya:
    • Laburaren Nuna
    • Dakunan Kwaikwayo
    • Dakin Ƙwarewa
    • Ayyukan Haɗin Asibiti
  • Ababen Ilimi:
    • Ajin zamani
    • Laburare
    • Dakin Kwamfuta
    • Wuraren Karatu

Sharuddan Karɓa

  • Sharuɗɗan Asali:
    • Credit biyar na O'Level ciki har da Turanci, Lissafi, Bioloji, Sinadari, da Fisika
    • Samun nasara a Jarabawar Shiga Makaranta
    • Ƙwarewa a Tattaunawa
  • Ƙarin Sharuɗɗa:
    • Shaidar Lafiya
    • Sharuɗɗan Shekaru (17–35)
    • Shaidar Halaye

Hanyar Horarwa

  • Horarwar Nazari:
    • Lekuna a Ajin
    • Taron Ƙungiyoyi
    • Nazarin Lamurra
  • Horarwar Aikace‑aikace:
    • Atisayen Dakin Gwaji
    • Daidai a Asibiti
    • Kwarewa a Lafiya ta Jama’a

Rayuwar Dalibai

  • Matsuguni: Akwai a cikin harabar makaranta
  • Kiwon Lafiya: Samun ayyukan likita
  • Ƙungiyoyin Dalibai: Ƙungiyar Daliban Jinya
  • Jagoranci: Shirin ba da shawara ta ƙwararru
