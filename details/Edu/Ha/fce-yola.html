Cikawa a Ilimin Malamai

Game da FCE Yola

Federal College of Education, Yola wata cibiya ce ta farko ta horar da malamai da aka sadaukar da samar da malamai masu cancantar da kwarewa. Kwalejin tana taka muhimmiyar rawa a bunkasa ma'aikatan ilimi na jihar Adamawa da Najeriya gaba daya.

Shirye-shiryen Ilimi

FCE Yola tana ba da shirye-shiryen daban-daban na NCE a:

  • Fasaha da Kimiyyar Zamantakewa: English, History, Social Studies
  • Kimiyyoyi: Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Biology
  • Ilimi na Sana'a da Fasaha: Business Education, Agricultural Science
  • Ilimi: Primary Education, Early Childhood Care
  • Harsuna: Arabic, Hausa, French

Kayan Aiki

  • Laburare: An cika shi da albarkatun ilimi
  • Cibiyar ICT: Dakunan kwamfuta na zamani
  • Dakunan Gwaji na Micro-Teaching: Don gwaninta koyarwa ta aiki
  • Kayan Aikin Wasanni: Don ilimin jiki da nishaɗi
  • Gidajen Dalibai: Gidajen zama masu lafiya da kwanciyar hankali

Abubuwan da ake Bukata don Shiga

  • Credits biyar na O'Level ciki har da Turanci da Lissafi
  • Makin JAMB (don shirye-shiryen NCE)
  • Cancantar Shiga Kai Tsaye don shirye-shiryen digiri
  • Nasa a jarrabawar bincike na makaranta

Rayuwar Dalibai

  • Ƙungiyar Dalibai: Wakilcin dalibai mai aiki
  • Ƙungiyoyi da Ƙungiyoyi: Ƙungiyoyin ilimi da zamantakewa
  • Wasanni: Gasar tsakanin kwalejoji
  • Ayyukan Al'adu: Bikin al'adu na shekara-shekara
