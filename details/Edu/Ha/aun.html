American University of Nigeria (AUN)

Jami'ar Ci Gaba don Afirka

Game da AUN

American University of Nigeria (AUN) wata jami'a ce ta sirri da ke Yola, jihar Adamawa. An kafa ta a 2003, ita ce jami'ar ci gaba ta farko a Afirka. AUN tana ba da ilimi irin na Amurka wanda aka daidaita don bukatun Afirka, tana shirya dalibai don zama shugabanni a al'ummarsu da kuma nemo mafita ga ƙalubalen ci gaban Afirka.

Shirye-shiryen Ilimi

AUN tana ba da shirye-shiryen undergraduate da graduate ta hanyar makarantunta:

  • Makaranta ta Kasuwanci & Kasuwanci: Accounting, Business Administration, Finance
  • Makaranta ta IT & Kwamfuta: Computer Science, Software Engineering, Information Systems
  • Makaranta ta Fasaha & Kimiyyoyi: Natural & Environmental Sciences, Petroleum Chemistry
  • Makaranta ta Shari'a: Shirin LLB
  • Shirye-shiryen Graduate: MBA, MSc, da sauran digiri na ci gaba

Kayan Aiki na Duniya

  • Laburare na Dijital: Daya daga cikin mafi kyawun laburare a Afirka ta Yamma
  • Ajuju na Smart: Wuraren koyo masu fasahar
  • Cibiyoyin Bincike: Ciki har da Cibiyar Nazarin Ci Gaba
  • Cibiyar Wasanni: Kayan aiki na zamani na ciki da waje
  • Gidajen Dalibai: Gidajen zama na zamani tare da tsaro 24/7

Abubuwan Musamman

  • Tsarin ilimi irin na Amurka
  • Mai da hankali kan kasuwanci da ci gaba mai dorewa
  • Shirye-shiryen haɗin gwiwar al'umma mai ƙarfi
  • Malamai na duniya daga bangarori daban-daban
  • Ƙananan girman azuzuwa don hankali na mutum

Tsarin Shiga

Tsarin shiga na AUN ya haɗa da:

  • Gabatar da aikace-aikace na kan layi
  • Makin SAT/ACT (don dalibai na duniya)
  • Makin JAMB (don dalibai na Najeriya)
  • Tsarin hira
  • Jarrabawar kwarewa a Turanci (don wadanda ba 'yan asali ba)
