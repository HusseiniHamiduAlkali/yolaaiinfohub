About the College

The College For Legal Studies in Yola is a premier institution dedicated to legal education and training. Established to meet the growing demand for legal professionals in Adamawa State and beyond, the college offers comprehensive programs in legal studies and paralegal education.

  • Diploma Programs:
    • Diploma in Legal Studies
    • Advanced Diploma in Legal Studies
    • Diploma in Paralegal Studies
  • Certificate Courses:
    • Certificate in Legal Practice
    • Certificate in Law Office Management
    • Certificate in Legal Research
