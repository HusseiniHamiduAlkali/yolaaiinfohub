About MAU

Modibbo Adama University (MAU), located in Yola, is a federal university renowned for its excellence in science, technology, and research. Established as a Federal University of Technology, it has evolved into a comprehensive university offering a wide range of programs while maintaining its strength in technological education.

Academic Programs

MAU offers diverse undergraduate and postgraduate programs through its faculties:

  • Faculty of Engineering: Civil, Electrical, Mechanical, Chemical Engineering
  • Faculty of Science: Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Biology
  • Faculty of Technology: Computer Science, Information Technology
  • Faculty of Agriculture: Agricultural Engineering, Food Science
  • Faculty of Environmental Sciences: Architecture, Urban Planning
  • School of Management and Information Technology

Research Centers and Facilities

  • Central Research Laboratory: Advanced research equipment and facilities
  • ICT Center: Modern computing facilities and internet services
  • Engineering Workshops: Practical training facilities
  • University Library: Extensive collection of academic resources
  • Agricultural Research Farm: Practical agricultural training

Student Life

  • Accommodation: Modern hostels with basic amenities
  • Sports Facilities: Football field, basketball court, indoor sports
  • Student Unions: Active student organizations and societies
  • Health Services: University clinic for medical care
  • Transportation: Campus shuttle services

Admission Requirements

Requirements for admission include:

  • JAMB UTME with competitive score
  • Five O'Level credits including English and Mathematics
  • Post-UTME screening
  • Faculty-specific requirements
