Banyere Al-Na'ab Academy

Al-Na'ab Academy bụ ụlọ akwụkwọ Islam kacha mara amara nke jikọtara agụmakwụkwọ okpukpe na mmemme agụmakwụkwọ oge a. Akadimị a na-elekwasị anya n’ịzụ ụmụ akwụkwọ zuru oke ndị na-eme nke ọma na nkuzi Islam na agụmakwụkwọ nke oge a.
