Banyere UBEC Smart School

UBEC Smart School Yola bụ ụlọ ọrụ mmụta oge a nke na-etinye teknụzụ na ngwá ọrụ mmụta dijitalụ iji nye agụmakwụkwọ ọhụụ. Ụlọ akwụkwọ ahụ bụ akụkụ nke mmemme Universal Basic Education Commission iji mee ka agụmakwụkwọ baa ọgaranya ma kwadebe ụmụ akwụkwọ maka ọdịnihu dijitalụ.
