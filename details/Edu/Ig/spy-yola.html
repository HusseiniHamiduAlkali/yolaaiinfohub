Igbo: State Polytechnic Yola (SPY)

Ịdị elu teknụzụ maka mmepe

Banyere SPY

State Polytechnic Yola bụ ụlọ ọrụ steeti nke kwere nkwa inye agụmakwụkwọ teknụzụ dị mma na ọzụzụ ọrụ. Ụlọ ọrụ ahụ nwere ọrụ dị mkpa na ịzụlite ndị ọrụ nka maka mmepe ụlọ ọrụ na teknụzụ nke steeti Adamawa na Naịjirịa.

Mmụta Akwụkwọ

SPY na-enye mmemme National Diploma (ND) na Higher National Diploma (HND) na:

  • Engineering Technology: Civil, Electrical, Mechanical
  • Business Studies: Accountancy, Banking and Finance
  • Science and Technology: Computer Science, Statistics
  • Environmental Studies: Architecture, Building Technology
  • Agriculture: Agricultural Technology

Ngwaanyị

  • Technical Workshops: E kwere nke ọma maka ọzụzụ aka
  • Computer Laboratories: Akụrụngwa ICT oge a
  • Library: Ihe ọmụma teknụzụ na agụmakwụkwọ emelitere
  • Entrepreneurship Center: Maka mmepe nka azụmahịa
  • Student Housing: Ụlọ obibi dị na campus

Arịrịọ nbanye

Arịrịọ na-adabere na mmemme ma ọ na-adịkarị:

  • Ogo O'Level ise gụnyere Bekee na Mathematics
  • Nkọwa JAMB (maka mmemme ND)
  • ND nwere ọkwa Lower Credit maka mmemme HND
  • Asambodo Pre-ND maka mmemme ụfọdụ

Ọzụzụ Ụlọ Ọrụ

  • Mmemme SIWES nke a na-achọkarị
  • Mmekọrịta ụlọ ọrụ maka ntinye ọrụ
  • Mmụta nka aka
  • Nlekọta ụlọ ọrụ

Ndụ Student

  • Student Union: Gọvanmenti ụmụ akwụkwọ na-arụsi ọrụ
  • Technical Clubs: Ụlọ ọrụ mmepe ọkachamara
  • Sports: Omume egwuregwu dị iche iche
  • Cultural Events: Omume omenala kwa afọ
