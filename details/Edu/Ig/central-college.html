Ịma mma na agụmakwụkwọ nlekọta ahụike

Banyere Kọleji

Central College of Health Sciences and Technology, Yola bụ ụlọ akwụkwọ kacha elu nke na-enye agụmakwụkwọ zuru ezu na sayensị ahụike na teknụzụ ọgwụ. Kọleji a na-achọ imepụta ndị ọkachamara nlekọta ahụike nwere ọgụgụ isi na nka ọrụ.
