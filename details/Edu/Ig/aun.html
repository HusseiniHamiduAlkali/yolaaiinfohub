Mahadum Mmepe maka Africa

Banyere AUN

American University of Nigeria (AUN) bụ mahadum nkeonwe dị na Yola, steeti Adamawa. E hiwere ya na 2003, ọ bụ Mahadum Mmepe nke mbụ na Africa. AUN na-enye agụmakwụkwọ ụdị Amerịka nke akwadoro maka mkpa Africa, na-akwadebe ụmụ akwụkwọ ka ha bụrụ ndị ndu na obodo ha ma chọta azịza maka ihe isi ike mmepe Africa.

Mmụta Akwụkwọ

AUN na-enye mmemme ndị na-emecha akwụkwọ mbụ na mmemme gredụeyt site na ụlọ akwụkwọ ya:

  • School of Business & Entrepreneurship: Accounting, Business Administration, Finance
  • School of IT & Computing: Computer Science, Software Engineering, Information Systems
  • School of Arts & Sciences: Natural & Environmental Sciences, Petroleum Chemistry
  • School of Law: LLB Program
  • Graduate Programs: MBA, MSc, and other advanced degrees

Ngwaọrụ Ebee E Si Nweta Ihe

  • Digital Library: Otu n'ime ụlọ akwụkwọ kacha mma nke nwere akụrụngwa na West Africa

Usoro Ndebanye Aha

Usoro ndebanye aha AUN gụnyere:

  • Nyefe ọrụ ntinye n'ịntanetị
  • Nkọwa SAT/ACT (maka ụmụ akwụkwọ mba ọzọ)
  • Nkọwa JAMB (maka ụmụ akwụkwọ Naịjirịa)
  • Usoro ajụjụ ọnụ
  • Nnwale asụsụ Bekee (maka ndị na-abụghị ndị ama asụsụ)
