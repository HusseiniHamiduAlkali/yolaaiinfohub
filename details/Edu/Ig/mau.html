Banyere MAU

Modibbo Adama University (MAU), dị na Yola, bụ mahadum etiti njikọta nke ama ama maka ọdịmma ya na sayensị, teknụzụ, na nnyocha. E hiwere ya dị ka Federal University of Technology, ma ọ gara n'ihu bụrụ mahadum zuru oke nke na-enye ọtụtụ mmemme ma na-edebe ike ya na agụmakwụkwọ teknụzụ.

Mmụta Akwụkwọ

MAU na-enye mmemme di iche iche nke akwụkwọ mbụ na akwụkwọ gredụeyt site na mahadum ya:

  • Faculty of Engineering: Civil, Electrical, Mechanical, Chemical Engineering
  • Faculty of Science: Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Biology
  • Faculty of Technology: Computer Science, Information Technology
  • Faculty of Agriculture: Agricultural Engineering, Food Science
  • Faculty of Environmental Sciences: Architecture, Urban Planning
  • School of Management and Information Technology

Research Centers and Facilities

  • Central Research Laboratory: Ngwaọrụ nnyocha dị elu na akụrụngwa
  • ICT Center: Akụrụngwa kọmputa oge a na ọrụ ịntanetị
  • Engineering Workshops: Ngwa ọmụmụ aka
  • University Library: Nnyocha zuru ezu nke ihe ọmụmụ akwụkwọ
  • Agricultural Research Farm: Ọzụzụ ọrụ ugbo

Ndụ Student

  • Accommodation: Hostels oge a nwere ngwa ọrụ dị mkpa
  • Sports Facilities: Ogige bọọlụ, ụlọ basketball, egwuregwu n'ime ụlọ
  • Student Unions: Ụlọ ọrụ ụmụ akwụkwọ na ụmụ amaala
  • Health Services: Klinik mahadum maka nlekọta ahụike
  • Transportation: Ụgbọ campus

Arịrịọ nbanye

Arịrịọ maka nbanye gụnyere:

  • JAMB UTME nwere akara asọmpi
  • Ogo O'Level ise gụnyere Bekee na Mathematics
  • Nnwale post-UTME
  • Arịrịọ pụrụ iche nke faculty
