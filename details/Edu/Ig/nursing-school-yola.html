Igbo: School of Nursing Yola

Na-elekọta site n'amamihe na obi ebere

Banyere School

School of Nursing Yola bụ ụlọ ọrụ ọzụzụ ahụike a ma ama nke na-enye aka imepụta ndị nọọsụ nwere ikike na ihe ọmụma. Ụlọ akwụkwọ ahụ na-edobe ụkpụrụ dị elu na agụmakwụkwọ nọọsụ na ọzụzụ aka, na-akwadebe ụmụ akwụkwọ maka ihe ịma aka nke inye nlekọta ahụike nke oge a.

Mmụta Mmemme

  • Basic Nursing Program:
    • Mmụta Nursing General afọ 3
    • Na-eduga na akara RN
    • Nkwado site na Nursing and Midwifery Council
  • Post-Basic Programs:
    • Midwifery
    • Public Health Nursing
    • Pediatric Nursing

Ngwaanyị

  • Clinical Facilities:
    • Laboratorị ngosi
    • Ụlọ ngosi ihe ọmụma
    • Laboratorị nka
    • Njikọ ụlọ ọgwụ
  • Academic Facilities:
    • Ụlọ klas oge a
    • Ụlọ akwụkwọ
    • Laboratorị kọmputa
    • Ụlọ ọmụmụ

Arịrịọ nbanye

  • Basic Requirements:
    • Ogo O'Level ise gụnyere Bekee, Mathematics, Biology, Chemistry, na Physics
    • Iga nke ọma na nnwale nnweta ụlọ akwụkwọ
    • Ọnụ arụmọrụ ajụjụ ọnụ
  • Additional Requirements:
    • Asambodo ahụike
    • Afọ ndị achọrọ (17-35 afọ)
    • Nkwupụta àgwà

Usoro Ọzụzụ

  • Theoretical Training:
    • Nkuzi klas
    • Nzuko otu
    • Case Studies
  • Practical Training:
    • Omume laboratorị
    • Ịgagharị n'ụlọ ọgwụ
    • Ahụmahụ ahụike obodo

Ndụ Student

  • Accommodation: Dị na campus
  • Healthcare: Enweta ọrụ ahụike
  • Student Associations: Nursing Students Association
  • Mentorship: Program nduzi ọkachamara
