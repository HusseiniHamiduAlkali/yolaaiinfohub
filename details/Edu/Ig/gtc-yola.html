Ịdị elu teknụzụ maka mmepe

Banyere GTC Yola

Government Technical College Yola bụ ụlọ ọrụ teknụzụ ọha nke meghere iche na inye agụmakwụkwọ teknụzụ na ọmụmụ ọrụ. Ụlọ akwụkwọ ahụ na-elekwasị anya na ịzụlite ndị ọrụ nka na ndị nwere nka ndị nwere ike inyere mmepe ụlọ ọrụ na teknụzụ Naịjịria.

Mmụta Teknụzụ

  • Mechanical Engineering Trades:
    • Mechanics ụgbọala
    • Welding na Fabrication
    • Machine Shop Practice
    • Refrigeration na Air Conditioning
  • Electrical/Electronic Trades:
    • Wiring eletrik
    • Electronics
    • Ngwa kọmputa
    • Solar installation
  • Construction Trades:
    • Wuru ụlọ
    • Carpentry na Joinery
    • Plumbing na Pipefitting

Ngwaanyị

  • Technical Workshops:
    • Mechanical Workshop
    • Electrical Workshop
    • Construction Workshop
    • Laboratory kọmputa
  • Learning Resources:
    • Technical Library
    • Drawing Studio
    • Demonstration Rooms
  • Support Facilities:
    • Hostel ụmụ akwụkwọ
    • Ogige egwuregwu
    • Nri ụlọ oriri

Arịrịọ nbanye

  • Basic Requirements:
    • Asambodo Junior Secondary School
    • Credits na Mathematics na English
    • Nnwale nnweta
    • Nnwale ikike teknụzụ
  • Additional Requirements:
    • Asambodo ahụike
    • Afọ ndụ (14-20 afọ)
    • Nkwupụta àgwà

Usoro Ọzụzụ

  • Practical Training: 70% nke mmemme
  • Theoretical Classes: 30% nke mmemme
  • Industrial Attachment: Nchọghị
  • Skills Certification: NABTEB examination
