Banyere Adroit Academy

Adroit International Academy bụ ụlọ akwụkwọ nzuzo kacha elu na Yola, nke na-enye agụmakwụkwọ ọkwa ụwa niile ma na-elekwasị anya n’ịzụ ụmụ akwụkwọ zuru oke ka ha dị njikere maka ihe isi ike ụwa. Akadimị a jikọtara usoro nkuzi nke oge a na mmepe agwa iji kwadebe ụmụ akwụkwọ maka ihe ịga nke ọma n’ọdịnihu.
