Ịma mma na agụmakwụkwọ nkuzi

Banyere FCE Yola

Federal College of Education, Yola bụ ụlọ ọrụ nkuzi onye nkuzi kacha elu nke kwadebere imepụta ndị nkuzi nwere ikike na nkà. Kọleji a na-egwuri ọrụ dị mkpa n’ịmepụta ike ọrụ agụmakwụkwọ nke steeti Adamawa na Nigeria niile.

Mmụta Akwụkwọ

FCE Yola na-enye mmemme NCE dị iche iche na:

  • Arts na Social Sciences: Bekee, Akụkọ, Ụmụaka ọmụmụ mmekọrịta
  • Sayensị: Mátimatiks, Fisiks, Kemistri, Bọlọjị
  • Agụmakwụkwọ Ọgụ Ọrụ na Teknụzụ: Agụmakwụkwọ Ahịa, Sayensị Ugbo
  • Agụmakwụkwọ: Agụmakwụkwọ Primary, Nlekọta Ụmụaka Mmalite
  • Asụsụ: Arabic, Hausa, French

Ụlọ Ọrụ

  • Ụlọ Akwụkwọ: Jupụtara na akụrụngwa agụmakwụkwọ
  • ICT Center: Ụlọ ọrụ kọmpụta oge a
  • Ụlọ Nnyocha Micro-Teaching: Maka ahụmịhe nkuzi n’ọrụ
  • Ụlọ Ọrụ Egwuregwu: Maka agụmakwụkwọ ahụike na ntụrụndụ
  • Ụlọ obibi ụmụ akwụkwọ: Ụlọ obibi nchekwa na nkasi obi

Arịrịọ nbanye

  • Akwụkwọ ikike O'Level ise tinyere Bekee na Mátimatiks
  • Nsonaazụ JAMB (maka mmemme NCE)
  • Nkwenye Direct Entry maka mmemme ogo
  • Ogo ule nyocha ụlọ akwụkwọ

Ndụ Ụmụ Akwụkwọ

  • Njikọ Ụmụ Akwụkwọ: Nchịkọta ụmụ akwụkwọ na-arụ ọrụ
  • Klọb na Ụgbọ: Ụgbọ ọrụ agụmakwụkwọ na nke mmekọrịta
  • Egwuregwu: Asọmpi n'etiti kọleji
  • Mmemme Omenala: Ememme omenala kwa afọ
