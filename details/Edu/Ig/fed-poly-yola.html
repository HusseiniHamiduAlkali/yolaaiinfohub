Teknụzụ maka mmepe mba

Banyere Federal Polytechnic Yola

Federal Polytechnic Yola bụ ụlọ akwụkwọ ọkwa teknụzụ nke gọọmenti etiti na-ahụ maka inye agụmakwụkwọ teknụzụ na ọrụ. Ụlọ ọrụ a na-aghọ akụkụ dị mkpa n’ịmepụta ndị ọkachamara nwere nkà na teknụzụ iji nyere mmepe teknụzụ Nigeria aka.

Mmụta Akwụkwọ

  • Ụlọ Akwụkwọ Teknụzụ Injini:
    • Teknụzụ Civil Engineering
    • Electrical/Electronic Engineering
    • Teknụzụ Mechanical Engineering
    • Kọmputa Engineering
  • Ụlọ Akwụkwọ Mmụta Obodo:
    • Teknụzụ Ihe owuwu
    • Teknụzụ wụnye ụlọ
    • Quantity Surveying
    • Nchịkwa Ụlọ ala
  • Ụlọ Akwụkwọ Ọmụmụ Ahịa:
    • Akaụntansi
    • Njikwa Ahịa
    • Banking na Finance
    • Njikwa Teknụzụ Ụlọ Ọrụ

Ụlọ Ọrụ

  • Ụlọ Ọrụ Akwụkwọ:
    • Ụlọ ngosi akwụkwọ oge a
    • Ụlọ ọrụ injinia
    • Ụlọ ọrụ kọmpụta
    • Ụlọ ọrụ eserese teknụzụ
    • Ụlọ akwụkwọ dijitalụ
  • Ụlọ Nnyocha:
    • Ụlọ Nchọpụta Teknụzụ
    • Ụlọ Mmepe Mmepụta
    • ICT Center

Arịrịọ nbanye

  • National Diploma (ND):
    • Akwụkwọ ikike O'Level ise tinyere Bekee na Mátimatiks
    • Nsonaazụ JAMB ND
    • Ule nbanye Polytechnic
  • Higher National Diploma (HND):
    • National Diploma nwere ọkwa Lower Credit kacha nta
    • Ahụmịhe Ọzụzụ Ụlọ Ọgwụ otu afọ
    • Arịrịọ O'Level
