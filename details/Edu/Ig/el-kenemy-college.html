Banyere El-kenemy College

El-kenemy College of Islamic Theology, Yola bụ ụlọ akwụkwọ Islam kacha elu nke jikọtara agụmakwụkwọ Islam na mmemme agụmakwụkwọ oge a. Kọleji ahụ kwụsiri ike ịzụnta mụmụ, mmụọ, na mmepe agwa ụmụ akwụkwọ mgbe ọ na-akwadebe ha maka ọmụmụ dị elu na ọrụ ọdịnihu.
