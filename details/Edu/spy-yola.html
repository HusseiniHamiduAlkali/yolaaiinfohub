State Polytechnic Yola (SPY)

Technical Excellence for Development

About SPY

State Polytechnic Yola is a state-owned institution committed to providing quality technical education and vocational training. The institution plays a crucial role in developing skilled workforce for industrial and technological advancement of Adamawa State and Nigeria.

Academic Programs

SPY offers National Diploma (ND) and Higher National Diploma (HND) programs in:

  • Engineering Technology: Civil, Electrical, Mechanical
  • Business Studies: Accountancy, Banking and Finance
  • Science and Technology: Computer Science, Statistics
  • Environmental Studies: Architecture, Building Technology
  • Agriculture: Agricultural Technology

Facilities

  • Technical Workshops: Well-equipped for practical training
  • Computer Laboratories: Modern ICT facilities
  • Library: Updated technical and academic resources
  • Entrepreneurship Center: For business skills development
  • Student Housing: On-campus accommodation

Admission Requirements

Requirements vary by program but generally include:

  • Five O'Level credits including English and Mathematics
  • JAMB score (for ND programs)
  • ND with minimum of Lower Credit for HND programs
  • Pre-ND certificate for some programs

Industrial Training

  • Mandatory SIWES program
  • Industry partnerships for placement
  • Practical skill development
  • Industry-based supervision

Student Life

  • Student Union: Active student government
  • Technical Clubs: Professional development groups
  • Sports: Various sporting activities
  • Cultural Events: Annual cultural activities
