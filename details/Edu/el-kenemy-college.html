About El-kenemy College

El-kenemy College of Islamic Theology, Yola is a premier Islamic educational institution that combines Islamic education with contemporary academic curriculum. The college is committed to nurturing students' intellectual, spiritual, and moral development while preparing them for higher education and future careers.
