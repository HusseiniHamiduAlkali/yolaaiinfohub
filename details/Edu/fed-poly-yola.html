About Federal Polytechnic Yola

The Federal Polytechnic Yola is a federal government-owned tertiary institution dedicated to providing quality technical and vocational education. The institution plays a crucial role in developing skilled professionals and technologists who contribute to Nigeria's technological advancement.

Academic Programs

  • School of Engineering Technology:
    • Civil Engineering Technology
    • Electrical/Electronic Engineering
    • Mechanical Engineering Technology
    • Computer Engineering
  • School of Environmental Studies:
    • Architectural Technology
    • Building Technology
    • Quantity Surveying
    • Estate Management
  • School of Business Studies:
    • Accountancy
    • Business Administration
    • Banking and Finance
    • Office Technology Management

Admission Requirements

  • National Diploma (ND):
    • 5 O'Level Credits including English and Mathematics
    • JAMB ND Score
    • Polytechnic Entry Examination
  • Higher National Diploma (HND):
    • National Diploma with minimum of Lower Credit
    • One year Industrial Training experience
    • O'Level requirements
